Stainless steel wedge nets are filtration devices constructed using wedge-shaped wire mesh. These wires are aligned in such a way that they provide uniform slot sizes, which enables accurate and consistent particle separation. They are widely employed in industrial processes that require high-efficiency filtration, such as in <strong style="font-size:13px">water treatment plants</strong>, <strong style="font-size:13px">mineral processing</strong>, <strong style="font-size:13px">pulp and paper industries</strong>, <strong style="font-size:13px">food and beverage manufacturing</strong>, and <strong style="font-size:13px">chemical plants</strong>.</p><p>
