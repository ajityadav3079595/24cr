# Low Soda Alumina Market, Global Outlook & Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Low Soda Alumina is a high-temperature alumina product with a sodium content of less than 0.1%. The sodium oxide (Na2O) content is a critical technical indicator that directly influences the material's compressive strength and electrical insulation. Low soda alumina is particularly prized for its high melting point, excellent electrical insulation properties, high hardness, and superior wear resistance. These characteristics make it an ideal choice for use in various high-performance applications, including electronic ceramics, fine ceramics, refractory materials, automotive spark plugs, and electronic substrates.</p><p>
</p><p>The primary purpose of low soda alumina is to maintain its structural integrity and electrical performance under harsh conditions, which is why it is widely used in the electronics and materials industries.
