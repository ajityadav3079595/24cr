</p><p>4-vinylpyridine is an organic compound with the formula CH2CHC5H4N, primarily used as a monomer in polymer synthesis and specialty chemical applications. This versatile chemical serves as a building block for functional polymers, coatings, adhesives, and pharmaceutical intermediates. The compound's ability to enhance polymer properties through its reactive vinyl group and nitrogen-containing pyridine ring makes it valuable across multiple industries.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from the polymer industry, particularly for high-performance materials in automotive and electronics applications. Specialty chemical applications account for approximately 65% of total consumption, with Asia Pacific dominating production and consumption.
