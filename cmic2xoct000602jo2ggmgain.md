</p><p>The global household appliances market is witnessing a paradigm shift toward premium aesthetic designs, with brands increasingly adopting <strong>VCM metal sheets</strong> to enhance product appeal. Consumer surveys indicate that over 60% of buyers consider visual appearance as a critical factor when purchasing large appliances.
