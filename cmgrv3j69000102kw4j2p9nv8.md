# Polyethylene Imine Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Polyethylene Imine (PEI) is a polymer characterized by its amine groups and CH2CH2 spacers. It exists in various structural forms, including linear, branched, and dendrimeric types. The polymer’s strong polycationic nature makes it a valuable material across diverse industries, including papermaking, electroplating, biomedicine, coatings, and water treatment. PEI is widely used as a chelating agent, adhesion promoter, and flocculant, enhancing its demand in industrial and commercial applications.</p><p>
