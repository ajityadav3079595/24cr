</p><p>Powdered form reactive alumina is a highly porous and granular form of aluminum oxide (Al2O3) with high surface area and adsorption capacity. It is widely used as a desiccant, catalyst, and adsorbent in various industrial applications. The material is known for its ability to remove impurities, moisture, and fluoride from gases and liquids.
