</p><p>Heat transfer foils are specialized materials used for applying decorative or functional designs onto various substrates through heat and pressure. These foils consist of a carrier film coated with metallic or pigmented layers that transfer onto surfaces when activated by heat. The product range includes glossy and matte finishes, with applications spanning textiles, beauty products, household appliances, and other industrial uses.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by expanding demand in the textile industry for customized apparel decoration and increasing adoption in consumer electronics for aesthetic enhancements. While the beauty sector shows steady growth for nail art and cosmetic packaging applications, household appliances represent the fastest-growing segment due to rising demand for premium finishes. Key players like Nissha and Stahls are investing in innovative foil technologies to cater to evolving customer preferences for durability and design flexibility.</p><p>
