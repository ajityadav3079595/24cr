</p><p>Palm Mid-Fraction (PMF) is a specialized fraction obtained from palm oil, widely used in the food industry, especially in chocolate and confectionery products. It is highly valued for its ability to enhance texture and melting properties, often serving as an alternative to cocoa butter. Additionally, PMF is used in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals due to its unique fatty acid composition.</p><p>
