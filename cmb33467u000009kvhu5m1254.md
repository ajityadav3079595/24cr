</p><p>Gallic Acid (CAS 149-91-7) is a naturally occurring organic acid classified as a trihydroxybenzoic acid. It is also referred to as 3,4,5-trihydroxybenzoic acid and is primarily found in gallnuts, sumac, witch hazel, tea leaves, oak bark, and various other plants. Gallic acid is widely used in pharmaceutical, food, cosmetics, and industrial applications due to its antioxidant, antimicrobial, and anti-inflammatory properties.</p><p>
