</p><p>Horizontal split casing pumps are centrifugal pumps designed with a horizontally split casing that allows easy access to internal components for maintenance. These robust pumps feature an impeller mounted between bearings and are widely used for high-capacity fluid transfer applications across industries. The segment includes various pump types differentiated by material (cast iron, stainless steel, and other alloys) and end-use specifications.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is driven by increasing infrastructure investments in water treatment facilities and rising demand from the oil &amp; gas sector for reliable pumping solutions. The replacement cycle of aging industrial pumps in chemical processing plants further contributes to demand. Leading manufacturers are focusing on energy-efficient designs to meet stricter environmental regulations across end-user industries.</p><p>
