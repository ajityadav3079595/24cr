# Dimethylamine Market, Global Outlook & Forecast 2025-2032

<p><strong>Dimethylamine (DMA)</strong> is an organic compound with the molecular formula (CH3)2NH. It is a secondary amine that exists as a colorless, flammable gas with a strong ammonia-like odor. Commonly used in industrial applications, DMA is primarily available in aqueous solutions with concentrations up to 40%. It is widely utilized in the production of pharmaceuticals, pesticides, surfactants, and rubber processing chemicals.</p><p>
