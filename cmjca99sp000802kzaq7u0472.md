</p><p>Hydrogenated Styrene-Isoprene Block Copolymer (SEPS) is a thermoplastic elastomer characterized by excellent elasticity, thermal stability, and resistance to UV degradation. This advanced polymer is formed through selective hydrogenation of styrene-isoprene-styrene block copolymers, resulting in improved performance properties for specialized applications. SEPS demonstrates superior flexibility and durability compared to conventional rubber materials.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from key end-use industries such as cosmetics, medical devices, and automotive components.
