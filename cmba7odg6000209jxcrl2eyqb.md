</p><p>2. Expansion of the construction industry and increasing focus on green building practices create a favorable environment for the use of degradable non-woven materials in geotextiles and construction applications.</p><p>
</p><p>3. Collaborations and partnerships between manufacturers and research institutions can lead to the development of innovative degradable non-woven materials with enhanced properties.</p><p>
Challenges:</p><p>
</p><p>1. Limited awareness among end-users about the benefits and applications of degradable non-woven materials may hinder market growth and adoption across different industries.</p><p>
