The compound exists in varying purities, with grades such as <strong>Purity ≥98% and Purity ≥99%</strong> being the most commercially significant. Its primary functions include serving as a precursor in the synthesis of active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) and as a crucial component in pesticide formulations.
