It maintains stable pH levels (6.8-8.2) in cell culture media, protein extraction buffers, and other laboratory solutions. HEPES demonstrates superior buffering capacity compared to traditional buffers like phosphate, particularly in maintaining physiological pH ranges critical for cellular processes.</p><p>
