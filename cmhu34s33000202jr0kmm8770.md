</p><p>Coated white board paper, also known as coated duplex board, is a premium-grade paper material featuring one or both sides coated with kaolin or calcium carbonate to create a smooth, glossy surface. This specialized coating enhances printability, moisture resistance, and structural rigidity, making it ideal for high-end packaging applications across industries.
