</p><p>Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP) is a copolymer of hexafluoropropylene and tetrafluoroethylene, belonging to the fluoropolymer family. It is known for its <strong>high thermal stability, chemical resistance, and excellent electrical insulation properties</strong>. FEP is widely used in applications requiring non-stick surfaces, corrosion resistance, and high-temperature performance. Key products include FEP films, coatings, and tubing, which are utilized in industries such as electronics, automotive, and chemical processing.
