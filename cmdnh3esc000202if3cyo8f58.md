</p><p>Marine coatings for leisure boats are specialized protective layers applied to boat surfaces to enhance durability, prevent corrosion, and improve aesthetics. These coatings include <strong>anti-fouling, anti-corrosion, foul-release coatings, and others</strong>, each serving a specific purpose. Anti-fouling coatings prevent marine organisms from attaching to the hull, while anti-corrosion coatings protect against rust and degradation. Foul-release coatings reduce drag, improving fuel efficiency.
