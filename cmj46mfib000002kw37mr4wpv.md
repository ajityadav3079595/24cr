The material's properties vary based on crystalline orientation, allowing tailored solutions for industries requiring durability, scratch resistance, and light transmission. Due to these attributes, synthetic sapphire is widely used in semiconductor manufacturing, LED production, consumer electronics, and optical systems.</p><p>
