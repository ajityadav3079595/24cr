Its high reactivity, primarily due to its carbon-carbon double bond, makes it a crucial raw material for a vast array of downstream products. The primary production method is steam cracking of hydrocarbon feedstocks such as naphtha, ethane, and LPG.</p><p>

<strong>Shift Towards Bio-based and Sustainable Production</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The ethylene market is experiencing a significant trend towards sustainability, with growing investments in bio-based ethylene production.
