These formulations contain accelerators such as calcium chloride or proprietary chemical blends that reduce curing time from days to hours while maintaining compressive strength. Key applications include emergency repairs, cold-weather construction, and projects requiring rapid return-to-service, such as highways, airport runways, and industrial flooring.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing infrastructure development spending, particularly in emerging economies, coupled with rising demand for time-efficient construction solutions.
