# Immersion Cooling Fluids Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Immersion cooling fluids are cooling mediums used for IT components and other electronic equipment, including servers and storage equipment. These fluids enable direct contact of the circulating liquid with hot components, removing heat from the system through a cold heat exchanger. The fluids possess excellent insulating properties for safe interaction with energized electronic components.</p><p>
