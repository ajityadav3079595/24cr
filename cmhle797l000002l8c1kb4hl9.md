End User Industry Analysis</p><p>
</p><p>The <strong>textile and apparel industry</strong> is the largest consumer of pure wool, accounting for over 60% of global demand. Wool is widely used in high-end fashion, suits, and winter wear. The <strong>home furnishing industry</strong> is another major consumer, with wool being used in carpets, upholstery, and blankets due to its durability and insulation properties. The <strong>automotive industry</strong> also utilizes wool in premium car interiors. Additionally, the <strong>healthcare sector</strong> uses wool in medical textiles for its hypoallergenic properties.</p><p>
