These highly porous carbon materials chemically bind mercury vapor from flue gases through mechanisms like physisorption and chemisorption, preventing environmental contamination. The product comes in various forms including powder activated carbon (PAC), granular activated carbon (GAC), and impregnated carbon variants optimized for mercury capture.</p><p>
