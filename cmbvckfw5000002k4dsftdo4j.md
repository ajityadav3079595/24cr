</p><p>Glove liners are thin, inner gloves designed to be worn beneath outer gloves. Their primary functions include enhancing warmth retention, absorbing sweat, and offering antibacterial and deodorizing benefits. These liners are made from various materials such as wool, polyester, cashmere, and nylons, each offering distinct advantages in terms of insulation, moisture management, and comfort. Glove liners are crucial in extreme temperature settings, work environments requiring dexterity, and daily wear in colder climates. They act as a buffer between the skin and the glove, reducing skin irritation and increasing the overall lifespan and comfort of the gloves.</p><p>
