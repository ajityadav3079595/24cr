# 6PPD Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032, by Manufacturers, Regions, Technology, Application, Product Type

<p>6PPD (N-(1,3-Dimethylbutyl)-N'-phenyl-p-phenylenediamine) is a chemical antioxidant widely used in the rubber industry. It is primarily utilized as an antidegradant in tires and rubber products to enhance their longevity by preventing oxidative degradation, ozone cracking, and wear. Due to its superior protective properties, 6PPD has become an essential component in the automotive, industrial, and polymer industries.</p><p>
