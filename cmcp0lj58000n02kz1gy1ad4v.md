The copper oxide used in these applications must meet stringent purity standards to ensure the quality and reliability of electronic components.</p><p>
</p><p>Copper oxide is known for its excellent conductivity and stability, making it ideal for various industrial applications. It is used extensively in semiconductor packaging, IC carrier boards, HDI boards, and other key products within the electronics sector. Given its high purity levels, this compound ensures superior electroplating results, contributing to the durability and performance of electronic devices.</p><p>
