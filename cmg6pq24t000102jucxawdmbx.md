<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>EPTFE Venting Membrane is a specialized porous material used in various applications for venting and air diffusion purposes. It is a modified form of PTFE, known for its exceptional chemical resistance, non-stick properties, and high-temperature resistance. The expansion process creates a porous structure with interconnected nodes and fibrils, allowing air and gases to pass through while blocking the entry of liquids and particulates.</p><p>
