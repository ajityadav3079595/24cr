</p><p>Benzophenone-6 is an organic compound primarily used as a UV filter in personal care products and cosmetics. Chemically known as 2,4-Dihydroxybenzophenone, it functions by absorbing ultraviolet radiation in the 280-320 nm range (UV-B spectrum). This ingredient helps prevent skin damage while maintaining product stability against sunlight degradation. The compound is particularly valued for its compatibility with various formulations including sunscreens, lipsticks, and hair care products.</p><p>
