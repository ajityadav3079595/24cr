</p><p>N-Nonane (CAS 111-84-2) is a linear alkane hydrocarbon with the chemical formula C9H20, belonging to the family of Acyclic Alkanes. This clear, colorless liquid with a sharp odor is naturally found in plant oils such as oregano and olive oil. While N-Nonane has 35 structural isomers, its industrial-grade variants typically include 97-99% purity and 99% purity formulations. The compound is insoluble in water and less dense than water, making it useful in various applications but requiring careful handling due to potential health hazards.</p><p>
