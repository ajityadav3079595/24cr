</p><p>Fenugreek gum is a natural hydrocolloid extracted from the seeds of Trigonella foenum-graecum (fenugreek). It acts as a stabilizer, emulsifier, and thickening agent due to its high galactomannan content, which enhances viscosity and texture in food, pharmaceutical, and industrial applications. The gum is categorized into cold water-soluble and other variants, with increasing adoption in nutraceuticals, textiles, and paper production.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by rising demand for plant-based ingredients in food processing, particularly in gluten-free and functional foods.
