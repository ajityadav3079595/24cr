The rising demand for processed and convenience foods, coupled with increasing applications in non-food industries, is driving market expansion.Distilled Glycerin Monostearate (GMS) is a high-purity, non-ionic emulsifier derived from natural vegetable oil and fat. It is commonly used in the food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries to enhance texture, stability, and shelf life. Due to its excellent emulsifying, dispersing, and foaming properties, GMS is widely applied in bakery products, dairy items, frozen foods, and personal care products.</p><p>
