# Global Inks for Planographic Printing Market Research Report 2025-2032

<p>The global ink for planographic printing market was valued at <strong>US$ 1.93 billion in 2024</strong> and is projected to reach <strong>US$ 2.87 billion by 2032</strong>, exhibiting a <strong>Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 5.1%</strong> during the forecast period (2025–2032). The market growth is driven by increasing demand for high-quality printing in packaging, publication, and labeling industries. The shift toward sustainable and eco-friendly inks, such as water-based and UV-cured inks, is also contributing to market expansion.</p><p>
</p><p>Planographic printing refers to a printing process where the image and non-image areas are on the same plane, relying on the principle of oil and water repulsion. <strong>Inks for planographic printing</strong> are specially formulated to adhere to the image areas while being repelled by the non-image areas. These inks are widely used in <strong>lithography, offset printing, and other planographic techniques</strong>.
