<p>Photocatalytic materials and coatings are advanced substances that leverage light energy to catalyze chemical reactions. They are widely applied in air and water purification, self-cleaning surfaces, and antimicrobial coatings. These materials, primarily composed of titanium dioxide (TiO2) and zinc oxide (ZnO), are integral to sustainable development efforts due to their ability to break down pollutants and harmful microorganisms under light exposure.</p><p>
