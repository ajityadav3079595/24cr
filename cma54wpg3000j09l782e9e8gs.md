</p><p>HVO, or Hydrotreated Vegetable Oil, is a renewable diesel substitute derived from treating vegetable oils or animal fats with hydrogen in the presence of catalysts. Unlike conventional biodiesel (FAME), HVO has better cold flow properties and a longer shelf life, making it highly compatible with existing diesel infrastructure and engines without the need for modification.</p><p>
