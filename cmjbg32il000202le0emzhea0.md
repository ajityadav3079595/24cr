</p><p>Pile fabric is a textile characterized by raised fibers or loops that create a soft, plush surface. These fabrics are widely used in various applications, including clothing, upholstery, and accessories. The key types of pile fabric include <strong>velvet, velveteen, corduroy, and fake fur</strong>. These fabrics are known for their durability, aesthetic appeal, and comfort, making them popular in both fashion and home décor industries.
