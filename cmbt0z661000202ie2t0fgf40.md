<p>The global degradable resin market was valued at <strong>US$ 267 million in 2024 and is projected to reach US$ 519 million by 2032, exhibiting a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.6% during the forecast period (2025–2032)</strong>. The market is expected to witness significant growth due to increasing environmental concerns and stringent regulation regarding plastic waste management. The market is segmented by type, application, and region, with each segment contributing to the overall market dynamics.</p><p>
</p><p>Degradable resin are polymer that can decompose naturally through biological processes, such as microbial action, into water, carbon dioxide, and biomass. These resin are designed to reduce environmental pollution caused by conventional plastic. The global degradable resin market includes various types of biodegradable polymer, such as polylactic acid (PLA), polybutylene succinate (PBS), polybutylene terephthalate adipate (PBTA), and others. These material are widely used in application like food packaging, medical device, and agricultural film.
