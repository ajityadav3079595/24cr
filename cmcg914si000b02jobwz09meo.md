# Osmium Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2032

<p>Osmium is a rare and dense metal belonging to the platinum group, known for its extreme hardness, high melting point, and corrosion resistance. It is primarily used in the production of specialized alloys, electrical contacts, fountain pen nibs, and medical implants. Due to its scarcity and unique properties, osmium is considered a valuable material in high-precision applications and scientific research.</p><p>
