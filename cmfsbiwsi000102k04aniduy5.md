This compound's ability to selectively accumulate in tumor cells makes it particularly valuable for precise cancer detection and treatment. Recent clinical trials have demonstrated success rates exceeding 85% for certain superficial cancers when using 5-ALA HCl-based PDT protocols.</p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Expansion of Precision Agriculture to Fuel Demand</strong></p><p>
