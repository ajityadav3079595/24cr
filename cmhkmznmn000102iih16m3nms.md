<strong>MARKET DYNAMICS</strong></p><p>
<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Stringent Environmental Regulations Accelerate Adoption of Desulfurization Technologies</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The global push for cleaner air quality standards has significantly increased regulatory pressure on industries to reduce sulfur emissions. These regulations are forcing manufacturers to invest in advanced desulfurization technologies, driving demand for high-quality desulfurization mineral turpentine products. The enforcement of standards similar to Euro VI in several regions has created a surge in adoption, as non-compliant enterprises face heavy penalties.</p><p>

</p><p>The paints and coatings sector, which accounts for over 35% of global desulfurization mineral turpentine consumption, is experiencing robust growth due to rising construction activities and automotive production. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Technological Advancements in Refining Processes Create New Demand</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Recent innovations in petroleum refining and chemical processing have improved both the efficiency and output quality of desulfurization mineral turpentine production. These technological improvements are reducing production costs while increasing yield, making desulfurization mineral turpentine more accessible for industrial applications. The uncertainty in raw material costs presents a persistent challenge to market stability and investment planning.</p><p>
