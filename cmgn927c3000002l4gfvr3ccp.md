</p><p>Calcium ascorbate is a calcium salt of ascorbic acid (vitamin C), commonly used as a dietary supplement and food additive. It is a non-acidic form of vitamin C, making it suitable for individuals with sensitive stomachs. Calcium ascorbate is widely utilized in the <strong>food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries</strong> due to its antioxidant properties, stability, and bioavailability.
