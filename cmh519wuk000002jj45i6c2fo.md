</p><p>Polyphenylsulfone (PPSU) is an advanced <strong>high-temperature thermoplastic</strong> known for its exceptional mechanical strength, hydrolytic stability, and flame-retardant properties. As a member of the sulfone polymer family, PPSU offers superior performance in harsh environments compared to conventional plastics, making it indispensable for medical devices, aerospace components, and food processing equipment. Its ability to withstand repeated steam sterilization cycles has particularly driven adoption in healthcare applications.</p><p>
</p><p>While the medical sector remains a key growth driver, expanding applications in <strong>automotive lightweighting</strong> and <strong>water treatment membranes</strong> are creating new opportunities.
