</p><p>Sulfadiazine is a sulfonamide antibiotic active pharmaceutical ingredient (API) widely used in veterinary medicine for treating bacterial infections in animals. As a bacteriostatic agent, it works by inhibiting bacterial folic acid synthesis, making it effective against various gram-positive and gram-negative organisms. The veterinary formulation finds applications in both oral (tablets) and injectable forms, with purity grades typically ranging from 98% to 99% for pharmaceutical use.</p><p>
</p><p>The market expansion is supported by multiple factors including rising pet ownership rates, intensified livestock farming practices, and increasing awareness about zoonotic diseases.
