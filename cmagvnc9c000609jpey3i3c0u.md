<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Potato Pregel Starch is a pregelatinized starch derived from potatoes. This type of starch undergoes a specific processing method where it is cooked and then dried using a drum dryer or extruder, making it instantly soluble in cold water. Due to its excellent binding, thickening, and stabilizing properties, Potato Pregel Starch is widely utilized in industries such as food &amp; beverages, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and animal feed.</p><p>
