It is characterized by excellent magnetic permeability, low core loss, and high electrical resistance, making it ideal for manufacturing cores in transformers, motors, generators, and other electromagnetic devices. The material is produced through precise alloying and annealing processes to optimize its grain structure for reduced energy losses during alternating current magnetization cycles.</p><p>
