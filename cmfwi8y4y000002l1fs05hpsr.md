Additionally, the material's inherent rigidity complicates installation in compact baghouse designs, with an estimated 15-20% of retrofit projects requiring structural modifications to accommodate fiberglass filtration systems.</p><p>

</p><p>Recent advancements in PTFE and silicone-based coatings present transformative opportunities, enhancing fiberglass bags' chemical resistance while lowering pressure drop characteristics. Market leaders are investing heavily in nanofiber composite solutions that combine fiberglass structural benefits with enhanced surface filtration properties.
