</p><p>Food Grade Disodium Phosphate (DSP) is an inorganic compound primarily used as an emulsifier, stabilizer, and pH regulator in food applications. This white, water-soluble powder plays a critical role in processed cheese production, baked goods, meat products, and beverages by improving texture and shelf life. DSP also finds application in water treatment as a corrosion inhibitor and scale prevention agent.</p><p>
