# Rough Diamonds Market, Global Outlook & Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Rough diamonds refer to naturally occurring diamonds that have not undergone any cutting, polishing, or processing. These unrefined stones are mined from diamond deposits and vary in shape, size, and clarity. Rough diamonds serve as raw materials for jewelry manufacturing and industrial applications. Their value is determined by carat weight, color, clarity, and shape.</p><p>
