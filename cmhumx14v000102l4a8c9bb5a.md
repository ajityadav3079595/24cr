</p><p>Triple wall corrugated paperboard consists of three layers of fluted material sandwiched between four linerboards, providing superior strength and durability compared to single or double wall alternatives. This heavy-duty packaging solution is increasingly preferred for industrial applications because of its excellent stacking strength, puncture resistance, and cushioning properties. The product finds extensive use in sectors requiring robust protective packaging such as automotive components, machinery parts, and fragile consumer goods.</p><p>
