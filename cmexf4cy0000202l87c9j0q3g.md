</p><p>Foaming agents derived from Quillaja, Yucca, and seaweed extracts are natural surfactants used to stabilize foam in food products and enhance digestibility in animal nutrition. Quillaja extract is sourced from the Quillaja saponaria tree, Yucca extract comes from the Yucca schidigera plant, and seaweed extract is obtained from marine algae.
