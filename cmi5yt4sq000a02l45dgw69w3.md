</p><p>Low Alloy Steel Flux Cored Wire is a specialized welding consumable containing alloying elements (typically 1-5% by weight) that enhance mechanical properties and corrosion resistance. These wires feature a hollow core filled with flux compounds that generate shielding gases during welding, making them essential for critical applications in construction, shipbuilding, and heavy machinery manufacturing. The product is available in two primary variants: gas-shielded (requiring external gas) and self-shielded (flux-generated protection).</p><p>
