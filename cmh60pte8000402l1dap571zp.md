This white crystalline powder decomposes to generate free radicals at elevated temperatures, making it essential for producing synthetic rubbers, plastics, and acrylic fibers. AIBN finds extensive applications across PVC, PVA, POP, PMMA and other polymer production processes.</p><p>

<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Growing Demand from Polymer Industry Accelerates AIBN Market Growth</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The polymer industry accounts for over 60% of global AIBN consumption, acting as a primary market driver. Azobisisobutyronitrile serves as a crucial free-radical initiator in producing various polymers, particularly PVC, PMMA, and synthetic rubbers. Recent expansions in Asia-Pacific polymer production facilities have significantly boosted AIBN demand, with China alone witnessing a 7-9% annual increase in consumption since 2020.</p><p>
