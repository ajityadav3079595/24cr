</p><p><strong><strong style="font-size:13px">Food Grade Magnesium Silicate</strong> is a purified, natural, non-toxic mineral compound primarily composed of magnesium, silicon, and oxygen. It is sourced from magnesium silicate ore or synthetically derived through controlled chemical processes. Classified as a food additive, it is widely used in the <strong style="font-size:13px">food and beverage industry</strong> for its functional properties. Common applications include its use as an <strong style="font-size:13px">anti-caking agent</strong>, <strong style="font-size:13px">anti-sedimenting agent</strong>, and <strong style="font-size:13px">filler</strong> to improve the texture, consistency, and shelf-life of various food products.</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Due to its inert and stable nature, food grade magnesium silicate does not chemically interact with food ingredients, making it suitable for use in powdered and dry food items such as salt, powdered spices, sugar, flour, and instant mixes. Its role is critical in preventing clumping, ensuring free-flowing powders, and maintaining uniformity in blends.</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Food grade magnesium silicate complies with food safety standards established by regulatory bodies such as the FDA, EFSA, and JECFA, ensuring its safety for human consumption within prescribed limits.</strong></p><p>
