</p><p>Additionally, the increasing trend of adopting circular economy principles, such as using recycled materials and waste products, presents a major opportunity for the renewable styrene market. <strong>Challenges:</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The renewable styrene market faces certain challenges, including the need for investment in infrastructure and technology. </p><p>Furthermore, educating industry players and consumers on the benefits of renewable styrene and overcoming resistance to change from established petrochemical-based products is another challenge. The market will need to address these issues to achieve widespread adoption.</p><p>
