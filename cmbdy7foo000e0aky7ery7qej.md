</p><p>High purity magnesium hydroxide (Mg(OH)2) is a white, odorless powder primarily used as a flame retardant, pH regulator, and antacid. Its non-toxic and environmentally friendly properties distinguish it from other flame retardants, driving its adoption in sustainable manufacturing processes.</p><p>
