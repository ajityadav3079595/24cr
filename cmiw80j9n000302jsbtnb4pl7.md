# 2-Mercaptoethanol Market, Global Outlook & Forecast 2025-2032

<p>2-Mercaptoethanol (also known as β-mercaptoethanol, BME, 2BME, 2-ME, or β-met) is a chemical compound with the molecular formula HOCH2CH2SH. It appears as a colorless liquid with a strong amine-like odor. This compound is highly soluble in water and various organic solvents, making it a versatile component in industrial applications. Its primary usage spans across multiple industries, including chemical synthesis, pharmaceuticals, crop protection, and water treatment. Due to its reactive nature, it is also widely employed as a reducing agent in biochemical and molecular biology applications.</p><p>
