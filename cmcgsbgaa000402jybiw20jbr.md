</p><p>Nutraceutical Grade Glutamine refers to a highly purified form of glutamine that meets specific standards for use in nutraceutical products. Glutamine, abbreviated as Gln, is an amino acid that plays a crucial role in various bodily functions. It is the most abundant amino acid in the human body and is integral to nitrogen transportation within muscles. Additionally, glutamine is critical for protein synthesis, immune system function, and energy restoration, making it a key ingredient in sports nutrition and clinical supplements.</p><p>
</p><p>In the context of the nutraceutical industry, glutamine is widely used in the formulation of dietary supplements that support muscle recovery, enhance metabolic function, and boost immunity.
