</p><p>Mini Desiccant Bags refer to small-sized pouches containing moisture-absorbing substances such as silica gel, activated carbon, calcium chloride, or molecular sieves. These bags are designed to protect sensitive items from humidity and condensation during storage and transportation. Their compact size makes them ideal for packaging where space is limited, such as inside individual product boxes or small equipment cases. Common applications include pharmaceutical blister packs, electronics packaging, food items, textiles, and beauty products.</p><p>
