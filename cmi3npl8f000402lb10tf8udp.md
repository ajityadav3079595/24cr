<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>

</p><p>While PPS films offer superior performance characteristics, their high production costs remain a significant barrier to wider adoption. Despite recent advancements, PPS films below 0.1mm thickness remain prone to handling challenges during conversion processes, requiring specialized equipment and increasing production costs.</p><p>
