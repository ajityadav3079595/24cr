</p><p>FRP (Fiber Reinforced Plastic) gratings are composite materials consisting of fiberglass reinforcement in a polymer matrix, offering superior corrosion resistance, lightweight properties, and high strength-to-weight ratio compared to traditional metal gratings. These gratings are widely used in industrial flooring, walkways, trench covers, and marine applications due to their non-conductive and non-sparking characteristics.</p><p>
