</p><p>Multi-layer blown films are advanced packaging materials produced by co-extruding multiple polymer layers through a blown film extrusion process. These films offer enhanced barrier properties, mechanical strength, and flexibility compared to single-layer films, making them ideal for demanding applications. Common materials used include polyethylene (PE), ethylene vinyl alcohol (EVOH), polyamide (PA), and polyvinylidene chloride (PVDC), each contributing unique functional characteristics.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand from the food &amp; beverage packaging sector, which accounted for over 42% of total consumption in 2023. Strict FDA regulations regarding food safety and the growing preference for sustainable packaging solutions are accelerating adoption. Technological advancements in extrusion equipment and the development of bio-based multilayer films are creating new growth opportunities. However, volatility in raw material prices and recycling challenges pose significant constraints to market expansion.</p><p>
