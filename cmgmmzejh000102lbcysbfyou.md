# P-Toluenethiol Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>P-Toluenethiol (CAS 106-45-6) is a colorless crystalline powder with an unpleasant odor, widely used as an intermediate in the synthesis of dyes and pharmaceutical products. This compound plays a crucial role in various industrial applications due to its reactivity and ability to introduce sulfur-containing functional groups into organic molecules.</p><p>
