</p><p>Coupling agents for polypropylene are specialized chemical additives that enhance interfacial adhesion between polypropylene matrices and fillers or reinforcements. These agents chemically modify the polymer-filler interface through silane, maleic anhydride, or titanate-based chemistries, improving mechanical properties in composite applications. While powder formulations dominate current usage, emerging liquid variants offer easier processing for thin-film applications.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by expanding automotive production in Thailand and Vietnam, where lightweight composites increasingly replace metal components.
