# Positive Electron Beam Resists Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>The Positive Electron Beam Resists market is defined by the use of specific materials in the semiconductor manufacturing process, particularly during electron beam exposure. Electron beam resists are used to create patterns on a substrate when exposed to electron beams in lithography processes. PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate) is one of the most common positive photoresists used in this process. It is formed by the polymerization of methyl methacrylate (MMA) and is utilized in various applications, including semiconductors, printed circuit boards, and liquid crystal displays (LCDs). This market plays a crucial role in the advancement of semiconductor manufacturing and nanotechnology due to its precision and adaptability.</p><p>
