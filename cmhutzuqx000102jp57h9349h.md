These compounds are increasingly preferred over synthetic alternatives due to their natural origin and multifunctional properties in bakery products, dairy, and convenience foods. </p><p>Divergent food and chemical regulations across Southeast Asian countries present challenges for glycerol ester manufacturers. Recent changes in food additive classifications in Indonesia and Malaysia have forced reformulations, disrupting established supply chains. Research into modified glycerol esters continues, but commercial-scale solutions remain limited.</p><p>
