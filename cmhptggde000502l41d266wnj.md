</p><p>Compounding this issue, improper application techniques can lead to product performance issues that may be incorrectly attributed to the material rather than workmanship. However, bridging this skills gap represents a significant challenge that will require coordinated efforts across manufacturers, trade organizations, and educational institutions to resolve effectively.</p><p>
