</p><p>Seal coatings are protective layers applied to asphalt or concrete surfaces to extend pavement life and improve aesthetics. These formulations typically consist of refined materials like <strong>coal tar, asphalt, or petroleum-based binders</strong> combined with fillers and additives. Key applications include <strong>driveways, parking lots, pavements, and airport runways</strong>, where they prevent water penetration, resist oil spills, and reduce surface deterioration from UV exposure and traffic wear.</p><p>
</p><p>While North America dominates current consumption due to its vast road networks and cold climate requirements, Asia-Pacific is emerging as a high-growth region because of rapid urbanization. Market expansion is further driven by innovations in eco-friendly formulations and the development of high-performance polymer-modified sealants. Leading manufacturers are investing in production capacity expansions to meet the growing demand for durable pavement solutions.</p><p>
