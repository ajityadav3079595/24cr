<h1>Pitch Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p><span style="font-size:16px">Pitch is a viscoelastic polymer that is derived from various sources such as petroleum, coal tar, or plants. It is often referred to as tar, bitumen, or asphalt, depending on the specific application and composition. As a versatile material, pitch is primarily used in the construction, roofing, and paving industries due to its adhesive and waterproof properties. The nature of pitch can vary significantly depending on its source and the refining process used to produce it. This polymer material has a range of applications across industries, including its use in the production of carbon materials, battery manufacturing, and even as a binder for other materials in industrial processes.</span></p>
