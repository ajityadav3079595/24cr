 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Polyolefin foam is a lightweight, closed-cell polymeric foam derived from polyolefin resin. It is primarily manufactured using an organic blowing agent and a cross-linking agent. Depending on the production process, polyolefin foam can be cross-linked through irradiation or physical means. This foam type is known for its low thermal conductivity, near-zero vapor permeability, and excellent mechanical properties, making it ideal for applications in automotive, packaging, construction, and sports equipment industries.</span></p>
