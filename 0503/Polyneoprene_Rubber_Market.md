<h1>Polyneoprene Rubber Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Polyneoprene rubber, commonly referred to as neoprene, is a synthetic rubber known for its exceptional resistance to oils, chemicals, and extreme temperatures. It is widely utilized across various industries, including automotive, construction, electronics, and healthcare. Its properties make it an ideal material for gaskets, hoses, seals, and protective coatings.</p>
