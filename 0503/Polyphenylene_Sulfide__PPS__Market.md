<h1>Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) is a high-performance engineering thermoplastic known for its exceptional chemical resistance, thermal stability, and mechanical properties. PPS is widely used in automotive, electrical, electronics, aerospace, and industrial applications due to its ability to withstand high temperatures and corrosive environments. It is commonly used in manufacturing components such as connectors, pump housings, and electronic enclosures.</p>
