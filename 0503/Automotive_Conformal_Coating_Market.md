 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Automotive conformal coatings are specialized protective materials applied to electronic circuit boards in vehicles to safeguard against environmental hazards such as moisture, dust, chemicals, and temperature fluctuations. These coatings enhance the longevity and reliability of electronic components, which are crucial in modern vehicles equipped with advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), infotainment systems, and electric vehicle (EV) powertrains. The primary types of conformal coatings used in the automotive sector include acrylic, epoxy, polyurethane, silicone, and parylene coatings.</span></p>
