 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Polyimide (PI) plastics are high-performance polymer materials characterized by the presence of imide rings (-CO-NH-CO-) in their molecular structure. These materials exhibit exceptional thermal stability, chemical resistance, mechanical strength, and electrical insulation properties. Due to their robustness, polyimide plastics are widely used in advanced industrial applications such as aerospace, automotive, electronics, and medical industries. The most common product forms include polyimide films, resins, and molded parts, which contribute significantly to modern high-performance engineering solutions.</span></p>
