 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Thermoplastic Polyolefin (TPO) is a class of high-performance polyolefins known for their exceptional durability, flexibility, and resistance to environmental factors. TPOs are widely used in automotive, construction, packaging, and consumer goods industries due to their lightweight properties and recyclability. These materials typically consist of a blend of polypropylene (PP), rubber, and reinforcing fillers, providing a balance of rigidity and impact resistance.</span></p>
 
 
 
 <p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>Increasing Demand in Automotive Industry:</strong> TPOs are extensively used in manufacturing bumpers, dashboards, and other interior automotive components due to their lightweight nature and impact resistance.</span></p>
 
 
 
 <p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>Growing Adoption in Roofing and Construction:</strong> TPO membranes are widely utilized in roofing applications due to their weather resistance and energy efficiency.</span></p>
