<h1>PNCB (P-Nitrochlorobenzene) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p><span style="font-size:13px">P-Nitrochlorobenzene (PNCB) is an organic compound with the molecular formula ClC6H6NO2. Commonly known as 4-Chloro-1-nitrobenzene, it is a pale yellow, solid chemical used in a wide array of industrial applications. PNCB is primarily synthesized through the nitration of monochlorobenzene, a process that introduces a nitro group (-NO2) at the para position of the chlorobenzene molecule. This versatile compound plays a crucial role as an intermediate in the production of other chemicals, including P-Nitroaniline (PNA), which is further processed into dyes, pharmaceuticals, and agrochemicals.</span></p>
 
 
 
 <p>The broad range of applications for P-Nitrochlorobenzene spans various industries, including dyes & pigments, pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, chemicals & polymers, and the rubber industry.
