 <p>Polyimide resin refers to a class of heterocyclic polymers containing imine groups in the main chain of macromolecules. It is one of the highest-performing organic polymer materials, known for its excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties. These resins are widely used in aerospace, electronics, automotive, and industrial applications due to their ability to withstand extreme conditions. The superior performance of polyimide resins makes them indispensable in various high-tech industries.</p>
