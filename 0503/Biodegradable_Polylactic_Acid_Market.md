 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Polylactic acid (PLA) is a biodegradable, thermoplastic polymer made primarily from renewable resources such as sugar, starch, and cellulose. PLA is considered a crucial development in the world of biodegradable plastics due to its reduced environmental impact compared to traditional petroleum-based plastics.</span></p>

 <p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>Development of New PLA Applications</strong>: PLA is being explored for use in a wide variety of industries, including the automotive sector, medical industry (biodegradable implants), and textiles.
