<h1>Sodium Undecylenate Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Sodium Undecylenate is a sodium salt derived from undecylenic acid, an organic compound primarily extracted from castor oil. It is widely used as an antifungal agent in personal care and pharmaceutical products. This compound exhibits excellent antimicrobial properties, making it a key ingredient in skincare formulations, deodorants, and medicated soaps. Additionally, Sodium Undecylenate is utilized in industrial applications, such as coatings and surfactants, due to its emulsifying properties.</p>
