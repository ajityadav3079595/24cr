<h1>Solvent-based Laminating Adhesive Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Solvent-based laminating adhesives are specialized adhesives formulated with organic solvents, providing excellent bonding strength and flexibility for multilayer applications. These adhesives are extensively used in the flexible packaging industry for food, pharmaceuticals, and industrial applications. They offer high durability, heat resistance, and superior adhesion to diverse substrates such as plastic films, aluminum foils, and paper-based materials.</p>

The growth is driven by the rising demand for flexible packaging, increasing industrial applications, and technological advancements in adhesive formulations.</p>
