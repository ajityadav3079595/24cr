</p><p>Woven fiberglass fabric is a composite material made from fine glass fibers woven into a fabric structure. It is widely used in various industrial applications due to its high tensile strength, thermal stability, and resistance to chemicals and corrosion. The fabric is available in different weights, including 2 oz, 4 oz, 6 oz, and 10 oz fiberglass fabric, each suited for specific applications.
