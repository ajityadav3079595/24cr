<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Form in Place Gasket (FIPG) is a specialized type of gasket created by applying liquid sealants onto surfaces, where they cure to form a custom-fit seal. These gaskets are widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, and industrial machinery to ensure leak-proof sealing in various applications. The technology is preferred for its efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and ability to conform to complex surface geometries, making it a superior alternative to preformed gaskets.</p><p>
