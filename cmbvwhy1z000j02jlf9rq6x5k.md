</p><p>Heat-resistant ABS, also known as high heat-resistant ABS (HH ABS), is a specialized grade of acrylonitrile butadiene styrene (ABS) that offers superior heat resistance compared to standard ABS variants. Known for its robust mechanical properties, HH ABS demonstrates excellent shock resistance, outstanding workability, and an elevated Vicat softening point. The Vicat softening temperature for high-heat-resistant ABS is 109°C, while for super-high-heat-resistant ABS, it is 115°C. This makes HH ABS an ideal material for applications that require high heat resistance and dimensional stability.</p><p>
</p><p>The applications of Heat-resistant ABS are diverse and widespread. It is commonly used in automotive parts, electrical devices, and household appliances, such as washing machines, air conditioners, and televisions. Due to its ability to withstand high temperatures without losing its structural integrity, HH ABS has become an essential material in the production of durable and high-performance household appliances.</p><p>
