</p><p>Sodium Methallyl Sulfonate (CAS 1561-92-8) is a specialty chemical compound primarily used as a reactive monomer in polyacrylonitrile fiber production. This anionic surfactant plays a critical role in improving fiber properties such as dyeability, thermal stability, and mechanical strength. Beyond textiles, it finds applications in water treatment formulations and as a paint additive where its sulfonate groups enhance dispersion and stability.</p><p>
