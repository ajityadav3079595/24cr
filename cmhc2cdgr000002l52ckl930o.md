These logistics tools enable efficient material handling by allowing forklifts with specialized attachments to push and pull unitized loads without requiring pallets. Their lightweight design reduces shipping costs while maintaining load stability across supply chains.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing adoption in food &amp; beverage and retail sectors where cost-efficient logistics solutions are prioritized.
