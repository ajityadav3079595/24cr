<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Production Costs and Price Volatility to Limit Market Penetration</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The wheat germ oil market faces significant constraints due to its premium pricing and complex production process. As wheat germ constitutes only 2.5% of the wheat kernel by weight, large quantities of raw material are required to produce commercially viable oil volumes. Cold-pressed wheat germ oil, which preserves nutritional value but yields only 8-10% oil content, typically retails at 3-5 times the price of common vegetable oils. This price premium restricts broader adoption in price-sensitive food applications and developing markets, where consumers prioritize affordability over nutritional benefits.</p><p>
