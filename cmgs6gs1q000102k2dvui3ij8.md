</p><p>Benzoin is a balsamic resin obtained from trees of the genus Styrax, commonly found in Southeast Asia. It is widely used in industries such as food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, and perfumery due to its aromatic properties and medicinal benefits. Benzoin resin is known for its antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and preservative properties, making it valuable in various applications.</p><p>
</p><p>
</p><p><strong> Market Dynamics (Drivers, Restraints, Opportunities, and Challenges)</strong></p><p>
