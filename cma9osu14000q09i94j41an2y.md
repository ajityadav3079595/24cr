</p><p>High Temperature Labels and Tags are specialized identification products designed to withstand extreme heat environments, typically ranging from 150°C to over 1000°C depending on material composition. These labels are primarily used in industries where conventional labels would degrade or become illegible due to intense heat exposure. Common applications include automotive engine components, aerospace equipment, electronics manufacturing, and metal processing.</p><p>
</p><p>Materials such as polyimide, polyester, polycarbonate, and metallized films are frequently used to manufacture these labels due to their high thermal resistance and durability. Adhesives used in these labels are also formulated to retain adhesion and legibility even after exposure to harsh heat and chemicals. As a result, they are crucial for maintaining traceability, safety standards, and operational efficiency in high-temperature environments.</p><p>
