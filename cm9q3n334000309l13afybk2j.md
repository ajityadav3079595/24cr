# Potassium Niobate Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Potassium Niobate (KNbO3) is an inorganic compound classified as a perovskite ferroelectric material. It exhibits strong nonlinear optical properties, making it a key component in various laser applications. This colorless solid is widely recognized for its piezoelectric and dielectric properties, particularly in advanced ceramics and semiconductor technologies. Potassium Niobate is also used in developing lead-free capacitors and tunable coherent light sources, enhancing its significance in modern optoelectronic and photonic applications.</p><p>
