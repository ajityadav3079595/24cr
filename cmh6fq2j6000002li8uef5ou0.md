</p><p>A Stress Absorbing Membrane Interlayer (SAMI) is a specialized paving solution composed of non-woven, stitch-bonded glass filament bundles. When combined with bitumen or asphalt layers, SAMI acts as a stress-relieving interlayer that prevents reflective cracking by absorbing low-strain stresses from underlying pavement structures. This technology effectively seals existing road cracks (including potholes), enhances water resistance, and extends pavement lifecycles – making it a critical component in modern infrastructure maintenance.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing road rehabilitation projects worldwide, particularly in North America and Asia-Pacific where aging infrastructure requires cost-effective maintenance solutions. The U.S. currently dominates the market, while China is emerging as the fastest-growing regional market due to rapid urbanization. Among product types, <strong>Asphalt Rubber SAMI</strong> holds significant market share owing to its superior elasticity and temperature resistance properties. Key industry players including Cemex, Holcim, and Tensar are expanding their SAMI product portfolios through strategic R&amp;D investments to capitalize on growing infrastructure spending globally.</p><p>
