</p><p>Divinylbenzene (DVB) is a highly versatile cross-linking agent primarily used to enhance polymer properties. As a chemical intermediate, DVB improves material strength, stability, and performance, making it essential in various industrial applications. It plays a critical role in manufacturing adhesives, plastics, elastomers, ceramics, biological materials, coatings, catalysts, membranes, pharmaceuticals, specialty polymers, and ion exchange resins. The most significant application of DVB is in producing cross-linked polystyrene resin beads, which serve as the foundation for ion exchange resins.</p><p>
