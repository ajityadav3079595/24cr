</p><p>Diisopropylaminosilane (DIPAS) is a specialized organosilicon compound primarily used as a precursor in semiconductor and display manufacturing. This high-purity chemical plays a critical role in vapor deposition processes for thin-film transistor (TFT) production and advanced semiconductor packaging. The compound's unique properties enable precise deposition of silicon nitride and other dielectric layers essential for modern electronic devices.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is being driven by Southeast Asia's expanding semiconductor manufacturing sector, particularly in Malaysia, Singapore, and Vietnam.
