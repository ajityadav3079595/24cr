# Hinge for Furniture Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Hinges for furniture are essential hardware components that enable doors, cabinets, and other furniture pieces to open and close smoothly. These hinges come in various types, including concealed, butt, pivot, and European hinges, each serving specific applications based on aesthetic and functional requirements. They are primarily made from materials such as stainless steel, brass, and zinc alloy to ensure durability and corrosion resistance. The growing demand for modern and multifunctional furniture designs has significantly influenced the hinge market, making it a critical segment in the furniture industry.</p><p>
