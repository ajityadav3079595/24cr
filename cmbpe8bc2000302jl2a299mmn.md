# Global Polyurethane Foam (PUR) Sandwich Panel Market Research Report 2025 -2032(Status and Outlook)

<p>Polyurethane Foam Sandwich Panel refers to a construction and insulation material composed of a core layer of rigid polyurethane foam bonded between two outer layers, typically made of metal, such as steel or aluminum. These panels offer excellent thermal insulation, strength, and lightweight properties, making them highly suitable for applications in industrial buildings, cold storage facilities, commercial infrastructures, and residential constructions. The polyurethane foam core is particularly valued for its low thermal conductivity, contributing to energy efficiency and sustainability in modern building designs.</p><p>
</p><p>These panels are prefabricated and easy to install, significantly reducing construction time and labor costs.
