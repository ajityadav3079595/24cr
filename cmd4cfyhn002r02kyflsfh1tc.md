<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Rising Health Awareness and Demand for Functional Foods to Drive Sterol Market Growth</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Global sterol market is experiencing significant growth due to increasing consumer awareness about health and wellness. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Regulatory Support for Functional Ingredients to Boost Market</strong></p><p>

The European market has seen particularly strong growth following EFSA's approval of health claims for sterol-fortified foods. This regulatory support has encouraged manufacturers to invest in product development and marketing, further stimulating market expansion across food and nutraceutical sectors.</p><p>

</p><p>While demand for sterols is growing, high production costs remain a significant challenge. In price-sensitive markets, this price premium can limit adoption, particularly among lower-income consumers who could benefit most from cholesterol-lowering products.</p><p>

Additionally, the approval process for new sterol-based products can be lengthy and expensive, particularly in markets with stringent food additive regulations.</p><p>
