</p><p>Dispersing Agent MF (CAS 9084-06-4), also known as dispersant MF, is a formaldehyde condensate of sodium methylnaphthalene sulfonate. This anionic surfactant is synthesized through sulfonation, hydrolysis, condensation with formaldehyde, and neutralization processes using methyl naphthalene or washing oil as raw materials. The compound exhibits excellent diffusibility, thermal stability, and resistance to acid, alkali, hard water, and inorganic salts. As a brown powder soluble in water, it finds extensive application in improving pigment dispersion across multiple industries.</p><p>
