</p><p>Glue, also known as adhesive, cement, mucilage, or paste, refers to any non-metallic substance that is applied to the surface of materials to bind them together and prevent separation. These adhesives are formulated to create durable bonds between materials ranging from paper and wood to metal and plastic.</p><p>

The market includes both natural and synthetic adhesives, with innovations focusing increasingly on bio-based and environmentally sustainable options.</p><p>
