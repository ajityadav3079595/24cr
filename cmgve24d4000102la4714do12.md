</p><p>Powder Glass, commonly referred to as Glass Pastes, is a highly specialized material derived by grinding glass into a fine powder and mixing it with a binder and a fluxing agent. This unique mixture results in a paste that is applicable in various industrial processes, particularly in precision electronic applications. The glass powder acts as a functional material for sealing, bonding, or insulation purposes, while the flux helps facilitate melting at relatively low temperatures.</p><p>
</p><p>By adjusting the combination of different types of glass, ceramics, and their blending ratios, manufacturers can tailor the properties of the glass paste, including its sealing temperature, thermal expansion coefficient, and electrical properties. These versatile characteristics make Powder Glass (Glass Pastes) essential in a variety of high-precision and high-performance applications, such as electronics, semiconductors, LED &amp; OLED manufacturing, automotive electronics, and even photovoltaic modules.</p><p>
