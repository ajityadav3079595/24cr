This functional sugar consists of two galactose units, one glucose unit, and one fructose unit, making it valuable for digestive health applications. The compound is primarily categorized by purity levels (P60, P70, P80, P90) with increasing demand for higher purity variants in pharmaceutical applications.</p><p>
