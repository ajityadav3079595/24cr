</p><p>Carbon steel pipe fittings are components used to connect pipe sections in piping systems, providing directional control and ensuring secure flow of liquids or gases. These fittings include elbows, tees, reducers, couplings, and flanges, manufactured through either hot roll or cold roll processes to meet different pressure and temperature requirements. Their durability, cost-effectiveness, and high strength-to-weight ratio make them essential in industrial applications.</p><p>
