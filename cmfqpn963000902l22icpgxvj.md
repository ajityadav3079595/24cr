The rising awareness of health and wellness, along with the growing prevalence of vitamin deficiencies, is further propelling market expansion.</p><p>
</p><p>Fat-soluble vitamins are a group of vitamins that dissolve in fats and oils and are stored in the body's fatty tissues. These vitamins include <strong>Vitamin A, Vitamin D, Vitamin E, and Vitamin K</strong>. Unlike water-soluble vitamins, fat-soluble vitamins are absorbed through the intestinal tract with the help of fats and are stored in the liver and adipose tissues for long-term use. These vitamins play crucial roles in various bodily functions, including vision, bone health, immune function, and blood clotting.
