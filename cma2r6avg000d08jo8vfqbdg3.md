# Latin America Agar Market Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Agar is a natural polysaccharide extracted from red seaweed, commonly used as a gelling agent in the food, pharmaceutical, and biotechnology industries. It serves as an essential ingredient in microbiological culture media, plant tissue cultures, and various culinary applications. Its ability to form strong, stable gels at low concentrations makes it a preferred alternative to gelatin, especially in vegetarian and vegan products.</p><p>
