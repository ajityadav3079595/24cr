</p><p>PA6 Full Dull Chip is a specialized polyamide 6 variant manufactured by incorporating titanium dioxide (≥0.3%) and other matting agents during caprolactam polymerization. These white cylindrical particles exhibit a melting range of 210°C-220°C with decomposition temperatures near 300°C. The material demonstrates exceptional electrical insulation properties alongside notable resistance to alkalis and corrosion. Its primary applications include manufacturing full-dull nylon filaments, industrial yarns, and textile fibers, serving diverse sectors like automotive interiors, industrial textiles, and consumer apparel.</p><p>
</p><p>Market expansion is driven by increasing demand from the textile and automotive sectors, particularly in Asia-Pacific where China accounts for over 35% of global consumption.
