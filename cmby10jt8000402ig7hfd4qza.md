# 4,4'-Diamino-2,2'-Stilbenedisulfonic Acid Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032, by Manufacturers, Regions, Technology, Application

<p>4,4'-Diamino-2,2'-Stilbenedisulfonic Acid (DASDA) is a vital chemical compound primarily used in the production of optical brighteners, dyestuffs, and insecticides. It is an organic compound derived from stilbene, characterized by its high fluorescence properties, making it an essential component in the textile, paper, and detergent industries. DASDA enhances brightness in various materials by absorbing ultraviolet light and emitting visible blue light, counteracting yellowing effects.</p><p>
