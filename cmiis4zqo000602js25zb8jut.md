</p><p>Polyoxytetramethylene (PTMG) is a key polyether glycol primarily used in spandex fiber and polyurethane (PU) resin production. This specialty chemical exhibits excellent elasticity, chemical resistance, and thermal stability, making it indispensable in applications ranging from stretchable textiles to high-performance coatings. The market offers multiple product grades including PTMG 650, PTMG 1000, and PTMG 1800/2000, each tailored for specific end-use requirements.</p><p>
