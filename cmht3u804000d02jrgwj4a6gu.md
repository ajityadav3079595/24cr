</p><p>Cubic silicon carbide whiskers (β-SiC) are advanced ceramic materials known for their exceptional mechanical properties and thermal stability. These whiskers exhibit high hardness (Mohs hardness exceeding 9.5), superior toughness, and excellent resistance to corrosion, radiation, and extreme temperatures up to 1600°C. As single-crystal fibers with diameters typically ranging from 0.1-50μm, they find critical applications as reinforcement materials in metal matrix composites and ceramic matrix composites across demanding industries.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from aerospace and defense sectors, where β-SiC whiskers enhance the strength-to-weight ratio of turbine blades and thermal protection systems. Furthermore, the automotive industry's shift toward lightweight materials and the electronics sector's need for thermal management solutions contribute to market expansion. Asia-Pacific dominates consumption, accounting for over 40% of global demand in 2024, led by China's advanced manufacturing sector. Key players like Tateho Chemical and Xi'an Boer New Material are investing in production capacity expansions to meet growing requirements from composite material manufacturers.</p><p>
