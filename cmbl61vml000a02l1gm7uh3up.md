# Sodium-Ion Battery Electrolyte Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Sodium-ion battery electrolyte is a crucial component in sodium-ion batteries, serving as a medium for ion transport between the cathode and anode. These electrolytes consist of sodium salts dissolved in solvents such as ethylene carbonate and propylene carbonate. Compared to lithium-ion batteries, sodium-ion batteries offer cost advantages, better sustainability, and improved safety, making them an attractive alternative for energy storage applications.</p><p>
