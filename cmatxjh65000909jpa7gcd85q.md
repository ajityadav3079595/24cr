# Hydroxypropyl Methyl Cellulose (HPMC) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Hydroxypropyl Methyl Cellulose (HPMC) is a semisynthetic, inert, viscoelastic polymer derived from cellulose. It is commonly used as an ophthalmic lubricant, an excipient in pharmaceutical formulations, and a thickening agent in industrial and food applications. Due to its non-toxic and biodegradable nature, HPMC is widely utilized in various industries, including construction, pharmaceuticals, personal care, and food processing.</p><p>
