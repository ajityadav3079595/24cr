</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Production Costs Limiting Market Penetration</strong></p><p>
</p><p>While demand grows, the protective wear fabrics market faces challenges from high manufacturing costs associated with specialized materials. Environmental regulations regarding synthetic fabric disposal are becoming stricter, with the EU implementing new textile waste directives that could add 15-20% to end-of-life processing costs for protective wear manufacturers by 2026.</p><p>

</p><p>The integration of IoT and wearable technologies into protective fabrics presents a transformative opportunity.
