<strong>Market Overview</strong></p><p>
</p><p>
</p><p><strong>Benzophenone-1 Market</strong> refers to the global industry centered around the production, distribution, and application of Benzophenone-1, a widely used organic compound belonging to the benzophenone family. Benzophenone-1, also known as BP-1 or 2,4-dihydroxybenzophenone, is primarily employed in UV protection formulations due to its effective ultraviolet light absorption properties. It is extensively used in personal care products like sunscreens, lotions, and cosmetics to prevent UV-induced skin damage. Additionally, Benzophenone-1 finds application in plastics, coatings, adhesives, and other industrial sectors to enhance durability and prevent photodegradation.</p><p>
