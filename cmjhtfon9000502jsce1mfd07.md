</p><p>Novolac resins are phenol-formaldehyde thermosetting polymers characterized by their excellent heat resistance, chemical stability, and mechanical strength. These resins are widely used as binders, coatings, and adhesives across industries due to their superior performance in high-temperature applications. The product exists in two primary forms - thermoplastic and thermosetting - each catering to specific industrial requirements.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is driven by increasing demand from construction and automotive sectors, where these resins are essential for manufacturing heat-resistant components. However, environmental regulations concerning formaldehyde emissions pose challenges. Key players like Hexion and BASF are investing in sustainable formulations to address these concerns while expanding their production capacities to meet growing industrial demand, particularly in Asia-Pacific emerging markets.</p><p>
