</p><p>Bulk Molding Compound (BMC) is a thermoset plastic resin blend of inert fillers, fiber reinforcement, catalysts, stabilizers, and pigments. It forms a viscous, 'puttylike' injection molding compound highly filled and reinforced with short fibers. BMC offers excellent electrical and mechanical properties, heat resistance, and chemical corrosion resistance, making it suitable for various industries including automobiles, railway transportation, construction, and mechanical/electrical products.</p><p>
