Fluorine Containing Electronic Special Gas refers to a category of highly specialized gases that contain fluorine and are critical in various electronic manufacturing processes. These gases are essential for producing high-purity semiconductors and other electronic components. The applications of these gases span across industries such as integrated circuits (ICs), photovoltaic (PV) new energy, and flat-panel displays (FPDs). Examples of fluorine-containing gases include sulfur hexafluoride (SF6), nitrogen trifluoride (NF3), carbon tetrafluoride (CF4), and others.</p><p>
