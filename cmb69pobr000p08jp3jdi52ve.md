End User Industry Analysis</p><p>
</p><p>The <strong>automotive industry</strong> is a major consumer of rubber tubes, using them in fuel lines, brake systems, and air conditioning. The <strong>agricultural sector</strong> relies on rubber hoses for irrigation and pesticide application. The <strong>oil &amp; gas industry</strong> uses high-pressure rubber tubes for drilling and fluid transfer. The <strong>food &amp; beverage sector</strong> requires food-grade rubber tubes for liquid transport. The <strong>chemical industry</strong> utilizes chemical-resistant rubber tubes for handling corrosive substances. The <strong>mining industry</strong> employs heavy-duty rubber tubes for slurry transport and ventilation.</p><p>
