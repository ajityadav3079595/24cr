Driver</p><p>
</p><p>The <strong>growing demand for eco-friendly varnish</strong> is a major driver, as consumer and manufacturer increasingly prefer low-VOC and waterborne coating. Restraint</p><p>
</p><p>Stringent <strong>environmental regulation</strong> on VOC emission pose challenge for solventborne varnish manufacturer. The <strong>high cost of waterborne varnish</strong> compared to traditional solventborne product also limit market growth in price-sensitive region.</p><p>
Opportunity</p><p>

Challenge</p><p>
