<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>
</p><p>1,4-Butanediol Monovinyl Ether (HBVE), also known as Hydroxybutyl Vinyl Ether, is a colorless liquid compound used extensively in various industrial applications. Its primary roles include serving as a reactive diluent for radiation-curable coatings, a comonomer in specialty coatings manufacturing, a monomer for tackifying adhesives, and a functional monomer for weathering-resistant and radiation-curing paints. Due to its versatile chemical properties, HBVE contributes significantly to the production of high-performance materials, especially in the coatings and adhesives industries.</p><p>
