These resins play a critical role in separating electrodes while facilitating ion transport, making them essential components in fuel cells, electrolyzers, and redox flow batteries. The market offers both natural and synthetic resin variants, with key properties including chemical stability, proton conductivity, and mechanical strength under varying operational conditions.</p><p>
