</p><p>Tris(hydroxymethyl)aminomethane (TRIS or THAM) is an organic amine compound with the formula (HOCH2)3CNH2 that serves as a <strong>versatile buffering agent</strong> in biochemical applications. Its unique properties make it indispensable in molecular biology for nucleic acid solutions (TAE/TBE buffers), protein research, and pharmaceutical formulations.
