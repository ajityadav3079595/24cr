</p><p>While silicone fluids excel in many applications, their hydrophobic nature can create formulation challenges with certain hydrophilic active pharmaceutical ingredients. This incompatibility leads some drug manufacturers to seek alternative excipients, particularly for aqueous-based formulations where silicone's interfacial properties may cause stability issues.</p><p>
