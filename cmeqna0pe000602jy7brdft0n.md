These grinding media exhibit superior hardness, wear resistance, and impact toughness compared to conventional steel balls, making them critical components in ball mills and vertical roller mills. The product segments include Normal High Chrome Type (10%-18% Cr) and Special High Chrome Type (18%-30% Cr), with the latter gaining traction in high-abrasion applications.</p><p>
