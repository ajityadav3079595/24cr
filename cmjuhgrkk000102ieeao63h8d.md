</p><p>Thermal latent curing agents are specialty chemicals that initiate cross-linking reactions in epoxy resins and other thermosetting polymers only when exposed to specific elevated temperatures. These agents remain chemically inert at room temperature but activate upon heating, enabling controlled curing processes in applications requiring precise timing and temperature conditions. Major product categories include amine-based, anhydride-based, and imidazole-based curing systems, with varying activation temperatures ranging from 80Â°C to over 200Â°C.</p><p>
