</p><p>Ground protection mats are temporary surface coverings designed to distribute heavy loads and prevent ground damage across construction sites, events, and industrial applications. These mats come in various materials including composite, rubber, and high-density polyethylene (HDPE), with thickness options ranging from 10mm to 20mm for different weight capacities. Their primary function is to provide stability while protecting underlying surfaces from rutting, erosion, and compaction.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing infrastructure development projects globally, particularly in emerging economies where urbanization rates are accelerating. However, North America currently dominates demand due to strict environmental regulations requiring ground protection in sensitive areas.
