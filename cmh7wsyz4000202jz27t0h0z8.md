</p><p>CT Contrast Media APIs (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients) are chemical compounds used in contrast agents for computed tomography (CT) scans to enhance the visibility of internal structures. These APIs include iodine-based compounds such as Iohexol, Iodixanol, Iopamidol, and Ioversol, which improve image clarity by increasing the contrast between tissues. These agents are essential in diagnostic imaging for detecting tumors, vascular diseases, and other medical conditions. The market includes APIs supplied to pharmaceutical companies and research institutions for drug formulation and clinical studies.</p><p>
