</p><p>Sodium methyl cocoyl taurate is a mild, sulfate-free anionic surfactant primarily used in personal care products such as shampoos, body washes, facial cleansers, and baby care formulations. Derived from coconut fatty acids and taurate, this ingredient is prized for its gentle cleansing properties, excellent foaming behavior, and high skin compatibility. Unlike traditional sulfates, which can strip the skin of natural oils and cause irritation, sodium methyl cocoyl taurate helps maintain skin's natural moisture barrier, making it suitable for sensitive skin types. It is also biodegradable, aligning with the growing demand for eco-friendly and sustainable ingredients in the cosmetics industry.</p><p>
