</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Increasing Demand for Floral and Spicy Fragrances in Personal Care Products</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Global dihydroeugenol market is experiencing significant growth due to rising consumer preference for floral and spicy fragrance profiles in personal care products. </p><p>The dihydroeugenol market faces significant challenges from fluctuating raw material costs, particularly for its key feedstock eugenol. This unpredictability creates pricing pressures for dihydroeugenol manufacturers, who must either absorb cost increases or risk losing customers to synthetic alternatives.
