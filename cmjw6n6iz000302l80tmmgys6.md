</p><p>Despite its numerous applications, potassium oxalate faces significant regulatory challenges that restrain market growth. </p><p>Additionally, the chemical's toxicity profile raises concerns about occupational exposure and environmental impact. These safety considerations have led to increased scrutiny from regulatory bodies and may prompt further restrictions, particularly in consumer-facing applications.</p><p>
