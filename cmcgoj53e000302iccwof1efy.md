</p><p>Fiberglass multi-end roving is a specialized reinforcement material composed of multiple glass fiber strands bundled together without twisting. These rovings are crucial for composite manufacturing processes like pultrusion, filament winding, and spray-up applications, offering high strength-to-weight ratios and corrosion resistance. The product segments include continuous multi-end roving for structural applications and chopped multi-end roving for injection molding.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing infrastructure development and automotive lightweighting initiatives across Southeast Asia. Thailand and Indonesia collectively account for over 45% of regional demand, supported by their expanding construction and transportation sectors.
