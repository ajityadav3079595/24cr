<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Growing Global HIV Prevalence to Accelerate Market Expansion</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The increasing global burden of HIV infections remains a primary driver for the Tenofovir Alafenamide Fumarate (TAF) market.
