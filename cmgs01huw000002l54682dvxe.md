</p><p>Copper coated wire for welding purpose refers to a specialized type of welding wire that is coated with a thin layer of copper to enhance its conductivity, corrosion resistance, and overall performance in welding applications. These wires are primarily used in submerged arc welding (SAW), metal inert gas (MIG) welding, and tungsten inert gas (TIG) welding processes. The copper coating ensures better electrical contact, reduces oxidation, and improves the wire's durability, making it ideal for high-performance welding tasks. Key product segments in this market include <strong>Copper Coated SAW Wire, Copper Coated MIG Wire, and Copper Coated TIG Wire</strong>, each tailored for specific welding applications.
