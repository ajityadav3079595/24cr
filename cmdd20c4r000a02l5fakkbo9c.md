# Solid Sodium Cyanide Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>
</p><p>Sodium Cyanide (NaCN) is a highly toxic yet crucial chemical compound widely utilized in industrial applications. As a white, water-soluble solid, Sodium Cyanide plays an essential role as a basic chemical raw material. It is extensively used in gold mining, where it facilitates the extraction of gold and silver through cyanidation. Other applications include electroplating, chemical synthesis, and pharmaceuticals. Despite its toxic nature, the versatility and effectiveness of Sodium Cyanide ensure its continued demand across multiple sectors.</p><p>
