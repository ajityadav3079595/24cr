Market Restraints
</p><p>Despite the positive outlook, the market faces certain challenges. </p><p>Another challenge is the <strong>limited availability of raw materials</strong> in certain regions. While copper is widely available, high-purity gallium is less common and requires complex extraction and refinement processes. This creates supply chain challenges that can affect production consistency and pricing.</p><p>
</p><p>The market also faces challenges from <strong>stringent environmental regulations</strong> regarding the use and disposal of certain materials used in sputtering target production. Compliance with these regulations adds to operational costs and complexity.</p><p>
