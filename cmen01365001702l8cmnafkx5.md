# Polyurethane Catalysts Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Polyurethane catalysts are chemical compounds that accelerate the reaction between polyols and isocyanates to form polyurethane products. These catalysts play a crucial role in determining the physical properties, reaction speed, and overall performance of polyurethane-based materials. Polyurethane catalysts are broadly classified into two categories:</p><p>
</p><p><strong>Gelling Catalysts</strong>: These catalysts facilitate the reaction between isocyanate and hydroxyl groups, helping form the polymer network.</p><div><b>Download FREE Sample of this Report @
