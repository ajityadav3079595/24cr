<p>The Global Post-Consumer Recycled (PCR) PET Resin Market refers to the industry focused on producing and supplying recycled polyethylene terephthalate (PET) resin derived from post-consumer waste, such as plastic bottles. This market plays a pivotal role in sustainability by reducing plastic waste and promoting circular economy principles. PCR PET resin is widely used in industries such as food and beverage packaging, personal care, cosmetics, and more, offering an eco-friendly alternative to virgin PET.</p><p>
