</p><p>Butylated Tripyl Phosphate is a chemical compound primarily used as a flame retardant, lubricant, and hydraulic fluid additive. It is a derivative of phosphoric acid, known for its thermal stability and flame-retardant properties. The compound is widely utilized in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics due to its ability to enhance fire resistance and lubrication efficiency.
