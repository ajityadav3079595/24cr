It functions as a photosynthesis inhibitor, effectively controlling broadleaf weeds and annual grasses in key crops including soybeans, carrots, and potatoes. Monitoring programs in agricultural regions frequently detect linuron residues, prompting tighter usage restrictions in vulnerable watersheds.</p><p>
