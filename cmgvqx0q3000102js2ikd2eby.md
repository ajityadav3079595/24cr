</p><p>The biotin market refers to the global industry revolving around the production, distribution, and consumption of biotin (also known as vitamin B7). Biotin is a water-soluble B-vitamin that plays an essential role in various metabolic processes in the body, including the breakdown of fatty acids, amino acids, and glucose. Biotin has been widely recognized for its importance in maintaining healthy hair, skin, and nails, making it a key ingredient in personal care products. It is also utilized in the pharmaceutical sector for treating conditions such as biotin deficiency and other metabolic disorders.</p><p>
</p><p>In addition to its biological significance, biotin is incorporated into various dietary supplements that support overall wellness, and as an ingredient in functional foods and beverages. The growing awareness of health, wellness, and self-care is driving demand for biotin across several industries. In the context of the biotin market, the key products and services include synthetic biotin, biotin-based formulations, dietary supplements, and biotin-enriched personal care products.</p><p>
