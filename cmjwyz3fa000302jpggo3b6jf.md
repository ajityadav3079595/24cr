# Crack Sealing and Crack Filling Market Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Crack sealing and crack filling are essential techniques for maintaining and extending the lifecycle of asphalt and concrete pavements. Crack sealing involves applying a hot-applied sealant to prevent water and debris from entering cracks in the pavement. Crack filling, on the other hand, uses a different approach to fill non-working cracks with non-movable openings. These processes are highly cost-effective, protecting pavements from further deterioration and reducing maintenance expenses in the long term.</p><p>
