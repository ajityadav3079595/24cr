This colorless liquid with a mild odor has the chemical formula CH3C(OH)(CH3)2CH2 and serves multiple industrial applications including lubricant additives and coating solvents. While water solubility is limited, MIBC mixes well with most organic solvents, making it versatile for various formulations.</p><p>
