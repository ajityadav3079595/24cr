<h1>Alkyl Polyglycoside (APG) Market, Global Outlook &amp; Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Alkyl polyglycosides (APGs) are non-ionic surfactants derived from renewable raw materials such as sugars and fatty alcohols. These surfactants are widely used in various industrial and household applications due to their biodegradability, low toxicity, and excellent detergency. The increasing preference for sustainable and eco-friendly surfactants has propelled the demand for APGs in multiple sectors, including cosmetics, detergents, and industrial cleaners.</p><p>
