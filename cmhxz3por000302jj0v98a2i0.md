</p><p>Desiccant wheels are rotary dehumidification devices that use adsorbent materials like silica gel or molecular sieves to control humidity in air streams. As the wheel rotates, it alternately absorbs moisture from incoming air and releases it in a regeneration zone, typically heated by water coils or direct-fired gas burners. These systems play a critical role in maintaining precise humidity levels across industrial and commercial applications.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing adoption in lithium battery manufacturing, where moisture control is critical for production quality, along with expanding applications in pharmaceuticals, food processing, and electronics. Technological advancements, including IoT-enabled humidity monitoring and energy-efficient materials, are further accelerating market expansion. Key players like Seibu Giken, DRI, and Trane are investing in next-generation desiccant wheel solutions to cater to evolving industry requirements.</p><p>
