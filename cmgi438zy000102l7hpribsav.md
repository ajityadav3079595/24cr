These dyes are applied as fine aqueous dispersions at high temperatures, allowing the dye molecules to penetrate hydrophobic fibers. The market includes various dye types such as azo dyes, anthraquinone dyes and heterocyclic disperse dyes.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is being driven by increasing demand for synthetic textiles in the apparel industry, particularly in emerging economies.
