</p><p>Calcium zinc stabilizers are specialized additives crucial for polyvinyl chloride (PVC) processing and durability. These stabilizers prevent thermal degradation during manufacturing while enhancing UV resistance and weatherability in final products. The compound typically contains calcium salts, zinc salts, lubricants, and antioxidants in carefully balanced formulations.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth stems from stringent environmental regulations phasing out lead-based stabilizers across major economies, coupled with expanding PVC applications in construction and healthcare.
