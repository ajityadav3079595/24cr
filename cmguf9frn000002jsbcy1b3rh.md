Hindered amine light stabilizers (HALS) are chemical compounds that contain an amine functional group and are utilized as stabilizers in plastics and polymers. These compounds do not absorb UV radiation but work to inhibit the degradation of the polymer. HALS play a crucial role in trapping free radicals, thus helping to retain surface properties like gloss and prevent paint cracking and chalking.
