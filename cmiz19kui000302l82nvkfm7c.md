Its ability to neutralize residual hydrogen peroxide - commonly used as a sterilizing agent in food packaging - makes it indispensable for maintaining product quality and safety standards.</p><p>

</p><p>Moreover, the lack of harmonized international standards for enzyme activity measurement continues to complicate quality control processes, particularly for manufacturers operating in multiple regions.</p><p>
