<h1>Polyurethane Joint Sealant Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polyurethane joint sealant is a type of sealing material derived from polyurethane, a synthetic polymer. It is available as a one-component or two-component formulation and is primarily used in various construction applications to fill and seal joints. These sealants offer superior adhesion, flexibility, and resistance to environmental factors, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications. The versatility of polyurethane joint sealants extends to sectors such as automotive, marine, and infrastructure, where they play a crucial role in structural integrity and durability.</p><p>
