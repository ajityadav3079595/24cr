<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Powder coating for pipes refers to a dry finishing process where fine particles of resin and pigment are electrostatically applied to pipes and then cured under heat to form a durable, uniform, and high-quality finish. Unlike liquid coatings, powder coatings do not require solvents, making them an environmentally friendly solution for industrial applications. The primary advantages of powder coatings for pipes include high corrosion resistance, superior durability, cost-effectiveness, and compliance with stringent environmental regulations.</p><p>
