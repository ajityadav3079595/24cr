<h1>PTFE Tape Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) tape, also known as plumber’s tape, is a non-adhesive tape primarily used for sealing threaded pipe joints. It serves multiple purposes, including preventing leaks, reducing friction between pipe threads, and facilitating easier disassembly. PTFE tape is widely utilized across industries such as plumbing, automotive, aerospace, electronics, and chemical processing due to its high resistance to heat, chemicals, and corrosion. The tape is available in various densities and grades to suit specific applications, making it a crucial component in mechanical and industrial operations.</p><p>
