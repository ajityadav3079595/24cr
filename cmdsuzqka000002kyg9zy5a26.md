</p><p>Polyarylate resin is a high-performance thermoplastic polymer known for its excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties. It is widely used in industries requiring durable and heat-resistant materials. The resin is synthesized through the polymerization of aromatic dicarboxylic acids and dihydric phenols, resulting in a material that can withstand extreme conditions. Key applications include automotive components, medical devices, and consumer electronics, where durability and resistance to environmental factors are critical.
