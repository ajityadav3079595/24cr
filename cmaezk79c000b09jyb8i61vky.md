# Polyurethane Screen Panel Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Polyurethane screen panels are high-performance screening media used in vibrating screens across industries such as metallurgy, mining, chemicals, and construction. These panels, made from polyurethane elastomers, offer superior wear resistance, extended service life, and excellent flexibility. Their design allows for efficient material separation, making them a preferred choice over traditional metal screens.</p><p>
