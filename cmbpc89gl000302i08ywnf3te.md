Historical Growth Trends</p><p>
High Purity Hydrogen Peroxide (H2O2) is a critical chemical compound widely used in the semiconductor industry for cleaning and etching semiconductor wafers. It is an essential material in wafer fabrication, helping remove organic and inorganic contaminants from silicon surfaces before further processing. Due to the growing demand for semiconductors in electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, the market for high purity hydrogen peroxide has been expanding significantly.</p><p>
</p><p><strong>2018-2022:</strong> The market witnessed steady growth due to the increasing production of consumer electronics and smart devices.</p><p><strong>2022-2024:</strong> Semiconductor shortages during the pandemic highlighted the need for supply chain improvements, leading to increased investments in chemical purity.</p><p><strong>2024-2030:</strong> With the rise of AI, IoT, and 5G technologies, semiconductor manufacturers are ramping up production, boosting the demand for high purity hydrogen peroxide.</p><p>
