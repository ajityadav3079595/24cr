</p><p>Hydrazine sulfate is a salt formed from hydrazine and sulfuric acid. It serves as a crucial chemical intermediate and reagent used in the synthesis of a range of organic compounds. This includes the production of azobisisobutyronitrile (AIBN), a common radical initiator. The compound is also used in the manufacture of pharmaceuticals, pesticides, sterilants, and other specialty chemicals. Additionally, it acts as a reducing agent during electroless plating and functions as a foaming agent in plastics and rubber processing industries.</p><p>
</p><p><strong>Market Dynamics (Drivers, Restraints, Opportunities, and Challenges)</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Drivers</strong></p><p>
