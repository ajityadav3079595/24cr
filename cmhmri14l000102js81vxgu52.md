These ceramics include barium titanate (BaTiO3), lead zirconate titanate (PZT), calcium titanate (CaTiO3), and strontium titanate (SrTiO3), among others. Their unique characteristics make them indispensable in electronics, energy storage, sensors, and environmental applications.</p><p>
