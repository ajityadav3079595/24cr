These copolymers are synthesized from dimethylaminoethyl methacrylate (DMAEMA) and other methacrylic esters, offering pH-dependent solubility characteristics crucial for targeted drug delivery systems. The material is classified into two primary grades based on purity: above 99.5% and above 99%, with applications spanning tablets, capsules, and other pharmaceutical formulations.</p><p>
