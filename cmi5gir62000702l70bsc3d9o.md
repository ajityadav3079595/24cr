These pastes are primarily categorized into <strong>waterborne</strong> and <strong>oily</strong> formulations, with applications spanning lithium batteries, antistatic coatings, and other advanced electronic components. The material’s unique properties, such as low percolation threshold and high surface area, make it ideal for next-generation energy storage and flexible electronics.</p><p>

Graphene's lightweight and flexible nature make it suitable for applications in flexible displays and printed electronics, further expanding its market potential.</p><p>
