# Refined Functional Carbohydrates Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>The <strong style="font-size:13px">Refined Functional Carbohydrates (RFC™)</strong> market revolves around the use of carbohydrates derived from yeast that offer functional benefits to various industries, particularly in agriculture, livestock, and aquaculture. RFC™ products are utilized to alleviate various production stressors in animals, improving profitability in agricultural and livestock operations. These carbohydrates act through several mechanisms, such as managing cryptosporidiosis and coccidiosis, reducing bacterial morbidity and mortality, and decreasing mycotoxin damage, thereby enhancing animal health, growth, and overall productivity.</p><p>
</p><p>The products in this market primarily consist of functional ingredients like <strong>mannan oligosaccharides</strong>, <strong>beta-glucan</strong>, and <strong>D-mannose</strong>, which are incorporated into feed formulations for cattle, swine, poultry, and aquaculture species. The use of RFC™ products aids in optimizing the immune system, reducing inflammation, and improving nutrient absorption, among other benefits.
