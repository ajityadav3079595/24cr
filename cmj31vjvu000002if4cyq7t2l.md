</p><p>The colloidal oatmeal market faces persistent supply chain challenges that affect both product quality and availability. These technical demands limit the number of qualified suppliers capable of producing pharmaceutical-grade colloidal oatmeal, creating potential bottlenecks as market demand grows.</p><p>
</p><p><strong>Raw Material Sourcing Concerns</strong></p><p>
