</p><p>The global stockinette market refers to the production and distribution of a stretchable, tubular fabric primarily used in medical and healthcare applications. Stockinette is commonly made from materials such as cotton, nylon, or synthetic blends and is widely used for wound dressing, surgical bandages, and orthopedic applications.
