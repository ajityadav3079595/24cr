</p><p>Aerosols are pressurized dispensers that release fine sprays or mists of liquid or solid particles suspended in gas. These products are categorized into upright aerosols, designed for upright use (spray paints, lubricants, cleaners), and inverted aerosols, formulated for upside-down application (hairsprays, sunscreens, air fresheners). The technology relies on propellants and specialized valves to control product dispersion, offering precision and convenience across industries.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is driven by increasing demand in construction, automotive, and personal care sectors, where aerosol applications enhance efficiency. While inverted aerosols dominate 54% of product types, construction leads applications with 22% market share.
