# Illite Market, Global Outlook & Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Illite is a secondary mineral precipitate and a member of the phyllosilicate group, a category of layered alumino-silicates. This mineral is commonly found in hydrothermal and weathering environments as an alteration product of muscovite and feldspar. Structurally, illite is similar to muscovite but has a higher silicon, magnesium, and iron content, with a lower tetrahedral aluminum and interlayer potassium composition.</p><p>
</p><p>Due to its unique properties, illite is widely used in various industries, including coatings and paints, rubber and plastic manufacturing, ceramics, personal care, and cosmetics.
