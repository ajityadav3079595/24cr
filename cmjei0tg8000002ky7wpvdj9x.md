</p><p>A raised access floor comprises load bearing floor panels laid in a horizontal grid supported by adjustable vertical pedestals to provide an under floor space for the housing and distribution of services. Calcium Sulphate Raised Access Floor is a raised floor system which uses a calcium sulphate tongue and groove panel which are then glued on joints for additional structural support and bond. The panels are available in a variety of thicknesses and provide a fantastic surface to apply an assortment of finishes such as ceramic tiles, sheet vinyl, wood and stone.</p><p>
