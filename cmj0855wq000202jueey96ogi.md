Key categories encompass inorganic biocides, such as metal-based compounds, and organic biocides, including halogenated and nitrogen-based formulations.</p><p>

</p><p>The specialty biocides market, while innovative, faces notable restraints from increasingly rigorous environmental regulations that prioritize sustainability over traditional chemical use.
