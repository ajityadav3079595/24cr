</p><p>Glycerin triacetate, also known as triacetin, is a triester of glycerol and acetic acid. This colorless, viscous liquid is widely used as a plasticizer, solvent, and humectant in various applications. The compound finds extensive use in food, pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and tobacco industries due to its non-toxic nature and excellent solvent properties. Its ability to enhance flavor and aroma makes it particularly valuable in food and tobacco applications.</p><p>

</p><p>Global glycerol triacetate market is witnessing substantial growth due to its widespread application as a food additive.
