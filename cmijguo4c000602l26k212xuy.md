These gases are critical in processes such as chemical vapor deposition, etching, doping, and chamber cleaning during the fabrication of semiconductors, flat panel displays, and photovoltaic cells. The category includes gases like Nitrogen Trifluoride (NF3), Tungsten Hexafluoride (WF6), Nitrous Oxide (N2O), Ammonia (NH3), and Carbon Monoxide (CO), all requiring ultra-high purity levels, typically 99.999% (5.0 grade) or higher, to prevent contamination of delicate microelectronic components.</p><p>
