<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Expanding Demand in Dental and Healthcare Applications Fuels Market Growth</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The silicone putty market is experiencing robust growth driven by its expanding applications in dental prosthetics and medical device manufacturing. The material's biocompatibility and non-toxic properties make it particularly valuable in healthcare applications where patient safety is paramount.</p><p>

</p><p><strong>Alternative Technologies</strong></p><p>
Competition from 3D printing and digital molding technologies presents a growing challenge, particularly in industrial applications where speed and customization are prioritized. While silicone putty maintains advantages in certain niche applications, technological substitution threatens portions of its traditional market.</p><p>
