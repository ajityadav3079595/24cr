These versatile products vary in thickness, diameter, and tensile strength to accommodate diverse applications ranging from office supplies to industrial packaging. The market offers two primary variants: <strong>standard rubber bands</strong> for general-purpose use and <strong>industrial-grade bands</strong> with enhanced durability for heavy-duty applications.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by steady demand from end-use sectors including packaging, agriculture, and healthcare, where rubber bands serve as cost-effective fastening solutions.
