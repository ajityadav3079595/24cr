</p><p>Trinitrobenzene (TNB) is a high-energy explosive compound derived from benzene, characterized by the presence of three nitro groups. It is primarily used in military applications, mining, and as a reagent in chemical synthesis. The compound is known for its stability and high detonation velocity, making it suitable for controlled explosive applications. The market includes various purity grades, ranging from below 5% to above 90%, catering to different industrial needs.
