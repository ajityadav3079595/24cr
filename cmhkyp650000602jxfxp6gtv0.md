</p><p>Hydrophilic oils are water-soluble formulations designed to enhance lubrication, emulsification, and wetting properties across various industrial applications. These specialty oils contain hydrophilic (water-attracting) molecular groups that enable stable mixtures with aqueous solutions. Key product variants include both ionic and nonionic formulations, with applications spanning agriculture, textile processing, detergents, and horticulture.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand for biodegradable lubricants in agriculture and stringent environmental regulations favoring water-based formulations. Recent innovations in textile finishing processes have particularly accelerated adoption, with major manufacturers like Pulcra Chemicals and Rudolf introducing advanced hydrophilic oil solutions for sustainable fabric treatment.
