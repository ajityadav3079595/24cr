<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Ultra-high-purity hydrogen (UHPH) is a refined form of hydrogen gas with an exceptionally high purity level, typically exceeding 99.9999%. It is an essential component in semiconductor manufacturing, where even the smallest impurities can impact device performance. UHPH is used in processes such as wafer fabrication, chemical vapor deposition (CVD), and epitaxial growth, ensuring the precise manufacturing of semiconductor components.</p><p>
