</p><p>Linear alkylbenzene sulfonic acid is an anionic surfactant widely used in detergent formulations and industrial applications. It is characterized by hydrophobic alkyl chains (typically C10-C14) and a hydrophilic sulfonate group, making it highly effective in reducing surface tension. The compound is produced through sulfonation of linear alkylbenzene, resulting in varying chain-length distributions that influence its performance across different applications.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand for household and industrial cleaning products, particularly in emerging economies.
