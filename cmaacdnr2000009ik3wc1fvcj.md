<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>GRE (Glass Fiber-Reinforced Epoxy) Pipe consists of epoxy resin as a polymer matrix and glass fiber as reinforcement. GRE pipes are primarily used for medium-to-high-pressure oilfield and industrial applications in harsh environments exposed to corrosive or chemically aggressive fluids or gases. They are highly resistant to corrosion caused by saltwater, chemicals, residues, and bacteria.</p><p>
