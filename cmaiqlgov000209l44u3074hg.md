# Inactive Dried Yeast Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Inactive Dried Yeast, also known as nutritional yeast, is a natural source of protein containing essential and non-essential amino acids. It is rich in B Complex vitamins crucial for energy production. Produced through non-alcoholic fermentation of Saccharomyces Cerevisiae yeast on molasses, this product is then dried and milled into a fine powder with a toasted yeast flavor, commonly used to enhance dishes with a nutty, cheese-like taste.</p><p>
