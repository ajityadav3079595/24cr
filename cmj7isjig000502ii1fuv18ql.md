</p><p>Sandwich Composite Core Foam refers to lightweight foam materials used as the core layer in sandwich composite structures. These structures consist of a core material sandwiched between two outer skins, offering an excellent balance of strength, rigidity, and low weight. Core foams are primarily used to enhance structural integrity, energy absorption, and thermal insulation properties in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, marine, construction, and wind energy.</p><p>
