<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Rising Semiconductor Demand Accelerates Polyfluoroalkoxy Plastic Consumption</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The semiconductor industry's exponential growth is driving significant demand for high-performance materials like Polyfluoroalkoxy (PFA) plastics. With the global semiconductor market projected to exceed $1 trillion by 2030, manufacturers increasingly rely on PFA for its exceptional chemical resistance and purity characteristics in chip fabrication.
