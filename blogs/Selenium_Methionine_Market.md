 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Selenium Methionine is a specialized feed additive that combines two essential components: selenium and methionine.  <p><span style="font-size:14px">Selenium Methionine is considered the third-generation trace element additive, designed to provide both the trace element selenium and a balanced amount of methionine. This product is commonly used in the livestock and poultry industries to enhance animal feed formulations and improve the nutritional value of animal diets.</span></p>
