<h1>Slow-release Urea-formaldehyde (UF) Fertilizer Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p><span style="font-size:13px">Slow-release urea-formaldehyde (UF) fertilizer is a specialized type of controlled-release fertilizer designed to release nitrogen gradually into the soil, improving nutrient use efficiency. The fertilizer consists of urea, a widely used nitrogen source, which is chemically modified by formaldehyde to form a resin-like compound. This coating slows down the release of nitrogen, allowing it to be available to plants over a longer period, which reduces the need for frequent fertilization and minimizes nitrogen loss through leaching or volatilization.</span></p>
