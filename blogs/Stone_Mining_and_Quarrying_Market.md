 <p>Stone mining involves multiple activities such as drilling, blasting, transporting, and processing stones into usable forms. For instance, crushed stones are used for concrete aggregate, road construction, and as flux in blast furnaces. Limestone, which is abundant in many regions, is also utilized in a variety of chemical applications.</p>
 
 
 
 <p>The process of quarrying stone generally involves <strong>surface mining</strong>, which means the rock is extracted from open pits or quarries. After extraction, the rock is processed into smaller pieces, such as gravel, sand, or crushed stone, or even large blocks used for architectural purposes (dimension stone). These stones are crucial for several industries, including construction, infrastructure development, and industrial production.</p>
