 <p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>Semiconductor plating chemicals</strong> are specialized chemical solutions used in the semiconductor manufacturing process to prepare surfaces and deposit metal layers through electroplating. These chemicals include electroplating solutions, additives, and corrosion inhibitors, which are critical for achieving precise metal layer deposition with high conductivity and durability.  <p><span style="font-size:14px">The semiconductor plating process involves applying thin layers of metals like copper, gold, nickel, and tin onto semiconductor substrates to enhance electrical conductivity and component reliability.
