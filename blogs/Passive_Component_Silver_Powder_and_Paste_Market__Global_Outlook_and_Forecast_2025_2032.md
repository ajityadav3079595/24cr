Silver paste, on the other hand, is a mixture of silver powder, resin or glass oxide, and solvents, which is applied in various electronic processes for enhanced conductivity and performance.</span></p>

The ongoing advancements in the electronics sector have fueled the demand for high-quality silver powder and paste, which are key materials for the production of these components.</span></p>
