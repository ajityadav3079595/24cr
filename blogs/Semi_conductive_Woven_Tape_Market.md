 <p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>Overview</strong></span></p>
 
 
 
 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Semi-conductive woven tape is an essential component in the cable industry, primarily used to manage the electrical field around the cable conductor. This tape is made from materials with conductivity levels between those of a full conductor and an insulator. The primary function of semi-conductive woven tape is to ensure that electrical fields within cables are managed efficiently, preventing issues like corona discharge, which can damage cables and reduce their lifespan.</span></p>
