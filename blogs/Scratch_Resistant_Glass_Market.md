<h1>Scratch-Resistant Glass Market Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p><strong>Scratch-Resistant Glass</strong> is a specialized type of glass designed to resist minor scratches and surface abrasions, ensuring durability and aesthetic appeal. This glass is primarily used in applications where clarity and long-term surface integrity are critical, such as in watch lenses, eyeglasses, smartphones, tablets, and automotive displays. The scratch-resistant properties stem from its composition, which often includes sapphire glass or chemically-treated mineral glass, both of which enhance its hardness and resistance to scratches caused by daily wear and tear.</p>
