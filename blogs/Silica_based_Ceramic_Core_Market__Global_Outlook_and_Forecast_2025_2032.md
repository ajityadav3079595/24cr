<h1>Silica-based Ceramic Core Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p><span style="font-size:14px">Silica-based ceramic cores are specialized materials used extensively in investment casting for the production of complex components, particularly in the aerospace, automotive, and industrial sectors. These cores are primarily composed of high-purity silica and other additives, offering high mechanical strength, excellent heat resistance, low thermal expansion, and high porosity. Their primary function is to create intricate internal cavities within metal castings, ensuring precision and durability in final products.</span></p>
