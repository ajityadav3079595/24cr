 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Self-compacting concrete (SCC) is a specialized concrete mixture that has been designed for optimal workability, flowability, and durability without the need for external vibration. Unlike traditional concrete, SCC has a low yield stress, high deformability, and excellent resistance to segregation. It is particularly advantageous in construction projects where complex forms, intricate reinforcement, or limited access hinder the use of vibrators for compaction. SCC&#39;s ability to flow easily under its own weight ensures that it fills gaps and corners of formwork, even in densely reinforced areas, reducing the risks of voids or air pockets in the concrete structure.</span></p>
