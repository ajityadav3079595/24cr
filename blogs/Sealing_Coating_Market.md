<h1>Sealing Coating Market Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Sealing coatings are specialized protective layers applied to surfaces to prevent rain, groundwater, and other external elements from causing leaks or damage. These coatings are vital in enhancing durability and waterproofing various structures. Commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings, sealing coatings are integral to building roofing, basements, underground garages, kitchens, bathrooms, balconies, and facades. They are also widely applied in swimming pools, water towers, steel structures, and cooling tower walls, making them a versatile solution for modern infrastructure.</p>
 
 
 
 <p><strong>In 2023, the global sealing coating market was valued at USD 13,290 million, with projections indicating growth to USD 17,023.28 million by 2030, at a steady compound annual growth rate (<strong>CAGR</strong>) of 3.60%.</strong> This growth is fueled by increasing urbanization, rising construction activities, and heightened awareness of waterproofing solutions.</p>
 
 
 
 <p>North America accounted for a significant portion of this market, valued at USD 3,462.99 million in 2023, with a <strong>CAGR</strong> of 3.09% expected through 2030. Technological advancements in material science and the adoption of sustainable coatings contribute to the expanding demand in this region.</p>
