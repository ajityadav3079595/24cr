 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Semiconductor electroplating chemicals refer to a group of chemical compounds used in the electroplating process to deposit metal layers on semiconductor substrates. These metal layers are essential for creating interconnects and metallization patterns in semiconductor devices, which are vital for the functioning of modern electronics, such as computers, smartphones, and other consumer gadgets. Electroplating is crucial in semiconductor fabrication as it ensures the formation of high-quality and highly reliable conductive metal layers on silicon wafers or other semiconductor substrates.</span></p>
