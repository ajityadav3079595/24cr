</p><p>Phosphoric acid solution is an inorganic chemical compound that appears as a clear, colorless liquid or transparent crystalline solid. This versatile chemical serves as a key raw material across multiple industries because of its unique properties as a chelating agent, pH adjuster, and source of phosphates. The solution is primarily produced through two processes: the wet process (using sulfuric acid) and the thermal process (burning phosphorus).</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is being driven by increasing demand from food &amp; beverage applications, particularly in carbonated drinks as an acidulant.
