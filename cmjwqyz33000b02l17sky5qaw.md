The region benefits from high standards of animal welfare, which drive the demand for high-quality, comfortable, and absorbent bedding materials to ensure the health and comfort of horses. Strict environmental regulations and a focus on sustainable, biodegradable materials also influence product innovation and consumer preferences. Mature distribution networks and the presence of key industry players further solidify Europe's central position in the global market, fostering consistent demand and advanced product offerings.

</p><p><strong>Middle East &amp; Africa</strong></p><p>

The market is driven by high standards for premium horse care in these segments, with a demand for imported, high-quality bedding products. In other parts of the region, the market is less developed, with limited adoption of specialized bedding due to economic constraints and different traditional horse-keeping practices.
