# Plant Growth Hormone Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Plant growth hormones, also known as plant growth regulators (PGRs), are chemicals that influence the growth processes of plants. These hormones are naturally occurring compounds within plant cells and tissues. Their primary role is to regulate various aspects of plant development, such as cell division, elongation, and differentiation, ultimately affecting growth patterns and environmental responses.</p><p>
</p><p>In simple terms, plant growth hormones control a wide range of functions such as germination, flowering, root growth, and fruiting. There are five main classes of plant hormones: auxins, cytokinins, gibberellins, abscisic acid, and ethylene.
