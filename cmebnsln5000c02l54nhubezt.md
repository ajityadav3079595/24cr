</p><p>Polyester Partially Oriented Yarn (POY) is a type of multi-filament yarn that is only partially stretched during its production process. It is derived from spinning polyester melt or chips and is primarily used as an intermediate product for creating textured yarn. Polyester POY is versatile, serving multiple applications, including the manufacturing of textured yarn for garments, industrial fabrics, and home textiles. Additionally, it can be utilized in processes like draw warping for weaving and warp knitting, further expanding its potential applications in various industries.</p><p>
</p><p>POY's key feature is that it is only partially oriented and not fully stretched, making it different from fully oriented yarns like Polyester Fully Oriented Yarn (FOY). This unique characteristic offers the material certain advantages such as flexibility in processing and easier handling during subsequent textile operations.</p><p>
