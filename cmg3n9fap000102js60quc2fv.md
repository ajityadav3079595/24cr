# Hydrogels for Tissue Engineering Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Hydrogels are a unique group of biocompatible, three-dimensional (3D) polymeric substances designed to mimic the properties of natural tissues. These materials act as scaffolds, supporting cell growth and gradually degrading to leave behind healthy tissue. Hydrogels are widely used in tissue engineering due to their ability to maintain high water content, promote cellular interactions, and provide structural integrity for regenerative medicine applications.</p><p>
