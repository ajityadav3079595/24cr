<strong>Regional Analysis: Tamper Evident Labels Market</strong></p><p>

While price sensitivity keeps barcode labels dominant, rising middle-class expectations are pushing premiumization. The food export sector shows promising adoption rates as producers seek compliance with international safety standards, particularly for shipments to North American and European markets.</p><p>
