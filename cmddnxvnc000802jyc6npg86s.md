</p><p>Polyacrylamide paper dry strength agents are synthetic polymers specifically designed to improve the mechanical properties of paper and paperboard products. These additives work by forming hydrogen bonds with cellulose fibers, increasing the paper's tensile strength, burst strength, and surface strength without significantly affecting its bulk or porosity. The product is available in two primary grades: industrial grade for standard paper production and technical grade for specialized applications requiring higher performance characteristics.</p><p>
