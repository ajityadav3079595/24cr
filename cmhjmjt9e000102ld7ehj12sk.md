# High-purity Solid Potassium Hydroxide Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>High-purity solid potassium hydroxide, commonly known as caustic potash, is a white, deliquescent solid with the chemical formula KOH. It is highly soluble in water and alcohol but insoluble in ether. Due to its strong alkaline properties, it is extensively used in various industrial applications, including the production of soft and liquid soaps, potassium salts, electroplating, and textile processing. Its high purity levels make it particularly suitable for sensitive applications in the semiconductor and photovoltaic industries.</p><p>
