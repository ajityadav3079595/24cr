These specialty materials are engineered for demanding applications, particularly in automotive sealing systems and industrial machinery components where durability under extreme conditions is critical. The material's performance characteristics make it indispensable for gaskets, hoses, and vibration dampers exposed to high temperatures and harsh chemical environments.</p><p>
