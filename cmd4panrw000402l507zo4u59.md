# Sodium Humate Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>
</p><p>Sodium humate is a type of macromolecular organic weak sodium salt, composed of humic substances derived primarily from weathered coal, peat, and lignite. This versatile compound is produced through specialized processes to enhance its functionality for various applications. Due to its complex structure, sodium humate serves a broad range of uses, particularly in agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and as feed additives. It plays a pivotal role in soil conditioning and improving plant growth due to its water retention properties and its ability to provide essential nutrients.</p><p>
