<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Heat Resisting Alloys, also known as high-temperature alloys, are utilized in high-temperature environments due to their stable structure and excellent mechanical, physical, and chemical properties. These alloys include heat resistant steel, heat resistant aluminum alloy, heat resistant titanium alloy, high-temperature alloy, and refractory alloy. They exhibit specific tensile, creep, fatigue properties, as well as physical, chemical, and technological properties under high-temperature conditions.</p><p>
