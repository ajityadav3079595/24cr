</p><p>Isooctyl nitrate, commonly used as a diesel fuel additive, plays a critical role in improving the ignition quality of diesel by increasing its cetane number. It is an organic nitrate compound primarily used in secondary processing diesel fuel, where it serves as a cetane improver. Isooctyl nitrate facilitates a more complete and rapid combustion process, which in turn enhances fuel efficiency, reduces emissions, and contributes to engine longevity. Beyond its function as a diesel additive, isooctyl nitrate also serves as an effective organic solvent in various industrial applications due to its chemical stability and compatibility with hydrocarbons.</p><p>
