</p><p>N-Butyl Bromide, also known as 1-Bromobutane, is an organic compound primarily used as an alkylating agent in pharmaceutical synthesis, a solvent in industrial applications, and an intermediate in agrochemical production. It is a colorless to pale yellow liquid with a characteristic odor, widely utilized due to its reactivity and solubility properties. The compound plays a critical role in the synthesis of active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) and specialty chemicals. Its industrial-grade variant is employed in manufacturing processes requiring high purity, while pharmaceutical-grade N-Butyl Bromide adheres to stringent quality standards for medical applications. The versatility of N-Butyl Bromide makes it indispensable across multiple industries, driving its consistent demand globally.</p><p>
