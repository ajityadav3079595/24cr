</p><p>Molybdenum Copper (Mo-Cu) alloys are high-performance pseudo-alloys combining the thermal conductivity of copper with the low thermal expansion properties of molybdenum. These materials exhibit exceptional heat dissipation capabilities, making them ideal for applications requiring precise thermal management. The alloy composition typically ranges from 15-30% copper with the remainder being molybdenum, though specialized formulations exist.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from electronics and aerospace sectors, where thermal management solutions are critical.
