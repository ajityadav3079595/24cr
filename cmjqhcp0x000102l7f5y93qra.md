</p><p>Styrene Butadiene Latex (SBL) is a synthetic rubber latex produced through emulsion polymerization of styrene and butadiene monomers. The material combines the rigidity of styrene with the elasticity of butadiene, resulting in a versatile emulsion polymer with excellent binding properties, water resistance, and flexibility. These characteristics make it indispensable in applications ranging from paper coating to construction adhesives.</p><p>
