<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Product Costs and Limited Farmer Awareness Constrain Market Penetration</strong></p><p>

Manufacturers must invest in R&amp;D to develop resilient microbial strains capable of performing across wider temperature and moisture ranges to maintain product reliability.</p><p>
