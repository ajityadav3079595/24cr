# Global Self Cooling Packaging Market Research Report 2025-2032

<p>The global self-cooling packaging market was valued at <strong>US$ 68 million in 2024</strong> and is projected to reach <strong>US$ 134 million by 2032</strong>, exhibiting a <strong>Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 8.8%</strong> during the forecast period (2025-2032). This growth is driven by increasing demand for temperature-sensitive product packaging across various industries. The market has shown consistent growth since 2018, with metal packaging currently holding the largest market share, followed by plastic packaging solutions.</p><p>
</p><p>Self-cooling packaging refers to specialized containers that can maintain or lower the temperature of their contents without external cooling sources. These innovative packaging solutions typically use endothermic reactions, phase change materials, or evaporative cooling technologies. Key products include insulated containers, cooling pouches, and temperature-controlled boxes. The technology is particularly valuable for perishable goods, pharmaceuticals, and ready-to-eat meals that require specific temperature ranges during storage and transportation.
