Increased research and development efforts, including partnerships for precision agriculture tools, are further innovating product delivery methods, such as granular gypsum for targeted soil application. These expansions not only diversify revenue streams for manufacturers but also address pressing global challenges like food security and water scarcity. However, effective education on application techniques remains essential to maximize benefits and minimize overuse.
