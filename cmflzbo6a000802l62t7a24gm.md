</p><p>High-performance Melt Blown Nonwoven Fabrics are a category of synthetic fabrics produced by extruding thermoplastic polymers through small nozzles with high-speed blowing gas. The resulting fibers form a web-like structure with excellent filtration capabilities. These fabrics are recognized for their unique properties such as softness, elasticity, chemical resistance, and wettability. The fine fiber diameter (typically 1-5 microns) enhances their ability to trap airborne particles, making them ideal for use in medical masks, air filters, oil absorbents, and insulation products.</p><p>
