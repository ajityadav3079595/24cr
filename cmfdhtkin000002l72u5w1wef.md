# Sodium Hexa Meta Phosphate Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>
</p><p>Sodium hexametaphosphate (SHMP) is a chemical compound with the formula (NaPO3)6, which consists of a polymeric form of metaphosphate. This compound, commonly known as sodium polymetaphosphate, is primarily used in various industries as a dispersing agent, water treatment chemical, and food additive. Sodium hexametaphosphate is found in both industrial-grade and food-grade formulations. Its applications range from water treatment, detergent manufacturing, and ceramics, to the food industry, where it serves as an emulsifier, stabilizer, and sequestrant.</p><p>
