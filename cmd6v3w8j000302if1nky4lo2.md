</p><p>Delta Lactones are cyclic ester compounds characterized by a five-membered ring structure containing carbonyl (C=O) and ether oxygen (O) atoms. These compounds are prized in the flavor and fragrance industry for their distinctive fruity, creamy, and nutty aroma profiles. Major product variants include Dodecalactone delta, Nonalactone delta, and Decalactone delta, which find extensive application in food flavorings and cosmetic formulations.</p><p>
