# GMP Grade Sodium Chloride Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

</p><p>The GMP Grade Sodium Chloride market refers to the global market for high-quality sodium chloride (NaCl), also known as table salt or common salt, that is specifically manufactured and processed under Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) conditions. GMP is a set of guidelines that ensures the product is consistently produced and controlled according to quality standards, ensuring safety and efficacy in pharmaceutical and medical applications.</p><p>
