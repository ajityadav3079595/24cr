</p><p>Hydrogen dimethicone is a modified silicone polymer widely used in personal care and cosmetic formulations. This specialty ingredient acts as an emollient, providing superior spreadability and silky texture while reducing greasiness in skincare and haircare products. Its unique molecular structure combines the benefits of dimethicone with active hydrogen atoms, enhancing compatibility with various formulations. The compound is categorized by purity levels, primarily 98%-99% and 99% grades, with applications spanning moisturizers, sunscreens, foundations, and hair conditioners.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing consumer demand for premium cosmetic products with enhanced sensory profiles and performance benefits.
