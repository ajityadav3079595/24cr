</p><p>Firestop Bags are passive fire protection solutions designed to seal openings around pipes, cables, and other penetrations in fire-rated walls and floors. These specialized bags feature an outer layer of tightly woven, fire-resistant glass fiber cloth and are internally filled with thermal insulation and expansion materials. When exposed to heat, the materials expand to create an effective barrier against fire, smoke, and toxic gas spread, maintaining structural compartmentalization.</p><p>
