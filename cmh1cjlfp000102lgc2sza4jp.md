</p><p style="text-align:justify">The increasing focus on water conservation and environmental sustainability in fire safety is one of the major factors propelling the market for water mist fire extinguishing systems. This factor is anticipated to greatly propel the market's expansion in the upcoming years due to the expanding laws and guidelines centred on sustainable fire safety.</p><p>
