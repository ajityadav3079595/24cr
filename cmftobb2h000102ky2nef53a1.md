As a leading material in 3D printing, PLA filament is prized for its ease of use, low printing temperature, and minimal warping characteristics. The material's eco-friendly properties and versatility across industries including packaging, healthcare, and automotive have driven its adoption worldwide.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily fueled by increasing environmental awareness and stringent regulations promoting sustainable materials. Regulatory approvals for PLA-based medical devices have increased by 35% year-over-year as the material demonstrates excellent tissue compatibility and predictable resorption rates.</p><p>
