</p><p><strong>South America</strong></p><p>

Local production is nascent, relying on Chinese imports for 70% of supply. Despite these hurdles, the region shows promise with increasing Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) in feed mills and growing awareness of inositol’s benefits in shrimp farming across Ecuador and Chile. Regulatory frameworks remain underdeveloped compared to North America or Europe, creating both opportunities and quality control risks.</p><p>
