# Reclaimed Asphalt Pavement (RAP) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Reclaimed Asphalt Pavement (RAP) refers to the process of removing, reprocessing, and recycling asphalt materials that contain asphalt binder and aggregates. The materials are extracted from old pavements, resized, and reintroduced into new asphalt mixtures. This method significantly reduces the need for virgin materials, making it a cost-effective and environmentally friendly solution for road construction and maintenance.</p><p>
</p><p>Recycled asphalt involves the process of milling and repurposing asphalt layers, which are then stabilized and reused in various construction applications.
