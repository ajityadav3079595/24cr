<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>

</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Growth in Electronics Industry Fueling Market Expansion</strong></p><p>

This regulatory pressure is forcing manufacturers to invest in research and development of biodegradable and recyclable foam pouch alternatives.</p><p>

</p><p>The foam pouch industry faces significant challenges from fluctuating raw material prices, particularly for polyethylene and other polymer materials. </p><p>Foam pouches face increasing competition from alternative packaging solutions such as molded pulp, air pillows, and corrugated packaging.
