</p><p>TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) Films are high-performance substrate materials extruded from polyester, polyether, or polycaprolactone. These films exhibit a unique combination of properties including:</p><p>
</p><p>High elasticity</p><p>Transparency</p><p>Excellent low-temperature flexibility</p><p>Resistance to oil, grease, chemicals, and abrasion</p><p>
</p><p>Due to these characteristics, TPU films are widely utilized in diverse applications across automotive, construction, aerospace &amp; defense, medical &amp; healthcare, and textile sectors.</p><p>
