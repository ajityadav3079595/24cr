</p><p>Structural epoxy adhesives are high-performance bonding materials composed of epoxy resins and curing agents that provide superior strength and durability. These adhesives form permanent bonds that can withstand significant mechanical stress, thermal cycling, and environmental exposure. Commonly used in demanding applications, they exhibit excellent chemical resistance, adhesion to diverse substrates, and dimensional stability. The product types include one-component and two-component formulations, catering to different curing mechanisms and application requirements.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from key end-use industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction, where lightweight materials and strong bonding solutions are critical.
