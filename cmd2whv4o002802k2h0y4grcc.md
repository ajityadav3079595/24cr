<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>

</p><p>The Southeast Asia and U.S. decylene markets are experiencing robust growth due to increasing applications in detergent alcohol production. The U.S. synthetic lubricant market has grown at approximately 8% CAGR from 2020-2023, with major lubricant manufacturers increasingly shifting toward high-performance PAO-based formulations. </p><p>Furthermore, ongoing investments in petrochemical infrastructure across Southeast Asia, particularly in Thailand and Malaysia, are expanding regional capacity to produce decylene and its derivatives.
