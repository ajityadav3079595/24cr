# Industrial Fatty Alcohols Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Fatty alcohols are alcohols with hydroxyl groups connected to aliphatic hydrocarbon groups, commonly used in the production of synthetic detergents, cosmetics, and medicines. These alcohols also serve as additives in lubricating oils and antistatic agents in textiles. The industrial production of fatty alcohols is primarily through oxo synthesis or oil hydrogenolysis.</p><p>
