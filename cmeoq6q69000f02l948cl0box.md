</p><p>Potassium L(+)-Lactate is a chemical compound that serves as the potassium salt of L(+)-lactic acid, consisting of potassium cations and L(+)-lactate anions. This naturally occurring compound plays a crucial role in human metabolism and finds extensive applications across food &amp; beverage, pharmaceutical, and personal care industries. Its multifunctional properties include moisture regulation, pH balancing, and antimicrobial effects.</p><p>
