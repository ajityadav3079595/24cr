</p><p>Ultrafiltration (UF) membrane filtration is a pressure-driven separation process that uses semipermeable membranes to retain suspended solids and high-molecular-weight solutes while allowing water and low-molecular-weight solutes to pass through. This technology plays a critical role in water treatment, industrial processes, and biopharmaceutical applications due to its efficiency in removing contaminants while operating at lower pressures than reverse osmosis.</p><p>
