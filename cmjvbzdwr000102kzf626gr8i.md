</p><p>Color Steel Sandwich Panels are composite materials consisting of two steel sheets bonded to an insulating core, such as foam, rock wool, or polyurethane. These panels are widely used in construction for their <strong>durability, thermal insulation, and aesthetic appeal</strong>. They are commonly applied in industrial buildings, commercial structures, and residential projects. The steel sheets are coated with color finishes to enhance corrosion resistance and visual appeal.
