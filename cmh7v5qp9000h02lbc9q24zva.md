</p><p>Ovenable shrink bags are specialized packaging solutions designed to withstand high temperatures during cooking or reheating processes while providing superior barrier properties. These bags are commonly made from multilayer films, often incorporating materials like nylon, polyester, or ethylene-vinyl alcohol (EVOH), which ensure heat resistance and prevent moisture or oxygen permeation. Their primary applications include packaging pre-marinated foods, pre-processed meals, and ready-to-cook products.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand for convenience foods, particularly in North America and Europe, where busy lifestyles are accelerating the adoption of pre-packaged meal solutions.
