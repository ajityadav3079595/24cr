</p><p><strong>SPHERICAL ALUMINA POWDER</strong> is a type of high-purity aluminum oxide (Al2O3) that is engineered into uniform spherical particles. Unlike irregularly shaped alumina particles, spherical alumina offers superior thermal conductivity, better flowability, and enhanced packing density, making it an ideal material for a variety of high-performance applications. It is primarily used in thermal interface materials (TIMs), semiconductor packaging, LED lighting, adhesives, and high-performance ceramics. The spherical shape minimizes voids in materials, enhancing the overall mechanical and thermal performance of composite systems.</p><p>
</p><p>The powder is produced through a variety of advanced methods including flame fusion, precipitation, and thermal spray processes, which ensure tight control over particle size distribution and purity. These characteristics make spherical alumina essential in industries requiring efficient heat dissipation and high electrical insulation.</p><p>
