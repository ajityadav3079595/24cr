</p><p>Pitch-based microsphere activated carbon is a specialized form of activated carbon derived from pitch, a byproduct of petroleum or coal tar. It is characterized by its spherical shape, high surface area, and excellent adsorption properties. This material is widely used in applications requiring high purity and performance, such as medical treatments, water purification, and air filtration.
