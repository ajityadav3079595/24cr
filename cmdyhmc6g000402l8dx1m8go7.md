</p><p>Warm Mix Asphalt (WMA) Additives are specialized materials incorporated into asphalt mixtures to <strong>lower the production and compaction temperatures</strong> by 20–30°C compared to traditional hot mix asphalt. These additives not only enhance workability and reduce emissions but also <strong>improve pavement performance</strong>, particularly in:</p><p>
</p><p>Low-temperature construction environments</p><p>High-altitude regions</p><p>Tunnels</p><p>Urban and municipal roads</p><p>
</p><p>The market is primarily categorized into:</p><p>
</p><p><strong>Wax-Based Additives</strong></p><p><strong>Chemical-Based Additives</strong> — the dominant type, due to superior performance at reduced temperatures</p><p>
