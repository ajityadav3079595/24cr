</p><p>Special Mortar refers to advanced construction materials formulated with unique binders, aggregates, and additives designed to meet specific functional requirements such as waterproofing, heat resistance, chemical resistance, and enhanced adhesion. These mortars differ from traditional cement mixes due to their tailored compositions, which allow them to serve specialized roles in construction, including tile fixing, external wall insulation, decorative finishes, and repair works. Typically available in dry and wet forms, Special Mortars are essential in projects demanding high performance and longevity under diverse environmental conditions.</p><p>
