</p><p>Ventilated exterior wall systems are advanced building envelope solutions featuring an outer cladding layer separated from the inner wall by an air cavity. These systems provide superior thermal performance through natural convection in the cavity, effectively managing moisture while enhancing energy efficiency. The technology includes components like support structures, insulation layers, and various cladding materials such as fiber cement, terracotta, or metal panels.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by Germany's stringent energy efficiency regulations and the rising demand for sustainable construction solutions.
