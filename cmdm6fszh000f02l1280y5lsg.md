# Pigments for Poultry Feed Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Pigments for poultry feed are essential additives used to enhance the coloration of poultry products such as egg yolks, broiler skin, and shanks. These pigments are derived from both natural and synthetic sources and are primarily carotenoids such as beta-carotene, canthaxanthin, lutein, and zeaxanthin. The pigmentation in poultry feed is a crucial quality determinant as consumers associate vibrant egg yolks and golden skin in poultry with superior quality and nutritional value.</p><p>

<strong>Challenges:</strong></p><p>
