</p><p>Exposed aggregate surface retarders are chemical formulations used in concrete construction to create decorative finishes by selectively retarding surface hydration. These products delay the curing of surface mortar while allowing underlying concrete to set normally, enabling aggregate exposure through subsequent washing or brushing. The technology is widely adopted in architectural concrete applications where aesthetic appeal complements structural functionality.</p><p>
