</p><p>Concrete pipe gaskets are essential sealing components used in underground piping systems for water management, drainage, and sewage applications. These elastomeric gaskets create watertight seals between concrete pipe segments, preventing leaks while allowing for thermal expansion and joint movement. Major product types include 4G and 4F profile gaskets, which differ in their compression characteristics and sealing mechanisms.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing infrastructure development projects worldwide, particularly in emerging economies. Government investments in water and wastewater management systems, coupled with stricter environmental regulations on pipeline leakage, are accelerating demand. However, volatility in raw material prices for synthetic rubber compounds presents a market challenge.
