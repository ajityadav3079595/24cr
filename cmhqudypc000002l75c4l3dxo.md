</p><p>Rotary screw thickeners are wastewater treatment equipment designed to efficiently concentrate sludge through mechanical dewatering. These systems utilize an inclined wedge-wire screen and a slowly rotating screw mechanism to separate liquids from solids, significantly reducing sludge volume while optimizing water recovery. Their robust design makes them particularly effective for municipal sewage plants, industrial wastewater treatment, and specialized applications in paper, chemical, and pharmaceutical industries.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by stringent environmental regulations worldwide, particularly in North America and Europe, where wastewater treatment standards continue to tighten.
