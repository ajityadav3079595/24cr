</p><p>The <strong>Al2O3 Coated Separator</strong> is a critical component in lithium-ion batteries, designed to enhance thermal stability and prevent short circuits. It consists of a polyolefin-based separator coated with aluminum oxide (Al2O3) particles, which improve mechanical strength, thermal resistance, and electrolyte wettability. These separators are widely used in applications requiring high energy density and safety, such as electric vehicles, portable electronics, and grid storage systems.
