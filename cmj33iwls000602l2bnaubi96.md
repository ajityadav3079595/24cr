</p><p>The global push toward lightweight materials in transportation industries is accelerating adoption of polyphenylene sulfide (PPS) composites. <strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>

These economics make justification difficult in price-driven markets, particularly in developing regions where initial cost often outweighs lifecycle benefits. The specialized equipment required for processing PPS composites further compounds capital expenditure concerns for potential adopters.</p><p>
