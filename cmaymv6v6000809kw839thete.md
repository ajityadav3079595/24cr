Definition</p><p>
</p><p>The <strong>global aluminium profile and fittings market</strong> encompasses the production, distribution, and application of aluminium-based structural components and connectors used across multiple industries. Aluminium profiles are extruded shapes designed for specific applications, offering high strength-to-weight ratios, corrosion resistance, and versatility in design. Fittings include connectors, brackets, and fasteners that complement these profiles in assembly. The market serves industries such as construction (window frames, curtain walls), transportation (automotive frames, rail components), and industrial machinery (structural frameworks). The increasing adoption of aluminium in place of traditional materials like steel and wood, due to its sustainability and recyclability, further defines this market's significance in modern manufacturing and construction.</p><p>
