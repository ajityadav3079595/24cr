These materials find applications across electronics, energy storage, healthcare, and industrial catalysis. In China, the market is dominated by nano-oxides, quantum dots, and carbon-based nanomaterials, which accounted for over 65% of total production volume in 2024. The semiconductor industry alone consumed 38% of China's nanochemical output last year, while energy storage applications showed the fastest growth at 17% year-over-year.</p><p>
