</p><p>High Molecular Weight Polyester Resin is a polymer formed from the reaction of polybasic acids and polyols, characterized by a molecular weight of above 10,000. These resins offer excellent adhesion to a variety of substrates, including metals, plastics, and textiles. They are widely used in industries like paints and coatings, adhesives, sealants, and industrial inks due to their superior chemical resistance, flexibility, and durability. These resins are typically available in various formulations, including water-based, solvent-based, and hotmelt types, catering to different industrial needs.</p><p>
