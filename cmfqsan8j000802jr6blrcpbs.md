</p><p>Boron Nitride (BN) coatings are advanced ceramic materials known for their high thermal conductivity, electrical insulation, and lubricating properties. These coatings are widely used in industries requiring high-performance thermal management, corrosion resistance, and wear protection. The two primary types of BN coatings are <strong>water-borne coatings</strong> and <strong>solvent-based coatings</strong>, each offering distinct advantages depending on the application.
