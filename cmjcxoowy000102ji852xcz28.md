</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Production Costs and Complex Manufacturing to Limit Market Expansion</strong></p><p>
</p><p>While aluminum nitride offers superior properties, its widespread adoption faces challenges from high production costs and manufacturing complexities. The carbothermal reduction process, which accounts for <strong>over 70%</strong> of commercial AlN production, requires precise temperature control above 1600Â°C and carefully controlled atmospheres. These energy-intensive processes result in production costs that are significantly higher than competing materials like aluminum oxide.</p><p>
</p><p>The powder synthesis stage is particularly challenging, as impurities dramatically affect the final product's thermal and electrical properties. These cost factors make aluminum nitride prohibitive for price-sensitive applications despite its performance advantages.</p><p>
