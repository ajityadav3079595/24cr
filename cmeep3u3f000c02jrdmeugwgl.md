</p><p>Rosolic acid, also known as aurin, is an organic compound primarily used as a pH indicator and dye intermediate. It is a triphenylmethane dye that exhibits a color change from yellow to red in acidic to neutral pH ranges. Rosolic acid is widely utilized in chemical laboratories for pH determination and in the textile industry for dye production. Its versatility and stability make it a valuable component in various industrial applications.
