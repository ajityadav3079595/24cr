</p><p>Firestop putty sticks are specialized passive fire protection materials designed to seal openings around cables, pipes, and other penetrations in fire-rated walls and floors. These putty sticks expand when exposed to high temperatures, forming an insulating char that prevents the spread of flames and smoke. The two primary types are intumescent (which swells under heat) and cementitious (which hardens into a fire-resistant barrier).</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by stringent fire safety regulations in commercial and residential construction, particularly in North America and Europe where building codes mandate passive fire protection systems. Emerging economies in Asia-Pacific are witnessing accelerated adoption due to rapid urbanization and infrastructure development. Key players like 3M, Hilti, and Sika AG continue to innovate with halogen-free formulations and improved application methods, further propelling market expansion.</p><p>
