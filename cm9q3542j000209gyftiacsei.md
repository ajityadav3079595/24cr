As a type of provitamin A, Beta Cryptoxanthin is primarily recognized for its potential health benefits, including its antioxidant properties, its ability to support healthy vision, and its role in reducing oxidative stress. It is predominantly found in foods like oranges, tangerines, and papayas.</p><p>
</p><p>This antioxidant also plays a critical role in the body by neutralizing harmful free radicals, contributing to overall cellular health and well-being. Moreover, Beta Cryptoxanthin is used in dietary supplements, functional foods, and cosmetics, thanks to its reputation for improving skin and eye health.</p><p>
