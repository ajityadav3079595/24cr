</p><p>Dye transfer inhibitors are specialized chemical agents that prevent color bleeding during washing processes by absorbing or inhibiting the re-deposition of loose dyes. These substances play a crucial role in maintaining fabric color integrity, particularly in mixed-load laundry and industrial textile processing. The product category includes PVP polymers, chromabond polymers, and other advanced formulations.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from textile manufacturing and household care sectors, coupled with rising consumer expectations for color-fast fabrics. Furthermore, stringent environmental regulations are pushing manufacturers to develop eco-friendly formulations, creating new growth opportunities. Europe currently dominates the market with approximately 50% share, followed by North America and Asia-Pacific. In product segments, PVP polymers hold over 60% market share due to their superior performance characteristics.</p><p>
