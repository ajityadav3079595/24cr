</p><p>1,3-Di-o-tolylguanidine (CAS 97-39-2) is a white crystalline powder primarily used as an accelerator in rubber vulcanization processes. This specialty chemical compound acts as a key activator for thiazole and sulfenamide-type accelerators, significantly improving crosslinking efficiency in both natural and synthetic rubber formulations. Its non-toxic properties and effectiveness in thick rubber products like tires, industrial belts, and rubber rollers make it indispensable in the polymer industry.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by expanding automotive tire production, which accounts for over 65% of global rubber consumption. However, environmental regulations concerning chemical additives present challenges.
