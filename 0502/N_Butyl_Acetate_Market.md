This versatile compound is widely used in the production of lacquers, synthetic resins, and natural gums, as well as in applications within the chemical, pharmaceutical, and fragrance industries.</span></p>
 
 
 
 <p><span style="font-size:16px">N-Butyl Acetate has significant applications as a solvent due to its ability to dissolve a wide range of materials.
