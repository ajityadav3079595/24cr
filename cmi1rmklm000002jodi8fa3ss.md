</p><p>While demand grows, the ballistic protection fabric market faces significant production challenges. </p><p><strong>Performance Limitations in Extreme Conditions</strong></p><p>

Ongoing research aims to address these limitations, but current solutions increase production costs by approximately 15-20%.</p><p>
