</p><p>Phloroglucinol API (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient) is a phenolic compound widely used as an antispasmodic agent in pharmaceutical formulations. This white crystalline powder exhibits smooth muscle relaxant properties, making it effective for treating gastrointestinal and urinary tract spasms. The compound exists in two purity grades (0.98 and 0.99) with pharmaceutical applications requiring higher purity levels.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing gastrointestinal disorder prevalence and rising demand for effective antispasmodic drugs. Asia-Pacific dominates production with key manufacturers like Shouguang Fukang Pharmaceutical and Sichuan Renan Pharmaceutical, while North America leads in consumption due to advanced healthcare infrastructure.
