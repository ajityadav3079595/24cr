</p><p>Glued laminated timber (glulam) and cross laminated timber (CLT) are engineered wood products composed of layers of dimension lumber bonded with adhesives. <strong>Glulam</strong> features layers oriented parallel to the length, offering superior strength for beams and columns, while <strong>CLT</strong> consists of cross-oriented layers, creating large rigid panels ideal for walls and floors. Both materials provide sustainable construction solutions with high structural performance and design flexibility.</p><p>
