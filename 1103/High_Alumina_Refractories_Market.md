 <p><span style="font-size:16px"><strong>Iron & Steel Industry:</strong></span></p>

 <p><span style="font-size:16px">The cement and lime industry is another significant consumer of high alumina refractories.  <p><span style="font-size:16px">The nonferrous metals industry, including aluminum, copper, and lead production, relies on high alumina refractories for its smelting and refining processes.
