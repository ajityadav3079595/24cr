Its unique microstructure combines flake-like graphite particles with rounded edges, delivering <strong>45% higher tensile strength</strong> than conventional gray iron while maintaining excellent thermal conductivity. This makes CGI particularly valuable for high-stress applications in automotive powertrains, including cylinder blocks, heads, and brake components.</p><p>
