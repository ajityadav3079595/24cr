# United States Hyrdocarbon Resin Market Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Hydrocarbon resins are synthetic resins derived primarily from petroleum sources. These resins are used in a variety of applications due to their adhesive properties, stability, and versatility. Hydrocarbon resins play a key role in the production of adhesives, coatings, inks, rubber, and a range of other industrial products. The most common types of hydrocarbon resins are C5 aliphatic resins and C9 aromatic resins, with each offering distinct characteristics that cater to different industrial applications. These resins are essential in industries like automotive, packaging, construction, and consumer goods, where they are used for their strong bonding capabilities, improved product performance, and durability.</p><p>
