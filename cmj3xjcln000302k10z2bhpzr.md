</p><p>High Tibial Osteotomy (HTO) plates are specialized medical implants used in knee realignment surgeries to correct deformities and redistribute weight-bearing forces. These plates, typically made of titanium or polymer materials, provide structural support during bone healing post-osteotomy procedures. The technology is gaining traction as a preferred alternative to total knee replacements for younger patients with early-stage osteoarthritis.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing prevalence of knee osteoarthritis, particularly among aging populations, coupled with rising demand for joint preservation surgeries.
