</p><p><strong>Cold heading wire</strong> refers to a specialized material processed to achieve a balance of <strong>formability</strong>, <strong>ductility</strong>, and <strong>high tensile strength</strong>. It is primarily utilized in the production of fasteners such as:</p><p>
</p><p>Bolts</p><p>Screws</p><p>Pins</p><p>Rivets</p><p>Studs</p><p>Tie rods</p><p>
</p><p>This wire type is essential across multiple sectors including <strong>automotive</strong>, <strong>aerospace</strong>, <strong>construction</strong>, and <strong>industrial manufacturing</strong> due to its capacity to maintain shape and strength under extreme forming pressure.</p><p>
