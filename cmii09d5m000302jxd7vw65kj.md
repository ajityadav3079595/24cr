</p><p>Asset Care Management (ACM) refers to the strategic management of an organization’s assets throughout their lifecycle, with the goal of maximizing operational efficiency and extending the life of assets. This approach includes a variety of processes such as predictive maintenance, real-time monitoring, performance tracking, and risk management to ensure assets operate optimally and cost-effectively. The goal of asset care management is to reduce downtime, extend asset lifespan, and optimize productivity. It leverages a combination of modern technologies, including Internet of Things (IoT) sensors, automation tools, and asset management systems (AMS), to create smarter, more efficient asset management practices across industries like manufacturing, energy, and utilities.</p><p>

<strong>Opportunities</strong>:</p><p>
