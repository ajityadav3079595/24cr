</p><p>Rinse additives are chemical formulations designed to enhance drying performance and eliminate water spots on dishes, glassware, and utensils during commercial and institutional dishwashing processes. These products typically contain surfactants and wetting agents that lower water surface tension, enabling faster sheeting action and spot-free drying. The market offers both solid and liquid formulations tailored for machine warewashing applications across foodservice, healthcare, and hospitality sectors.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by stringent hygiene standards in foodservice operations and rising demand from institutional sectors.
