</p><p>Triacetate long fiber is a specialized cellulose-based synthetic fiber manufactured through the acetylation of wood pulp. This high-performance material exhibits excellent drapability, wrinkle resistance, and moisture absorption properties, making it particularly suitable for premium apparel applications. The fiber contains either 59%-60% or over 61% acetic acid content, with the higher concentration variant offering superior heat resistance and dimensional stability.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand for sustainable textiles in the fashion industry, coupled with the fiber's biodegradability and eco-friendly production process.
