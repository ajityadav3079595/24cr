</p><p><strong>Calcium Propionate</strong> is a chemical compound made from calcium salt of propanoic acid. It is widely used as a food additive for its preservative properties, preventing mold and bacterial growth, which extends the shelf life of various food products. Recommended by the World Health Organization (WHO) and the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) for its safety, it is particularly effective in the preservation of baked goods, processed meats, dairy products, and even in animal feed. Calcium propionate ensures food stays fresh for longer, maintaining quality, taste, and texture over time.</p><p>
