</p><p>Industrial grade calcium hydrogen phosphate, with the chemical formula CaHPO4, is a white powder or particles that are odorless and tasteless. It is easily soluble in water and serves as a high-quality phosphate calcium fertilizer. This compound provides essential phosphorus and calcium elements for plants, promotes crop growth, and enhances crop yield and quality. Additionally, it is used in livestock and aquaculture as a feed additive to supplement phosphorus and calcium requirements and improve bone development and physical fitness. In the food industry, calcium hydrogen phosphate acts as a leavening agent, moisture retention agent, and stabilizer, enhancing the taste, texture, and shelf life of food products.</p><p>
