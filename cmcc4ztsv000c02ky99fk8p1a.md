These tapes are engineered to deliver superior mechanical properties, including exceptional strength-to-weight ratio, stiffness, durability, and thermal stability. They are primarily used in applications where structural integrity, weight reduction, and performance under extreme conditions are criticalâ€”such as in aerospace, automotive, wind energy, sports equipment, and industrial applications.</strong></p><p>
