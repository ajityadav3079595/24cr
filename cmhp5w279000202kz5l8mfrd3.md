<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Growing Semiconductor Industry to Fuel Demand for Lanthanum Oxide Sputtering Targets</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The semiconductor industry's rapid expansion is significantly driving the demand for <strong>lanthanum oxide </strong>sputtering targets. With the global semiconductor market projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate of around 6-8% through 2032, the need for high-purity materials like lanthanum oxide targets is increasing. The transition to smaller nanometer nodes in chip manufacturing requires advanced deposition materials, where lanthanum oxide's dielectric properties make it highly valuable. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Expansion of Display Technologies to Accelerate Market Growth</strong></p><p>
