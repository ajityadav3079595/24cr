These products create seamless protective barriers on surfaces like roofs, walls, and foundations through moisture-cured or water-emulsion technologies. The market includes variants such as <strong>elastomeric coatings</strong> for crack-bridging and <strong>cementitious formulations</strong> for concrete protection, with growing adoption in both new construction and maintenance projects worldwide.</p><p>
