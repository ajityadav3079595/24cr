</p><p>Glycerol Mono-and Distearate is a versatile compound that results from the esterification of glycerol and stearic acid. It comprises a mixture of glycerol monostearate and glycerol distearate. This substance functions primarily as an emulsifier, stabilizer, and anti-caking agent across a range of industries including food and beverage, cosmetics, plastics, and pharmaceuticals. It plays a pivotal role in ensuring product consistency, texture, and shelf life. In food products, it helps in emulsifying fat with water, while in cosmetics, it enhances creaminess and spreadability. The compound is favored due to its biodegradable nature and compatibility with various formulation types.</p><p>
