</p><p>The tamanu oil market faces significant challenges stemming from its concentrated supply chain. The extended cultivation period required for tamanu trees (5-7 years to mature) further exacerbates supply chain responsiveness to demand changes.</p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Production Costs Limit Market Penetration</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Tamanu oil's labor-intensive extraction process and relatively low yield per hectare result in production costs substantially higher than many competing botanical oils.
