These fluids perform critical functions including friction reduction, heat dissipation, and equipment protection against wear. The market comprises two primary categories: <strong>transmission fluids</strong> (including automatic and manual variants) and <strong>hydraulic fluids</strong> (mineral-based, synthetic, and semi-synthetic formulations). These products find extensive application in automotive systems, industrial machinery, and power generation equipment, with formulations continuously evolving to meet stricter environmental and performance standards.</p><p>
