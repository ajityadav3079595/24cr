</p><p>Rubber accelerator MBTS(DM), also known as dibenzothiazyl disulfide, is a critical vulcanization agent widely used in the rubber industry to enhance the curing process. It acts as a secondary accelerator when combined with primary accelerators like thiazoles, significantly improving processing efficiency, heat resistance, and mechanical properties of rubber products. MBTS(DM) finds extensive application in automotive tires, industrial rubber goods, footwear, and consumer products.</p><p>
