</p><p>Anti-corrosion pigments play a vital role in modern protective coatings. They serve as barriers that prevent the penetration of water, oxygen, and aggressive ions into material surfaces. These pigments are known for their labyrinth-like structures which increase the path of corrosive media, reducing their access to the substrate and significantly improving the corrosion resistance of coatings.</p><p>
