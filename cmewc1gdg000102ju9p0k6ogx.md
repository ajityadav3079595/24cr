</p><p>Graphite Polystyrene Foam Board is a highly effective thermal insulation material that has been enhanced with graphite additives. These additives absorb infrared (IR) radiation and reflect heat, which significantly improves the thermal performance of the material compared to traditional Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) foam. The presence of graphite allows the material to possess superior insulation properties, making it an ideal choice for applications where temperature control and energy efficiency are critical.</p><p>
</p><p>These foam boards are commonly used in various construction projects, such as insulating roofs, walls, and floors in both residential and commercial buildings. They also find utility in cold storage facilities, insulated vehicles, and ships. Their capacity to reduce energy consumption by minimizing heat loss or gain is one of the primary reasons for their widespread adoption.</p><p>
