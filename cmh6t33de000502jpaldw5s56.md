</p><p>Low viscosity silicone rubber is a specialized elastomer characterized by its fluid-like consistency (typically 500-3000 mPa.s) and superior flow properties. This material is widely used for precision applications requiring thin-layer coating, intricate mold filling, or enhanced adhesion. Key product variants include viscosity grades of 500-1000 mPa.s, 1000-2000 mPa.s, and 2000-3000 mPa.s, each serving distinct industrial requirements.</p><p>
