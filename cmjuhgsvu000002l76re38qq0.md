</p><p>Anti-dripping agents are specialized polymer additives that prevent molten material from dripping during combustion, significantly improving flame retardancy. These compounds work by promoting char formation and cross-linking polymer chains, with common types including polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), silicone-based formulations, and fluorinated additives. They find extensive application in industries where fire safety regulations are stringent, particularly in electronics, construction materials, and automotive components.</p><p>

</p><p>The anti-dripping agent market faces considerable challenges due to elevated production costs associated with specialized formulations.
