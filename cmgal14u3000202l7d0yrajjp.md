</p><p>Environmental regulations and corporate sustainability goals are prompting manufacturers to develop bio-based and recyclable polyurethane foam variants. <strong>MARKET CHALLENGES</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Stringent Environmental Regulations Pose Compliance Challenges</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The industry faces increasing regulatory pressure regarding foam formulation chemistry, particularly concerning VOC emissions and flame retardancy. </p><p>High-density microcellular PU foam competes with silicone-based foams, expanded polypropylene, and other engineered materials that offer different performance trade-offs.
