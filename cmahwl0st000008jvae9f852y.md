</p><p>Gold targets are specialized materials made primarily of high-purity gold that are used in thin film deposition processes. These targets are most commonly employed in physical vapor deposition (PVD) and sputtering techniques to deposit thin layers of gold onto various substrates. The high purity of the gold ensures that the thin films produced are of superior quality, which is essential in applications requiring high conductivity and minimal contamination.</p><p>
</p><p>The use of gold targets is critical in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, optics and photonics, and decoration, where thin gold films are required for various technological advancements. These targets are often used to create coatings and components that are resistant to corrosion and have excellent electrical properties. The global demand for gold targets is linked to the increasing use of gold in various industrial applications and its inherent advantages in electronic and optical components.</p><p>
