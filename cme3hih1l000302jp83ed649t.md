It is a cost-effective technology used in assembling semiconductor packages, with applications at frequencies above 100 GHz. Aluminum wire bonding, an alternative to gold wire bonding, requires no heating of the surface, making use of force and ultrasonics for bond formation. This method offers advantages like temperature sensitivity and integrity over gold bonding, especially for hermetically sealed packages on aluminum surfaces.</p><p>
