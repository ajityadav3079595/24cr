</p><p>Bonderized steel is a specialized coated steel product where zinc phosphate treatment creates a corrosion-resistant surface that enhances paint adhesion. This electrochemical conversion coating process, often called "bonderizing," produces steel sheets and strips with superior durability for applications requiring both protection and aesthetic finishes. The technology serves as a critical intermediate step between bare steel and final painting processes in various industries.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth stems from expanding construction activities and rising automotive production globally, particularly in emerging economies. While Asia-Pacific currently dominates demand due to rapid infrastructure development, North America shows steady growth from roofing and HVAC applications. Key players like Rolled Steel Products and Briggs Steel are expanding production capacities to meet the growing demand from both traditional and emerging applications.</p><p>
