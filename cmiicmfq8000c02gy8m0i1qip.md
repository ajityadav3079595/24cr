</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Breakthroughs in Advanced Recycling Technologies Expand Market Potential</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Recent technological advancements in purification and decontamination processes have significantly improved the quality and safety of recycled PP for food contact applications.
