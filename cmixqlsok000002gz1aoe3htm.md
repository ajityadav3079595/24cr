</p><p>Steam chemical indicator strips are sterilization monitoring devices that provide visual confirmation of steam sterilization process parameters. These strips contain heat-sensitive chemicals that change color when exposed to specific temperature, time, and steam conditions. They are classified into different categories (Class 1 through Class 6) based on their sensitivity and the specific sterilization parameters they monitor.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing healthcare-associated infection prevention measures, stringent sterilization regulations, and rising surgical procedures worldwide. Pharmaceutical industry expansion and the growing adoption of single-use medical devices further contribute to demand.
