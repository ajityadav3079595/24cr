</p><p>The North American surface materials market, valued at $8.92 billion in 2024, is demonstrating robust growth at a 5.5% CAGR. Commercial interior applications now account for 38% of regional demand, particularly in healthcare and education sectors requiring hygienic surfaces.</p><p>
