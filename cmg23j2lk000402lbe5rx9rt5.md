</p><p>Eutectic solder is a specialized alloy mixture with a precise melting point that solidifies without passing through a plastic phase. These alloys typically contain tin-lead (Sn-Pb) or lead-free compositions like tin-silver-copper (SAC), which are widely used in electronics manufacturing due to their reliable thermal and electrical conductivity. The eutectic composition ensures minimal temperature variance during melting, making it ideal for precision applications in semiconductor packaging and surface-mount technology (SMT).</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by Southeast Asia's expanding electronics manufacturing sector, particularly in Malaysia, Thailand, and Vietnam where PCB production increased by 14% in 2023.
