<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Sustainable Fashion Movement Accelerates Cotton Fabric Demand</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The global shift toward sustainable and eco-friendly textiles is driving substantial growth in the cotton fabric market. As consumer awareness about environmental impact grows, demand for natural fibers like cotton has increased by approximately 15% annually since 2020. Major fashion retailers have pledged to increase their use of sustainable materials, with cotton being prioritized due to its biodegradability and lower environmental footprint compared to synthetic alternatives. Fast fashion brands now dedicate an average of 25-30% of their collections to organic or responsibly-sourced cotton products, creating significant market expansion opportunities.</p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Emerging Economies Boost Market Growth Through Manufacturing Expansion</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Asia-Pacific's textile manufacturing sector continues to drive global cotton fabric demand, accounting for over 60% of worldwide cotton consumption in 2024.
