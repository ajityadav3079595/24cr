</p><p>Norbornene, also known as norbornylene or norcamphene, is a bridged cyclic hydrocarbon with a pungent odor. It consists of a cyclohexene ring with a methylene bridge between carbons 1 and 4, and a double bond that introduces significant ring strain and reactivity.</p><p>
</p><p>Norbornene is a key compound in the chemical industry, used in various applications due to its unique chemical properties. It is primarily employed in the production of pharmaceuticals, cyclic olefin copolymers (COC), insecticides, and special spices.</p><p>
