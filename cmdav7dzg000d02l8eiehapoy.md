<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Rising Demand for Sustainable Packaging to Accelerate Market Expansion</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Global shift toward <strong>eco-friendly packaging solutions</strong> is significantly driving the plastic tubes packaging external coatings market. With increasing environmental concerns, manufacturers are investing in coatings that enhance recyclability while maintaining product integrity. Recent data indicates that over <strong>65% of consumers</strong> now prefer sustainable packaging, pushing brands to adopt coated tubes that align with circular economy principles. Major players are responding by developing water-based and powder coatings that reduce VOC emissions by up to <strong>90%</strong> compared to traditional solvent-based alternatives.</p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Growth in Personal Care and Pharmaceutical Industries to Fuel Adoption</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The <strong>$900 billion global personal care industry</strong> continues to expand at <strong>5.8% CAGR</strong>, with plastic tubes remaining the preferred packaging format for creams, ointments and cosmetics. Addressing this gap demands unprecedented collaboration between policymakers, waste management firms and packaging manufacturers.</p><p>
