These reactive compounds feature a trichlorosilyl group (-SiCl3) attached to an alkyl chain, making them highly versatile for applications ranging from surface modification to semiconductor manufacturing. Key variants include octadecyl, dodecyl, methyl, propyl, and octyl trichlorosilanes, each offering distinct chemical properties for industrial applications.</p><p>
