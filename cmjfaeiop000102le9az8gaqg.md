</p><p>Composite Aluminum Wall Panels are advanced architectural cladding systems consisting of two aluminum sheets bonded to a thermoplastic core material. These panels provide superior durability, weather resistance, and thermal insulation while offering design flexibility through various color and finish options. The core materials typically include polyethylene (PE), fire-retardant (FR) mineral cores, or other composite materials engineered for specific performance requirements.</p><p>

</p><p>The composite panel industry faces significant pricing pressures from fluctuating aluminum and polyethylene costs.
