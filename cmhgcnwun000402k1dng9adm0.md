</p><p><strong>Market Overview</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Potassium Cetyl Phosphate is a widely used ingredient in the cosmetics and personal care industry. It functions as an emulsifier, surfactant, and stabilizer, ensuring a smooth and homogenous texture in various skincare formulations. This compound is the potassium salt derived from a mixture of phosphoric acid esters and cetyl alcohol, a non-irritating fatty alcohol known for its mildness. Due to its gentle nature, it is commonly found in sunscreens, moisturizers, cleansers, and anti-aging creams. Its ability to stabilize oil-in-water emulsions makes it a valuable component in cosmetic formulations, particularly in sunscreen products where consistency and stability are essential.</p><p>
