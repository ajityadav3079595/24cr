</p><p>Helical submerged arc welded pipes are large-diameter steel pipes manufactured through a specialized welding process where the seam follows a spiral path along the pipe length. These pipes offer superior strength-to-weight ratios and are widely used in high-pressure fluid transportation applications across oil &amp; gas, water infrastructure, and industrial sectors. The production process involves continuous helical forming of steel strips followed by submerged arc welding for enhanced structural integrity.</p><p>
