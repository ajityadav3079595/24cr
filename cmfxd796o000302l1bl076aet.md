# Hollow Core Anti Resonant Fiber Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Hollow Core Anti Resonant Fiber (HCARF) is a specialized fiber technology that finds applications in fiber optic sensing, laser energy transmission, fiber optic gyroscopes, polarization control, and THz wave transmission. Typically made from various polymer materials, HCARF in the THz band offers unique advantages compared to metal THz waveguides. It can be manufactured using 3D printing technology and holds promise for various high-tech applications.</p><p>
