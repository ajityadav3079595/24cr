# Paper Sizing Agent Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Paper sizing agents are chemical additives applied during the paper manufacturing process to improve the paper's properties, such as water resistance, printability, and durability. Sizing agents control the paper's absorption of liquids and ink, ensuring a smooth and uniform surface for printing and other applications. Common types of paper sizing agents include <strong style="font-size:13px">alkyl ketene dimer (AKD)</strong>, <strong style="font-size:13px">rosin-based agents</strong>, and <strong style="font-size:13px">styrene-acrylic copolymers</strong>. These agents are integral in producing high-quality paper products for packaging, printing, and writing.</p><p>
