</p><p>While magnet bonding adhesives offer numerous performance advantages, their curing processes present significant challenges for manufacturers aiming to optimize production throughput. Even advanced light-cure formulations often need supplementary heat treatment to achieve full bond strength, adding complexity to production processes. These limitations become particularly problematic in automotive manufacturing, where cycle times frequently measure in seconds rather than minutes.</p><p>
