</p><p>The global push toward sustainable construction is driving unprecedented demand for Low-E coated glass. With buildings accounting for nearly 40% of worldwide energy consumption, architectural trends increasingly favor materials that enhance thermal performance. Low-E glass emerges as a preferred solution, capable of reducing HVAC energy consumption by 30-50% compared to conventional glazing.
