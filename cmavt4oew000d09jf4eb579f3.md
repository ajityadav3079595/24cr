Polyimide plastic is a high-performance polymer known for its exceptional thermal stability, mechanical strength, and resistance to chemicals and radiation. It is widely utilized across industries such as electronics, aerospace, automotive, and healthcare due to its ability to maintain performance in extreme temperatures exceeding 300°C. Polyimides are commonly found in applications such as flexible printed circuits, insulation films, heat-resistant coatings, and high-performance adhesives.</p><p>
