</p><p>Feed Grade High Selenium Yeast is a type of supplemental selenium primarily used in animal feed. It is a form of organic selenium derived from the yeast Saccharomyces cerevisiae, also known as baker’s or brewer’s yeast. When cultivated in a selenium-enriched medium, this yeast absorbs the selenium and converts it into selenomethionine, a naturally occurring form of selenium found in food. This organic form of selenium is considered more bioavailable to animals compared to inorganic selenium sources.</p><p>
</p><p>Selenium is an essential trace element for livestock, aiding in various biological functions such as enzyme activation, immune system enhancement, and reproduction. Selenium deficiency in animals can lead to several health issues, including reduced fertility, muscle degeneration, and weakened immune responses. Hence, the inclusion of selenium yeast in animal feed has become a critical practice in ensuring optimal animal health and productivity.</p><p>

	</p><p><strong>Increasing Demand for High-Quality Animal Feed</strong>: As the global population continues to rise, there is an increasing demand for high-quality animal products such as meat, dairy, and eggs.
