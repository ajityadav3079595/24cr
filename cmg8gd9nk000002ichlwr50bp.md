# Global Acoustic Plasterboard Market Research Report 2025-2032

<p>The global acoustic plasterboard market was valued at <strong>US$ 1.93 billion in 2024</strong> and is projected to reach <strong>US$ 2.97 billion by 2032</strong>, exhibiting a <strong>Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 5.5%</strong> during the forecast period (2025–2032). The market growth is driven by increasing demand for noise reduction solutions in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. The rising awareness of soundproofing benefits, coupled with stringent building regulations, is further propelling market expansion. Historically, the market has shown steady growth due to urbanization and infrastructure development, and this trend is expected to continue with advancements in material technology and sustainable construction practices.</p><p>
</p><p>The <strong>Global Acoustic Plasterboard Market</strong> refers to the industry focused on manufacturing and distributing specialized plasterboards designed to enhance sound insulation in buildings. These plasterboards are engineered with sound-dampening materials such as gypsum, mineral wool, or other composites to reduce noise transmission between rooms or from external sources. Key products in this market include <strong>10mm and 13mm plasterboards</strong>, as well as other customized thicknesses tailored for specific acoustic requirements. Acoustic plasterboards are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications to improve indoor environmental quality and comply with noise control regulations.
