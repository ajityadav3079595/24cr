</p><p>PVC Gypsum Ceiling Boards are lightweight composite panels consisting of a gypsum core laminated with PVC film on both surfaces. These boards combine the fire-resistant properties of gypsum with the moisture resistance and durability of PVC, making them ideal for interior applications in commercial and residential buildings. Key product variations include different thickness options ranging from 7mm to over 8mm, catering to diverse installation requirements.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is propelled by increasing construction expenditure in emerging economies, particularly in Asia-Pacific, where the material's cost-effectiveness and quick installation properties are highly valued. While residential applications dominate current usage, commercial segment adoption is accelerating due to rising demand for modular office spaces and educational facilities. Major manufacturers are expanding production capacities to meet this growing demand, with several Chinese firms increasing output by 15-20% annually.</p><p>
