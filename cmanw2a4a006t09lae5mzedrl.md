Carbon Nanotube (CNT) Materials are cylindrical molecules consisting of rolled-up sheets of single-layer carbon atoms, also known as graphene. These nanomaterials can be classified as either single-walled carbon nanotubes (SWCNTs) or multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWCNTs), depending on the number of concentric graphene cylinders.
