</p><p>Furthermore, pure copper solutions remain preferred for ultra-high conductivity applications, despite their higher cost. </p><p>Manufacturers face additional pressure from end-users demanding higher performance specifications. The push for thinner coatings with greater uniformity requires continuous process improvements and capital investments, creating barriers for smaller competitors.</p><p>
