</p><p>Rhodiola Rosea P.E. is a standardized plant extract derived from the roots of Rhodiola rosea, a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family. This adaptogenic herb has been traditionally used in Chinese and Tibetan medicine for its stress-relieving and fatigue-reducing properties. The extract contains active compounds such as rosavins and salidroside, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.</p><p>
</p><p>While the market shows steady growth, it faces significant supply challenges due to the plant's endangered status. Natural stocks have been declining since the 1970s due to overharvesting, with the species now listed as severely endangered in Siberia. This scarcity has created a supply-demand imbalance, leading to rising prices. China currently dominates production with a 74% market share, while key manufacturers like Layn, Martin Bauer Group, and Huisong Pharmaceuticals collectively hold 27% of the global market.</p><p>
