<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Geogrid Fabric is a geosynthetic material made of polymers used to reinforce soil behind retaining walls. It is installed in horizontal layers between wall courses, extending into the soil behind a wall to stabilize the soil, increase mass, and enhance wall stability.</p><p>
