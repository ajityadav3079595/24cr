<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>

</p><p>The integration of advanced technologies like RFID and IoT into security seals presents technical challenges that hinder widespread adoption. The maritime shipping sector, which accounts for approximately 35% of seal demand, has been particularly slow to adopt electronic seals due to harsh environmental conditions that can compromise device functionality.</p><p>
