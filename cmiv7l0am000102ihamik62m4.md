</p><p>While the EBA market shows strong growth potential, it faces significant challenges from raw material price volatility. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Environmental Regulations to Impact Market Growth Trajectory</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Increasing environmental regulations present another challenge for the EBA market. The European Union's Single-Use Plastics Directive and similar regulations in other regions are driving a shift toward bio-based and more readily recyclable materials. While EBA offers technical advantages, its petroleum-based nature makes it subject to scrutiny under these evolving regulations. The packaging industry, which accounts for over 30% of EBA consumption, is particularly affected by these regulatory changes as brands seek more sustainable alternatives.</p><p>
</p><p>Additionally, evolving REACH regulations regarding chemical substances may require reformulation of certain EBA grades, potentially increasing production costs.
