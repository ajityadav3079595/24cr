</p><p>Ferromagnetic fluids, also known as ferrofluids, are colloidal liquids containing nanoscale ferromagnetic particles suspended in a carrier fluid. These fluids exhibit strong magnetic properties when exposed to a magnetic field, making them valuable in various industrial and scientific applications. Key products include those based on <strong>oleic acid, tetramethylammonium hydroxide, citric acid, and soy lecithin</strong>. End User Industry Analysis</p><p>

The <strong>optical industry</strong> benefits from ferrofluids in lens polishing and adaptive optics.
