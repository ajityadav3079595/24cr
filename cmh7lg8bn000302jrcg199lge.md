Brazil is emerging as an integrated player through Sigma Lithium's green lithium project. Infrastructure bottlenecks and policy uncertainty continue to restrain output, causing the region to supply raw materials rather than battery-grade lithium carbonate. Recent trade agreements with China and Europe aim to add value to local lithium processing.</p><p>

While current market share remains below 3%, projects like Saudi Arabia's EV manufacturing hub signal long-term demand growth. Challenges include underdeveloped mining regulations and limited technical expertise, though partnerships with Chinese and European firms are accelerating capability building in battery material production.</p><p>
