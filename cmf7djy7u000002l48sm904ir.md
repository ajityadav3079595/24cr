</p><p><strong>Sludge Dispersant Market</strong> refers to the global market dealing with chemical agents specifically formulated to prevent the accumulation and promote the breakdown of sludge within industrial systems, such as boilers, marine engines, and wastewater treatment facilities. These chemicals function by dispersing suspended solids, oils, and other impurities, thereby enhancing system efficiency and preventing blockages or corrosion. The sludge dispersant market serves industries including marine, power generation, manufacturing, and oil &amp; gas, where heat exchange systems and high-temperature processes make sludge management essential.
