</p><p>Heat dissipation grease, also known as thermal grease or thermal compound, is a thermally conductive paste used to improve the heat transfer between surfaces, such as between a microprocessor and a heatsink. This material fills microscopic air gaps that would otherwise act as thermal insulation, significantly enhancing cooling efficiency. HD grease has good fluidity and can be applied to heating devices by dispensing, printing, etc. It is suitable for thermally conductive functional composite materials with smaller gaps or zero gaps. HD grease has ultra-low thermal resistance, so it is suitable for high-calorie close-fitting scenarios, and has the lowest thickness of thermally conductive products, which can quickly transfer heat from equipment to achieve good temperature control.</p><p>
