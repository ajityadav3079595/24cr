# Precious Metal Recovery Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>The Precious Metal Recovery market refers to the process of recovering precious and rare metals, such as gold, platinum, ruthenium, rhodium, palladium, and iridium, from waste and secondary sources. These metals, typically extracted from mining tailings or used electronics, are valuable due to their high rarity and importance in various industries. Precious metal recovery involves highly specialized, sometimes corrosive processes, but it is crucial in promoting sustainability by recycling materials that would otherwise go to waste. This market plays a vital role in securing a stable supply of precious metals, reducing the need for traditional mining operations, and contributing to environmental preservation.</p><p>
</p><p>Precious metal recovery is driven by the increasing demand for these metals across several industries, including automotive, electronics, healthcare, and manufacturing.
