The hot-dip galvanized aluminum plate is made of cold-rolled steel plate with various strength and thickness specifications as the base plate, which is hot-dip galvanized.The product is made of zinc and aluminum coating on the surface of steel plate. The hot-dip galvanized aluminum sheet not only maintains the strength and formability of steel, but also combines the corrosion resistance of zinc and aluminum. At the same time, the surface of the hot-dip galvanized aluminum sheet produces a highly decorative bright silver gray color and regular patterns, making the product more beautiful. Hot-dip galvanized aluminum sheet is widely used in industrial products such as buildings, automobiles, home appliances, etc. In the construction industry, light steel keel, building floor panel, corrugated board, roller shutter door, etc. used for various industrial and civil buildings; In the automobile industry, it is used for the shell, inner plate, bottom plate, etc. of the automobile body; In household appliances and light industry, it is used for the shell and bottom plate of various household appliances.</p><p>
