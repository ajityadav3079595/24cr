<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyurethane foam for automotive refers to a type of foam material made from polyurethane, a highly versatile and widely used polymer. This foam is utilized in various automotive applications due to its exceptional cushioning, insulation, and sound-absorbing properties. It is commonly found in vehicle seats, headrests, armrests, door panels, and even under-the-hood components. The material provides enhanced comfort, energy absorption, durability, and resistance to wear and tear, making it a preferred choice in modern automobile manufacturing.</p><p>
