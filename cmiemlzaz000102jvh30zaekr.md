<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Environmental Regulations Constraining Production Expansion</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Stringent environmental policies worldwide are limiting capacity growth in the high purity lead sector. While high-end semiconductors still require lead-based solders, this trend could potentially impact 15-20% of current high purity lead demand by 2032 unless new applications emerge to offset losses.</p><p>
