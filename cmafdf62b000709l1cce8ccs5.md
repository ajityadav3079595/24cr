# Illite Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Illite is a secondary mineral precipitate and a phyllosilicate, representing a layered alumino-silicate. It forms as an altered product of muscovite and feldspar in various environmental conditions. Structurally akin to muscovite, illite contains more silicon, magnesium, iron, and water, yet less tetrahedral aluminum and interlayer potassium.</p><p>
