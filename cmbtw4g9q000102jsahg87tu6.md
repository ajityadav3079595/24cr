</p><p>The <strong>global thermal spray material market</strong> involves the production and application of materials used in thermal spray coating processes. Thermal spray is a technique where melted or heated materials are sprayed onto a surface to form a coating. These coatings enhance properties such as wear resistance, thermal insulation, and corrosion protection. Key materials include metals, ceramics, polymers, and composites. The market serves industries like aerospace, automotive, electronics, and energy, where high-performance coatings are essential for durability and efficiency.
