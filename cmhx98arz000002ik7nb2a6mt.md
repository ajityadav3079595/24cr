It is a lactic acid with high optical rotation produced through bio-fermentation technology using carbohydrates similar to sugar. The finished product of D-lactic acid is a colorless or light yellow clear viscous liquid with a slightly sour taste. It is hygroscopic and shows an acidic reaction in aqueous solutions.</strong></p><p>
