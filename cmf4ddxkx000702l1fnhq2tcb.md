</p><p>Normal paraffins (n-paraffins) are straight-chain saturated hydrocarbons widely used as feedstock in petrochemical applications. These high-purity alkanes serve as essential raw materials for producing linear alkylbenzene (LAB) - a key component in biodegradable detergents. The product finds additional applications as solvents, lubricants, and intermediates in specialty chemicals.</p><p>
