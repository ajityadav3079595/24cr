</p><p>Wax dispersions are aqueous-based formulations containing micronized wax particles that provide surface protection, slip resistance, and matting effects across various applications. These dispersions primarily consist of polyethylene, polypropylene, paraffin, or Fischer-Tropsch waxes suspended in water with emulsifiers. They are widely used in industrial coatings, packaging materials, and specialty applications where surface modification is required.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from packaging industries, particularly for food and pharmaceutical applications where barrier properties are crucial. Furthermore, stringent environmental regulations promoting water-based solutions over solvent-based alternatives are accelerating adoption.
