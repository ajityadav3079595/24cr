</p><p>High Purity Pig Iron, commonly referred to as nodular pig iron or foundry pig iron, is a premium quality raw material with a low impurity profile and high carbon content. It is specially designed for use in foundries to produce high-quality cast iron products such as ductile iron and gray iron. This type of pig iron typically contains very low levels of sulfur, phosphorus, manganese, and other impurities that can adversely affect the mechanical properties of the final cast product.</p><p>
</p><p>Due to its consistency and superior metallurgical properties, high purity pig iron is crucial in achieving high-performance standards in applications that require superior tensile strength, thermal resistance, and machinability. It is extensively used in the automotive industry, heavy machinery production, and infrastructure sectors.</p><p>
