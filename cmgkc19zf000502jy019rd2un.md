<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Production Costs Limit Market Penetration in Price-Sensitive Segments</strong></p><p>
</p><p>While composite copper foils offer superior performance characteristics, their higher production costs relative to conventional copper foil present a significant market constraint. The specialized manufacturing processes involving vacuum deposition and precision bonding technologies can increase production expenses by 25-40% compared to standard electrolytic copper foil. This cost premium makes adoption challenging in price-sensitive applications such as consumer electronics and certain automotive components where manufacturers prioritize cost reduction over performance enhancements.</p><p>

</p><p>Scaling composite copper foil production to meet rapidly growing demand presents multiple technical challenges.
