</p><p>Caproic acid, also known as hexanoic acid, is a six-carbon saturated fatty acid with the chemical formula C6H12O2. It is characterized by a pungent odor and is naturally found in various animal fats and plant oils. Caproic acid is an important intermediate in the manufacture of esters for artificial flavors and perfumes and also serves as a precursor in the production of biofuels, lubricants, and plasticizers. It is also widely utilized in feed additives, owing to its antimicrobial properties and ability to enhance gut health in animals. As industries increasingly shift towards sustainable and bio-based chemicals, caproic acid has gained significant attention due to its biodegradability, low toxicity, and wide range of industrial applications.</p><p>
