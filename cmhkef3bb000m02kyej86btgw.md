</p><p>Ultra fine spherical alumina is a high-purity ceramic powder characterized by its uniform particle size distribution and spherical morphology. These engineered particles typically range between 0.2-100 microns, offering exceptional properties including high thermal conductivity, excellent dielectric strength, and superior chemical stability. The material finds extensive applications as thermal interface materials in electronics and as advanced fillers in composites.</p><p>
