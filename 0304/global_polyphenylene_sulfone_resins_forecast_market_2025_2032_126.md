</p><p>Polyphenylene Sulfone (PPS) Resin is a high-performance engineering thermoplastic composed of alternating sulfur atoms and phenylene rings in a para-substitution pattern. This polymer is renowned for its outstanding thermal stability, chemical resistance, dimensional stability, and inherent flame retardancy. These characteristics make PPS Resin a preferred material across various industries, including electronics, automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications.</p><p>
