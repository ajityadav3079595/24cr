</p><p>Highly Reactive Metakaolin (HRM) is a refined and thermally activated form of kaolin clay, processed at temperatures ranging between 650°C to 850°C to enhance its pozzolanic reactivity. This high-reactivity material is primarily used as a supplementary cementitious material (SCM) in concrete and mortar applications. Its chemical composition, typically rich in aluminosilicate, enables it to react with calcium hydroxide (a byproduct of cement hydration) to form additional calcium silicate hydrate (C-S-H), thereby increasing concrete's strength and durability.</p><p>
</p><p>HRM improves workability, reduces permeability, enhances resistance to chemical attacks, and minimizes efflorescence. It also contributes significantly to reducing the carbon footprint of concrete by allowing a partial replacement of Portland cement, whose production is highly energy-intensive.</p><p>
