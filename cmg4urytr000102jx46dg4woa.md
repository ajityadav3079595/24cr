</p><p>Tertiary fatty amines are nitrogen-containing organic compounds derived from fatty acids, primarily used as intermediates in surfactants, biocides, and corrosion inhibitors. These versatile chemicals find applications across textile chemicals, oilfield chemicals, water treatment, and daily care products due to their cationic properties and surface-active characteristics. Key product segments include C8-C10, C10-C12, and C12-C18 chain length variants.</p><p>

</p><p>The water treatment industry has emerged as a significant driver for the tertiary fatty amines market, with expanding applications in municipal wastewater treatment and industrial processes.
