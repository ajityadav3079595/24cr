</p><p>PVC-O (Molecularly Oriented Polyvinyl Chloride) pipes are a highly advanced version of traditional PVC pipes, known for their superior physical and mechanical properties. These pipes undergo a molecular orientation process that realigns the PVC molecules through high-pressure expansion, resulting in a layered structure that enhances strength, impact resistance, and flexibility while significantly reducing weight.</p><p>
</p><p>PVC-O pipes are extensively used in water supply and distribution, irrigation systems, sewage networks, and industrial piping. Their excellent durability, high fatigue resistance, and ability to withstand high internal pressures make them an ideal choice for large-scale infrastructure projects, especially in water-stressed and developing regions.</p><p>
