These intermediates serve as critical building blocks for contrast media formulations utilized in computed tomography (CT) scans, angiography, and other radiography procedures. Key types include iodine chloride, 3-amino-1,2-propanediol, and various triiodobenzoic acid derivatives, each serving distinct roles in contrast agent synthesis.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand for diagnostic imaging procedures worldwide, with over 80 million CT scans performed annually in the US alone. Leading players like GE Healthcare and Bayer AG are investing in production optimization to meet the growing demand while maintaining compliance with pharmaceutical-grade purity standards.</p><p>
