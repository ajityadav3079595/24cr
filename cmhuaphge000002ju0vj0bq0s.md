</p><p>Erythrosine, commonly known as Red No. 3, is a synthetic cherry-pink food dye derived from fluorone. </p><p>The market growth is being driven by rising demand for processed foods and vibrant food coloration, especially in emerging economies. Key players like BASF SE and Univar Colour are investing in reformulation strategies to address these concerns while maintaining market share. The Asia-Pacific region currently dominates consumption patterns due to booming food processing industries in China and India.</p><p>

</p><p>The global erythrosine market is experiencing significant growth driven by rising demand from the processed food industry. Recent formulations have improved erythrosine's stability in acidic environments, making it particularly valuable for fruit-flavored products and carbonated beverages.</p><p>
