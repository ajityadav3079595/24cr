</p><p>Quillaia extract, derived from the bark of the Quillaja saponaria tree native to Chile, is a natural surfactant and foaming agent widely used in food &amp; beverages, cosmetics, and animal feed industries. Its key bioactive components - saponins - provide emulsifying, foaming, and stabilising properties, making it particularly valuable as a natural alternative to synthetic additives. The extract is commercially available in both liquid and powder forms, with varying saponin concentrations (typically 10-80%) to meet different application requirements.</p><p>
