<p><strong style="font-size:13px">High hydrogen silicone oil</strong> is a type of silicone oil with a hydrogen content exceeding 1.5%. High hydrogen silicone oil is valued for its superior thermal stability, excellent water repellency, and chemical resistance, making it a preferred choice in various industrial and commercial processes.</p><p>
