The development of needle coke-based electrodes with superior thermal shock resistance has created additional demand for specialized billets. </p><p>These billets serve as essential components in electric arc furnaces used for steel production and are also crucial in electrochemical furnaces used in metallurgical and chemical industries. Short-term fluctuations in raw material supply can challenge producers, yet the material's unique properties ensure long-term reliability.</p><p>
</p><p>In steel production, graphite electrodes are used in electric arc furnaces (EAF) for melting scrap steel. The graphite electrodes facilitate the passing of electricity through the furnace, enabling the heat required for melting. As such, graphite electrode billets are vital for the production of steel and other alloys. Moreover, as recycling of scrap metal grows in popularity, the role of these billets becomes even more pronounced in sustainable manufacturing cycles.</p><p>
