</p><p>Polyester film is a high-performance material manufactured from polyethylene terephthalate (PET) resin, known for its exceptional mechanical and chemical properties. These films offer high tensile strength, thermal stability, excellent barrier properties against gases and moisture, along with superior electrical insulation capabilities. The material finds diverse applications across packaging, electronics, and industrial sectors due to its versatility and durability.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from the packaging industry, which accounts for over 57% of total consumption, and expanding applications in flexible electronics. However, environmental concerns regarding PET waste are prompting manufacturers to invest in sustainable alternatives. Key players like Toray and Mitsubishi Chemical are leading innovation in bio-based polyester films, while China dominates production with 39% market share due to established manufacturing infrastructure and growing domestic demand.</p><p>
