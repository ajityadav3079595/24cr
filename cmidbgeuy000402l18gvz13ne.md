</p><p>Lining coatings are protective layers applied to the interior surfaces of pipes, tanks and industrial equipment to prevent corrosion and chemical degradation. These specialized coatings include thermoplastics, fusion-bonded epoxies, polyurea and concrete weight coatings, each offering unique properties for different operational environments. The external counterpart, pipeline coatings, serves as the first line of defense against environmental factors.</p><p>
