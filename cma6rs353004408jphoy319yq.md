# EDM Wire (Consumable) Market, Global Outlook & Forecast 2025-2032

<p>The EDM Wire (Consumable) market encompasses the production and distribution of specialized wires used in Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM). These wires generate electrical discharges that shape and cut metal components with high precision. The main types of EDM wires include brass, copper, and zinc-coated wires, catering to industries like aerospace, automotive, medical, electronics, and tool &amp; die manufacturing.</p><p>
