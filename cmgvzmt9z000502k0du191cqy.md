# Nootkatone (CAS 4674-50-4) Market, Global Outlook & Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Nootkatone (CAS 4674-50-4) is a naturally occurring organic compound classified as a sesquiterpene and a ketone. It is the primary contributor to the characteristic aroma and flavor of grapefruit. Nootkatone is commonly found in its crystalline form but can also be synthesized through biotechnological processes. Due to its potent fragrance and bioactive properties, nootkatone is widely used in the flavors and fragrances industry, personal care products, and pest control applications.</p><p>
