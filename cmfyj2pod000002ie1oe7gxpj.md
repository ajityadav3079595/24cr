</p><p>Pharma Grade Lactose Powder is a high-purity excipient derived from whey through crystallization, refining, and micronization processes. As a critical pharmaceutical ingredient, it serves multiple functions including as a filler, binder, and diluent in tablet formulations, dry powder inhalers, and capsule manufacturing. The product is classified into two primary types - DPI (Direct Powder Inhalation) Lactose and OSD (Oral Solid Dosage) Lactose - each meeting stringent pharmacopeial standards for particle size distribution and flow properties.</p><p>
