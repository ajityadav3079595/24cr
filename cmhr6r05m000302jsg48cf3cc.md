<strong>MARKET CHALLENGES</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Technical Limitations in High-pH Applications to Restrict Market Potential</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Despite their numerous advantages, liquid sulfosuccinates face technical constraints in alkaline environments, limiting their use in certain industrial applications. The ester linkage in sulfosuccinates undergoes hydrolysis at pH levels above 9.0, significantly reducing product effectiveness. This becomes problematic in heavy-duty cleaning formulations and industrial processes where high alkalinity is required. While recent research has developed more stable derivatives, these modifications often come at a significant cost premium. Overcoming this limitation without compromising biodegradability or cost-effectiveness remains a key technical challenge for manufacturers.</p><p>
