</p><p>Painting gouache refers to a type of water-based paint that offers a rich, opaque finish compared to traditional watercolors. It is composed of pigment, water, and a binding agent such as gum arabic, similar to watercolor paints. However, gouache has a higher concentration of pigment, which makes it more opaque and less transparent. This gives gouache its signature matte finish.</p><p>
</p><p>Gouache paint is commonly used for fine arts, illustration, and design. It is highly favored for its smooth texture, fast-drying properties, and the ability to layer and rework without disturbing previous layers. It is also widely used in graphic design, advertising, and for educational purposes due to its affordability and ease of use.</p><p>
