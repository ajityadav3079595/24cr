These compounds form a protective barrier on plant surfaces, preventing spore germination and fungal penetration. The primary types include mancozeb (which dominates over 60% of market share), thiram, and propineb, with mancozeb being particularly effective against late blight in potatoes and downy mildew in grapes.</p><p>
