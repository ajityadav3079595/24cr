</p><p>Halogen-Free Flame Retardant Chemicals are specialized additives incorporated into polymers, textiles, and other materials to reduce the risk of fire. These chemicals typically utilize non-halogenated substances, such as aluminum hydroxide, magnesium hydroxide, phosphorus-based compounds, and zinc-based compounds. These flame retardants are used in a wide range of industries, including construction, automotive, electronics, and electrical wiring, to enhance the fire resistance of products. As a safer alternative to traditional halogenated flame retardants, halogen-free products provide benefits including reduced environmental impact and improved health safety profiles.</p><p>
