# Polyurethane Flexible Foams Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Polyurethane flexible foams are highly versatile materials widely used across multiple industries, including furniture, bedding, and automotive sectors. These foams are known for their lightweight nature, durability, and comfort-enhancing properties, making them a preferred choice for cushioning applications. Manufactured through a chemical reaction involving polyols and diisocyanates, polyurethane flexible foams are available in polyether and polyester types, each with distinct characteristics suitable for different applications.</p><p>
