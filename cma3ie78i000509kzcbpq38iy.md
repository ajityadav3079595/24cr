</p><p>High Purity Semiconductor Grade Solvents are highly refined chemical solutions used in the manufacturing of semiconductors and electronic components. These solvents are characterized by their extremely low contamination levels, with metallic impurities often controlled at parts per billion (ppb) or even parts per trillion (ppt) levels.</p><p>
</p><p>These solvents undergo multiple purification processes including distillation, filtration, and chemical treatment to meet the stringent purity requirements of semiconductor fabrication. They are crucial in applications such as photolithography, wafer cleaning, etching, and stripping of photoresist.</p><p>
</p><p>The two primary product categories include Functional Chemicals and Ultra High Purity Reagents, each serving specific roles in the microelectronics value chain. Functional chemicals are often used in less critical steps, while ultra-high purity reagents are essential in the most sensitive fabrication stages.</p><p>
