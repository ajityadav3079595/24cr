</p><p>PTC (positive temperature coefficient) resettable devices are thermally sensitive electronic components that protect circuits from overcurrent conditions. These polymer-based or ceramic-based devices exhibit a sharp increase in resistance when temperatures exceed a threshold, effectively limiting current flow. Unlike traditional fuses, PTC devices automatically reset when power is removed and the temperature drops below the trip point, making them ideal for applications requiring repeated protection cycles.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is driven by expanding electronics manufacturing in Vietnam and Thailand, coupled with increasing automotive electrification across the region. The rising adoption of Industry 4.0 technologies and 5G infrastructure deployment further accelerates demand for circuit protection solutions.
