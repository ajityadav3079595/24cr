</p><p>Dicaprylyl Carbonate is a synthetic or plant-derived diester of caprylyl alcohol with carbonic acid, widely used as a silicone alternative in cosmetic formulations. This ingredient serves multiple functions: acting as a solvent for even dispersion, forming a moisture-retentive film on the skin, and providing a velvety texture with excellent spreadability. Its unique chemical structure with alkyl chains makes it particularly effective for binding fatty substances, leading to widespread adoption in premium skincare applications.</p><p>
