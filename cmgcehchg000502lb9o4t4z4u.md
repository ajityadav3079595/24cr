</p><p><strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Industrial Heat-resistant ABS (HH ABS) is a grade of ABS known for its superior heat resistance, shock resistance, and workability. With the second highest strength and second lowest ductility among ABS variants, HH ABS finds extensive applications in automotive parts and electrical devices.</p><p>
</p><p>Market Dynamics</p><p>
</p><p>Drivers: </p><p>
</p><p>1. Increasing demand for heat-resistant materials in automotive and electronics industries due to stringent regulatory standards.</p><p>
</p><p>2. Growing adoption of HH ABS in architectural applications for its durability and heat resistance.</p><p>
</p><p>3. Technological advancements enhancing the properties of HH ABS, expanding its application scope.</p><p>
