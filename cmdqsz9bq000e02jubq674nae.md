<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Raw Material Supply Volatility Impacts Production Consistency</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The diatomite filter aid market faces significant challenges from inconsistent raw material supplies. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Competition from Synthetic Alternatives Restricts Market Penetration</strong></p><p>

</p><p>Diatomite filtration systems face operational challenges in high-throughput environments. These limitations are particularly problematic for Southeast Asia's growing petrochemical sector, where continuous processing is essential for economic viability.</p><p>
