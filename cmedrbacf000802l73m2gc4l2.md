# Pressure Sensitive Adhesives Tape Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Pressure-sensitive adhesive (PSA), also known as self-adhesive or self-stick adhesive, is a nonreactive adhesive that forms a bond when pressure is applied to attach it to a surface. Unlike other adhesives, it does not require solvents, water, or heat for activation. PSAs are widely used in various industries due to their ease of application and strong adhesion properties.</p><p>
