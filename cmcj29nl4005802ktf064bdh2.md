</p><p><strong>Drivers:</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Healthcare Industry Demand:</strong> Glutaraldehyde is extensively utilized as a high-level disinfectant and chemical sterilant, particularly in medical and dental applications where its efficacy against a wide range of pathogens, including spores, is critical.
