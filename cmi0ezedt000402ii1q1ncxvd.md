</p><p>Copper tungsten (CuW) is a composite material combining the thermal conductivity of copper with the high melting point and low thermal expansion of tungsten. This unique alloy finds extensive use in aerospace applications, particularly in high-temperature components such as rocket nozzles, thermal management systems, and aircraft electrical contacts. The material composition typically ranges from 10-50% copper content, with common grades including WCu 70/30 and WCu 80/20 for optimal performance in extreme environments.</p><p>
