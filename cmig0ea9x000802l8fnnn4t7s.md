These naturally occurring chemicals enable precise insect control by disrupting mating patterns (mating disruption), attracting pests to traps (mass trapping), or monitoring infestations (detection &amp; monitoring). The two primary types include sex pheromones (representing 62% market share in 2020) and aggregation pheromones (38% share), according to industry data.</p><p>
