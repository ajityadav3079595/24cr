# FEP Heat Shrink Medical Tubing Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>FEP heat-shrinkable tubes are known for their exceptional abrasion resistance, chemical resistance, and high-temperature performance. These tubes are primarily used in medical applications, such as microcatheters, due to their moisture-proof properties. The ability of FEP to be extruded with thin walls makes it suitable for surgical equipment and microcatheters, further enhancing its utility in the medical industry.</p><p>
