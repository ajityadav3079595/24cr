</p><p>Fusion Bonded Epoxy Powder Coating is a thermoset polymer coating applied through electrostatic spraying, forming a durable protective layer when heat-cured. This technology provides superior corrosion resistance for steel pipelines, reinforcing bars, valves, and industrial equipment across multiple sectors. The coating's molecular structure creates a chemically bonded protective barrier that withstands extreme temperatures, abrasion, and chemical exposure.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth stems from expanding pipeline infrastructure projects worldwide and increasing regulatory requirements for corrosion protection. <strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>

</p><p>The FBE coating industry faces significant margin pressure from fluctuating epoxy resin costs, which account for approximately 35% of total production expenses. The industry must balance performance requirements with environmental mandates, often necessitating costly testing and certification processes that can delay product launches by 12-18 months.</p><p>
