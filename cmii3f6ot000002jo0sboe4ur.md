</p><p>Corrosion resistant epoxy primers are specialized protective coatings designed to prevent substrate deterioration from environmental and chemical exposure. These two-component systems combine epoxy resins with polyamine hardeners to create a durable barrier against moisture, salt spray, and industrial chemicals. They are widely used as undercoats in multi-layer protective systems across industries requiring long-term asset preservation.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing infrastructure investments, stricter environmental regulations for asset protection, and rising demand from the oil &amp; gas sector.
