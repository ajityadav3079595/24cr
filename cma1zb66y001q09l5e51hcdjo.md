# 1,3-Hexafluorobutadiene (C4F6) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>1,3-Hexafluorobutadiene (C4F6) is an organofluorine compound with the chemical formula (CF2=CF)2. This colorless gas is utilized as an etchant in microelectronics and is the perfluoroanalogue of butadiene. It can be synthesized by coupling C2 compounds such as chlorotrifluoroethylene or bromotrifluoroethylene.</p><p>
