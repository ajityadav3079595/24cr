</p><p>Rare earth butadiene rubber, also known as neodymium series butadiene rubber, is a type of butadiene rubber polymerized by the catalytic system of rare earth neodymium metal. This rubber is used to manufacture tires, enhancing their quality and performance significantly.</p><p>
</p><p>Rare earth butadiene rubber, or neodymium series butadiene rubber, is a type of butadiene rubber polymerized using a rare earth neodymium metal catalytic system. It is characterized by its high strength, flexion resistance, low heat generation, wet slip resistance, and low rolling resistance. NdBR is preferred for high-performance and energy-saving tires due to its superior performance compared to nickel rubber.</p><p>
