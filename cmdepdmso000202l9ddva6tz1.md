</p><p>Glass frit and paste are materials formed by grinding glass into a fine powder, adding a binder to create a paste, and incorporating a fluxing medium to aid the melting process. The resulting products are extensively used for sealing, bonding, and coating applications, especially in the electronics industry. Glass frit is a critical component in the production of electronic components, such as semiconductors, circuit boards, and display devices, while glass paste is commonly used for the production of solar cells and fuel cells. These materials are designed to withstand high temperatures and harsh conditions, making them essential for components that must endure extreme operating environments.</p><p>
