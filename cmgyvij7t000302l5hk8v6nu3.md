<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Growing Demand for Natural and Sustainable Ingredients in Personal Care Products</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The global personal care industry is witnessing a paradigm shift toward natural and sustainable ingredients, significantly driving sarcosine demand. As consumers become increasingly conscious of product formulations, manufacturers are replacing synthetic surfactants with amino acid-based alternatives like sarcosine. The mild cleansing properties of sarcosine, combined with its moisturizing benefits, make it ideal for sensitive skin formulations. Major food manufacturers are increasingly incorporating sarcosine into new product development pipelines to meet the 22% annual growth in demand for plant-based proteins.</p><p>
</p><p><strong>The Asia-Pacific region currently leads sarcosine consumption, accounting for nearly 42% of global demand, driven by booming personal care industries in China and India.</strong></p><p>

Such fluctuations make long-term contract pricing challenging, leading some manufacturers to maintain lower inventory levels, which can disrupt supply chains during periods of peak demand.</p><p>
