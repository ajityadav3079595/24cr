<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>

Manufacturers are challenged to balance purity requirements with cost efficiency, particularly as competitors develop lower-cost alternatives with improving performance characteristics.</p><p>

</p><p>Barium fluoride's hygroscopic nature presents handling and storage challenges. The material absorbs moisture from the atmosphere, which can degrade optical performance if not properly managed. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Regulatory Compliance Burden</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Environmental and workplace safety regulations governing barium compounds create additional compliance costs.
