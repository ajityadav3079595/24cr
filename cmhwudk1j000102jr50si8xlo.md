</p><p>Perfluorinated type plastic optical fibers (PF-POF) are specialized polymer-based fibers with high optical clarity and low signal attenuation, primarily used for short-distance data transmission. These fibers leverage perfluorinated polymers to achieve superior light transmission properties compared to conventional plastic optical fibers, making them ideal for applications requiring high bandwidth and resistance to environmental factors.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand in industrial automation, automotive infotainment systems, and home networking solutions.
