# FRP Bridge Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>The global FRP (Fiber-Reinforced Polymer) Bridge market represents a segment within the larger composite material industry, focusing on the use of advanced fiber-based materials in the construction of bridges. FRP composites are made by combining fibers, such as glass or carbon, with a resin matrix, which is typically a type of polymer, to form a material that is both lightweight and extremely durable. These materials offer numerous advantages over traditional construction materials like concrete and steel, particularly in terms of corrosion resistance, high strength-to-weight ratio, and ease of installation.</p><p>
</p><p>FRP bridges are increasingly being used for infrastructure projects, especially in environments where conventional materials would degrade quickly due to exposure to harsh conditions.
