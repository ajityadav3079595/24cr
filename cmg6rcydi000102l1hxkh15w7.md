# 1-Deoxynojirimycin Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032, by Manufacturers, Regions, Technology, Application

It is primarily derived from mulberry leaves and is widely recognized for its potential health benefits, particularly in blood sugar regulation. DNJ finds applications in various industries, including food and beverage, dietary supplements, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and animal feed.
