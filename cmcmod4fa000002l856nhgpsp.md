# PEG Stearate Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>PEG Stearate is a surfactant produced by reacting polyethylene glycol (PEG) with stearic acid. PEG forms the hydrophilic component, while stearic acid acts as the lipophilic element, making it an excellent emulsifier. PEG Stearates are white to tan soft waxy solids commonly used in cosmetic and personal care products, such as skin creams, conditioners, shampoos, body cleansers, and soap-free detergents.</p><p>
