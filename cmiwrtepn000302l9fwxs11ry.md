</p><p>Sorbitan Oleate is a lipophilic nonionic surfactant derived from the esterification of oleic acid with sorbitol. Primarily functioning as an emulsifier, it plays a critical role in stabilizing formulations across cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food industries. Its unique molecular structure enables the formation of both water-in-oil and oil-in-water emulsions, depending on formulation requirements.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by expanding personal care industries and increasing demand for multifunctional ingredients in cosmetic formulations. With the rise of clean-label products, manufacturers are prioritizing Sorbitan Oleate due to its plant-derived origin and excellent safety profile.
