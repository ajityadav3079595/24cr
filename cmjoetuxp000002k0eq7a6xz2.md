Its high smoke point makes it suitable for cooking and frying, while its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties make it valuable in cosmetics and skincare applications. <strong>MARKET CHALLENGES</strong></p><p>

</p><p>The Camellia Oil market faces significant challenges from its inherently constrained supply chain. This seasonality often leads to price volatility, with premium grades seeing price fluctuations of up to 30% between harvest and off-season periods.</p><p>

</p><p><strong>Quality Control Issues</strong></p><p>
