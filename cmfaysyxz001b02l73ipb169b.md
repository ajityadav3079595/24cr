<strong>Global Integrated Circuit Etchants Market: Market Segmentation Analysis</strong></p><p>
</p><p>In the electronics industry, the etching solution chemicals are varied depending on the material to be etched. For example, to perform etching of silicon (Si), a mixture of Dry Etching Agent (electronic grade) and nitric acid (electronic grade) is used. Other compounds are also added in the mixture to control the etch rate, the etch selectivity, and the etch anisotropy. The optimal etchant composition depends on the substrate and resist materials as well as the etching method.</p><p>
