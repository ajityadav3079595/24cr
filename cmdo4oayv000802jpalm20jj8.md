</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Production Costs and Raw Material Challenges to Limit Market Penetration</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Ceramic GOS scintillator manufacturing requires specialized high-temperature sintering equipment and strict process controls, resulting in production costs 20-30% higher than traditional scintillator materials. These cost factors make ceramic scintillators prohibitive for many price-sensitive applications, limiting their adoption in certain market segments. Production scale remains another challenge, with current manufacturing capacity struggling to meet the rapidly growing demand from medical and security sectors.</p><p>
