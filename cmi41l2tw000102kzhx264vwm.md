</p><p>Ceramic epoxy coatings are advanced protective coatings that combine the durability of ceramics with the adhesive properties of epoxy resins. These coatings are widely used to protect surfaces from corrosion, abrasion, and chemical exposure. They are applied in various forms, including solventless, thick paste, and other specialized formulations. The primary function of ceramic epoxy coatings is to extend the lifespan of substrates such as metals, fabrics, and plastics by providing a robust protective layer.
