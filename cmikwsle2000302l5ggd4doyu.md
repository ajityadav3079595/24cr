This specialty chemical plays a crucial role in modifying polymer properties by introducing reactive methylol groups, which enable covalent bonding between polymer chains during curing processes. The compound finds extensive application in adhesives, coatings, textiles, and paper treatment due to its ability to enhance durability, water resistance, and mechanical strength.</p><p>
