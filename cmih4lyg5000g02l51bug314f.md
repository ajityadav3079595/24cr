</p><p>Solar wafer cutting fluid is a specialized chemical solution used in photovoltaic manufacturing to facilitate precision cutting of silicon wafers. These fluids serve multiple critical functions: cooling the cutting wire, removing silicon debris, reducing friction between wire and wafer, and preventing micro-cracks during the diamond wire sawing process. The composition typically includes polyethylene glycol (PEG) as base fluid along with surfactants, corrosion inhibitors, and stabilizers.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is being driven by Southeast Asia's rapidly expanding solar panel manufacturing capacity, particularly in Malaysia, Vietnam, and Thailand where production increased by 32% collectively in 2023.
