</p><p>The cell cast acrylic sheet market faces significant pressure from fluctuating raw material costs, particularly for methyl methacrylate (MMA) monomers. Over the past two years, MMA prices have experienced double-digit percentage swings due to supply chain disruptions and energy market volatility, creating challenging conditions for long-term planning and contract negotiations.</p><p>

</p><p><strong>Competition from Alternative Materials</strong></p><p>
Polycarbonate and extruded acrylic sheets continue to present formidable competition, particularly in price-sensitive applications. While cell cast acrylic offers superior optical clarity and surface hardness, alternative materials often compete effectively based on impact resistance or lower cost points, limiting market penetration in certain segments.</p><p>
</p><p><strong>Environmental Regulations</strong></p><p>
Stringent environmental policies regarding monomer handling and manufacturing emissions require significant capital investments in production facilities.
