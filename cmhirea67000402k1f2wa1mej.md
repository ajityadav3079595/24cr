</p><p>Rocuronium Bromide is a non-depolarizing neuromuscular blocking agent (NMBA) used clinically as an adjunct to general anesthesia. As an intermediate-acting aminosteroid compound, it works by competitively blocking acetylcholine receptors at the neuromuscular junction. The drug is particularly valued in surgical settings for its rapid onset (within 1-2 minutes) and intermediate duration of action (30-40 minutes), making it ideal for endotracheal intubation and muscle relaxation during procedures.</p><p>
