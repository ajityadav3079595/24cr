</p><p>Polishing &amp; Lapping Films are precision abrasive materials used for surface finishing in semiconductor, optical, and medical device manufacturing. These films consist of abrasive particles uniformly bonded to flexible polyester or polyimide substrates, available in micron-level grit sizes for controlled material removal. Key product types include aluminum oxide, silicon carbide, and diamond-based films, with applications ranging from wafer polishing to fiber optic connector finishing.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand for high-precision components in electronics manufacturing, particularly for 5G infrastructure and advanced semiconductor packaging.
