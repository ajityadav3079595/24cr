</p><p><strong>Expanded Polytetrafluoroethylene (ePTFE)</strong> is a specialized form of PTFE that has been expanded in a controlled process to form a microporous structure. This material retains PTFE's inherent properties such as high chemical resistance, low friction, and excellent thermal stability, while its expanded form offers enhanced permeability, flexibility, and mechanical strength. These attributes make ePTFE an ideal material for a wide range of applications including filtration, medical implants, gaskets, seals, and insulation in the chemical, transportation, and healthcare industries.</p><p>
