# Nonylphenol Market, Global Outlook & Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Nonylphenol is an organic compound derived from phenol with various industrial applications. It is primarily used in the production of nonylphenol ethoxylates (NPEs), which serve as surfactants in detergents, emulsifiers, and industrial cleaners. Additionally, nonylphenol is utilized in the manufacturing of antioxidants, lubricating oil additives, and textile auxiliaries. Due to environmental concerns and regulatory restrictions in some regions, the nonylphenol market has witnessed shifts in production and consumption patterns.</p><p>
