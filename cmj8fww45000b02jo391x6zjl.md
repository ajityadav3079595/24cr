</p><p>Asphaltene inhibitors are specialty chemicals designed to prevent the precipitation and deposition of asphaltenes in crude oil production systems. These formulations work by disrupting the aggregation of asphaltene molecules, maintaining their solubility in crude oil, and preventing flow assurance issues in pipelines, wells, and processing equipment. The product portfolio includes dispersants, stabilizers, and anti-agglomerants with varying chemical compositions tailored to different crude oil characteristics.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing global crude oil production, particularly from mature fields where asphaltene precipitation becomes more prevalent.
