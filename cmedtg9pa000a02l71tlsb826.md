<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Growing Demand for Air Purification Systems to Drive Type B Silica Gel Market</strong></p><p>

Type B silica gel's superior moisture absorption capacity makes it ideal for air purification systems in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.
