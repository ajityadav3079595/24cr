This nitrochlorobenzene derivative serves as a critical precursor for producing herbicides and insecticides that protect crops from pests and diseases. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Expanding Dye Industry Generating Significant Demand</strong></p><p>

</p><p>Additionally, the growing middle-class population with increasing disposable incomes is driving demand for dyed textiles and colored consumer goods. </p><p>Furthermore, obtaining and maintaining environmental clearances has become increasingly complex, with the average approval timeline stretching to 12-18 months for new facilities. Similarly, chlorine prices have been impacted by energy costs and production disruptions.</p><p>
</p><p>This raw material price volatility creates challenges in maintaining stable production costs and profit margins. Many manufacturers face difficulties in passing these cost fluctuations to downstream customers, particularly when dealing with long-term supply contracts.
