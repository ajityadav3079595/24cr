# Holographic Laminates Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Holographic laminated foil is a metal foil made using holographic technology. Its surface is coated with a holographic coating, which can record and reproduce holographic images. Holographic laminated foil has the characteristics of high transmittance, high reflectivity, and high heat resistance, making it widely used in packaging, printing, coating, medicine, water treatment, daily chemicals, and other fields.</p><p>
