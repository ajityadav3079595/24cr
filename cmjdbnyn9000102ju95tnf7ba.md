</p><p>Soft gaskets are compressible mechanical seals designed to fill irregularities between mating surfaces, preventing leakage of fluids or gases under compression. These components are manufactured from materials like <strong>rubber, graphite, PTFE (Teflon), cork, and fiber</strong>, selected based on temperature, pressure, and chemical resistance requirements. The market is segmented into <strong>semi-metallic and non-metallic materials</strong>, with non-metallic variants gaining traction due to their corrosion resistance and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>
</p><p>Key drivers include expansion in power generation infrastructure and stricter emissions regulations requiring reliable sealing solutions.
