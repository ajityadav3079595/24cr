# Post Etch Residue (PER) Cleaners Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>The global <strong style="font-size:13px">Post Etch Residue (PER) Cleaners</strong> market refers to the specialized chemicals or mixtures used to effectively remove post-etch residues from various surfaces, particularly in industries like electronics, semiconductor manufacturing, and materials processing. Post etching residues typically consist of organic contaminants and metal impurities left on the substrate after the via, poly, and metal etch processes.</p><p>
</p><p>These residues can compromise the quality and functionality of the final product, making residue removal essential. Post etch residue cleaners are formulated to efficiently target and eliminate these contaminants, ensuring the substrates are clean and ready for further processing or assembly.
