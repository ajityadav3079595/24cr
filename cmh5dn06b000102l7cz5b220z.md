The product category includes both enzymatic formulations for protein removal and non-enzymatic variants for general decontamination.</p><p>

<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Rising Healthcare-Associated Infections Fueling Market Growth</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The global pretreatment foam market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing prevalence of healthcare-associated infections (HAIs).
