</p><p>Flat back tapes are pressure-sensitive adhesive products featuring a paper-based backing with adhesive applied to one side. These versatile tapes are widely used across industries for packaging, splicing, binding, and other applications due to their clean removal properties and strong adhesion. The market primarily consists of two adhesive types: acrylic-based and rubber-based variants, each offering distinct performance characteristics for different environmental conditions.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is being driven by increasing e-commerce activities requiring secure packaging solutions and the expansion of manufacturing sectors globally.
