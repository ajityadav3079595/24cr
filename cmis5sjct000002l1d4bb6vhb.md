</p><p>Sealing alloys, also known as fixed expansion alloys or felling alloys, are specialized metallic compounds designed to create hermetic seals in electronic and vacuum applications. These alloys maintain dimensional stability across temperature variations while providing excellent corrosion resistance. Common types include iron-nickel (FeNi), iron-nickel-cobalt (FeNiCo), and iron-nickel-chromium (FeNiCr) compositions, each offering unique thermal expansion properties tailored for specific sealing requirements.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand from the electronics sector, where these alloys are essential for semiconductor packaging and sensor manufacturing. While the electric vacuum industry remains a traditional stronghold, emerging applications in aerospace and renewable energy are creating new opportunities. Recent developments include Sumitomo Metal Mining's 2023 expansion of its FeNiCo production capacity to meet growing demand from Asian electronics manufacturers, reflecting the industry's strategic response to market needs.</p><p>
