</p><p>Synthetic Rutile is an upgraded titanium dioxide feedstock produced from ilmenite, used primarily in the production of titanium metal and high-grade titanium dioxide pigments. This demand is further amplified by growing applications in paints, coatings, plastics, and paper industries across Asia-Pacific markets.</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Environmental Regulations Favoring Synthetic Alternatives</strong></p><p>Chinese environmental policies have increasingly restricted ilmenite mining operations due to ecological concerns, creating a strategic shift toward synthetic rutile production.
