</p><p>Low Molecular Weight Polyisobutylene (LMPIB) is a specialized synthetic rubber derivative characterized by its low viscosity and excellent thermal stability. Primarily used as an additive in lubricants, adhesives, sealants, and fuel additives, LMPIB enhances product performance through improved tackiness, oxidation resistance, and shear stability. The material's molecular weight typically ranges between 350 to 5,000 g/mol, making it distinct from high molecular weight variants used in different applications.</p><p>
