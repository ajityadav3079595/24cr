# Hoists and Trolley Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>The <strong>Hoists and Trolley Market</strong> comprises mechanical devices used for lifting, moving, and positioning heavy loads in industrial and commercial settings. Hoists are used to raise or lower a load using a drum or lift wheel, while trolleys help transport the hoist and load horizontally. These devices are commonly employed in manufacturing plants, construction sites, warehouses, and logistics operations to improve efficiency and safety.</p><p>
