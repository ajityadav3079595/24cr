# Flexible Fire Protection Sealants Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Flexible firestop sealants are a specific type of sealants that help prevent the spread of fire by sealing openings to fire-rated interiors of building constructions, floors, ceilings, or wall assemblies. They possess fire-resistant properties with exceptional longevity, reduce the formulation of smoke and harmful toxins, and can act as an acoustic barrier by reducing noise transfer. These sealants are paintable and easily cleaned with water.</p><p>
