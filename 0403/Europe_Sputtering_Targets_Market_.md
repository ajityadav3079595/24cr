These materials play a critical role in the manufacturing of semiconductors, solar panels, flat-panel displays, and optical communication devices. The sputtering process involves bombarding a target material with high-energy ions, causing atoms to be ejected and deposited onto a substrate, forming a thin film with desired properties.</span></p>
