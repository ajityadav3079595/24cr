 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Polyolefin (POF) shrink film is a class of polymer-based shrink wrap produced from olefin-based resins such as polyethylene (PE) and polypropylene (PP). This material is widely used in packaging applications due to its high clarity, durability, and strong shrink performance. POF shrink films are commonly used for packaging consumer goods, food products, pharmaceuticals, and industrial materials. The advantages of POF shrink film include its non-toxic and environmentally friendly composition, high flexibility, puncture resistance, and excellent sealing properties.</span></p>
