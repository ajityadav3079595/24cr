<h1>Technical Grade Tannic Acid Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p><span style="font-size:14px">Technical grade tannic acid is a naturally occurring polyphenol primarily derived from gallnuts, tara pods, and certain tree barks. It is widely used in industrial applications, including corrosion inhibition, leather tanning, ink production, pharmaceuticals, and food additives.
