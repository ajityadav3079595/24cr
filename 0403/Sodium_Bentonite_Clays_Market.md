It is primarily composed of montmorillonite and is widely used in industries such as oil and gas, construction, agriculture, and environmental applications. Its unique ability to form a gel-like consistency when mixed with water makes it ideal for applications like drilling fluids, cat litter, pond sealants, and wastewater treatment.</p>
