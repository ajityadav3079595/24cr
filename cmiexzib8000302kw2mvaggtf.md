<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Production Cost and Technical Complexity Limit Market Penetration</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Despite its advantages, NMMO faces significant barriers to widespread adoption due to its relatively high production costs. Additionally, the compound's hygroscopic nature necessitates expensive handling and storage solutions, further increasing total cost of ownership. These economic factors make price-sensitive industries hesitant to transition from established alternatives, particularly in developing markets where cost remains the primary purchasing criterion.</p><p>

These factors collectively slow market expansion, particularly among small and medium enterprises lacking resources for regulatory compliance.</p><p>
