# Pyrroloquinoline Quinone Acid Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Pyrroloquinoline Quinone (PQQ) Acid is a redox cofactor and a bioactive compound found in bacteria, plants, and mammals. It plays a crucial role in cellular functions such as energy metabolism, antioxidant protection, and neuroprotection. Initially discovered in bacteria, PQQ is recognized for its ability to stimulate mitochondrial biogenesis, making it a promising ingredient in dietary supplements, pharmaceuticals, and functional foods. Given its diverse biological activities, PQQ is widely studied for its potential applications in health and wellness industries.</p><p>
