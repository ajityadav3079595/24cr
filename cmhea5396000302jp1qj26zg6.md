</p><p>Food grade magnesium oxide is a high-purity mineral compound primarily used as an acidity regulator and anti-caking agent in food applications. This GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe) ingredient serves multiple functions including pH adjustment, nutrient fortification, and texture modification in various processed foods and dietary supplements. The product is available in two main forms - light (low bulk density) and heavy (high bulk density) variants - with distinct functional properties for different food applications.</p><p>

</p><p>The global food grade magnesium oxide market is experiencing significant growth driven by increasing consumer awareness about nutritional supplements and fortified foods.
