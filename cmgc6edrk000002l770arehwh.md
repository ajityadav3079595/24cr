</p><p>Cellulose Acetate Butyrate (CAB) is a butyric acid ester of partially acetylated cellulose, characterized by its butyryl content and viscosity as key performance indicators. This versatile material finds applications across coatings, nail care, printing inks, and other specialty segments due to its excellent solubility, toughness, and UV resistance properties.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand for high-performance coatings in automotive and industrial applications, where CAB improves flow properties and durability. </p><p>The coatings industry represents the largest application segment for cellulose acetate butyrate, accounting for over 45% of global CAB consumption. CAB's unique properties including excellent solubility, durability, and UV resistance make it indispensable in high-performance coatings for automotive, marine, and industrial applications. Recent market data indicates that automotive OEM coatings alone consumed approximately 28,000 tons of CAB in 2023, driven by increasing vehicle production and the demand for advanced coating formulations. The shift towards water-based coatings in architectural applications further supports CAB adoption, as it improves flow characteristics and reduces drying times without compromising environmental regulations.</p><p>
