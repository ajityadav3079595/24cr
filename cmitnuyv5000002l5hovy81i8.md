<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Price Volatility of Raw Materials to Challenge Market Stability</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The eucalyptus oil market faces significant challenges due to fluctuating raw material prices and supply chain disruptions. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Regulatory Constraints and Safety Concerns to Limit Expansion</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Stringent regulatory frameworks governing essential oil production and distribution present another significant challenge for market players. Regulatory bodies have implemented strict guidelines regarding purity levels, labeling requirements, and permitted applications, particularly for medicinal uses. The complexity of compliance increases operational costs and creates barriers to entry for smaller producers. Furthermore, potential toxicity concerns when consumed internally or applied undiluted have led to cautious adoption in certain consumer segments.</p><p>
</p><p>Additionally, the lack of standardized production methods across different regions creates quality inconsistency, affecting consumer trust and brand reputation in international markets.</p><p>
