These additives work by inhibiting free radical chain reactions that cause oil thickening, acid formation, and deposit accumulation. Major antioxygen types include phenolic compounds, aromatic amines, and organosulfur derivatives, which protect critical engine components across automotive, industrial, and marine applications.</p><p>
