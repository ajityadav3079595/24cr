# Polyisocyanurate Foam (PIR) Sandwich Panel Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Prefabricated Polyisocyanurate Foam (PIR) sandwich panels are construction materials composed of corrosion-resistant, corrugated steel facings bonded to a core of rigid PIR foam. These panels are widely used in industrial, commercial, and residential applications for roofing insulation and vertical walls. They offer excellent thermal insulation, fire resistance, and durability, making them a preferred choice for warehouses, factories, office buildings, and cold storage facilities.</p><p>
