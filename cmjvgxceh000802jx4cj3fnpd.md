</p><p>The silicone multicore cables market, despite its promising trajectory, grapples with substantial challenges stemming from elevated production expenses. In 2023, silicone prices rose by about 15% globally, impacting profit margins for manufacturers and making these cables less competitive against cheaper alternatives like PVC in cost-sensitive applications. This substitution trend restrains silicone's expansion, especially in non-high-temperature scenarios.</p><p>
</p><p>Additionally, technical limitations in achieving ultra-fine multicore configurations without compromising signal integrity challenge designers in high-density electronics. Scaling production while ensuring uniformity remains difficult, often leading to higher defect rates and production halts.</p><p>
</p><p>The biotechnology industry's rapid growth requires a skilled workforce; however, a shortage of qualified professionals, exacerbated by retirements, further complicates market adoption.
