# Puerariae (Kudzuvine Root) Extract Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Puerariae (Kudzuvine Root) Extract is a natural botanical substance derived from the roots of the Pueraria lobata plant, commonly known as Kudzu. This plant, which is native to East Asia, particularly China and Japan, has been used for centuries in traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) due to its wide range of medicinal properties. The extract is rich in active compounds such as isoflavones, flavonoids, and phytoestrogens, which are believed to provide various health benefits. Puerariae Extract is often used to support cardiovascular health, alleviate menopausal symptoms, and improve liver function. Additionally, it is recognized in the cosmetic industry for its potential to enhance skin health and reduce the visible signs of aging.</p><p>
