<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyketone resin is a high-performance aldehyde resin known for its colorless to slightly yellow appearance. It is insoluble in water but dissolves easily in most organic solvents, making it highly versatile in industrial applications. Polyketone resins exhibit excellent anti-yellowing properties and have remarkable compatibility with raw materials used in paints, coatings, and inks. These properties make them highly sought after in industries requiring durable and stable resins for their formulations.</p><p>
