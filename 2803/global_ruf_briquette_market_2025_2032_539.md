<h1>RUF Briquette Market Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>RUF briquettes are a type of compacted fuel made from biomass materials, which are shaped into a square or rectangular form for easy handling and storage. These briquettes are typically produced by compressing organic materials such as wood chips, sawdust, agricultural waste, or other biomass residues. The RUF briquette model is distinct in its square shape, which allows for efficient stacking and ease of handling. The briquettes are known for their high moisture content compared to other types of biomass briquettes, and they generate more ash during combustion. Despite these factors, their popularity has been growing due to their convenience, environmental benefits, and ability to be made from a wide variety of raw materials.</p><p>
