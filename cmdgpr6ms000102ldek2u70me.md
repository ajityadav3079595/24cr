<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyester polyols are key components in the production of polyurethane materials. They are synthesized through the condensation reaction of a glycol with a dicarboxylic acid. These polyols exhibit superior mechanical properties, chemical resistance, and hydrolytic stability, making them ideal for various applications such as rigid and flexible foams, coatings, adhesives, elastomers, and sealants. The versatility and efficiency of polyester polyols have driven their demand across multiple industrial sectors, positioning them as a crucial element in polymer science and advanced material manufacturing.</p><p>
