Many textile-producing countries are implementing stricter effluent treatment regulations, requiring substantial investments in processing infrastructure. In regions where treatment facilities are inadequate, these environmental concerns can lead to operational restrictions and increased compliance costs for dye manufacturers and users alike.</p><p>
