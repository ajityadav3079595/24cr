</p><p>Polybutene-1 (PB-1) is a high-performance polyolefin polymer produced through the polymerization of 1-butene using Ziegler-Natta catalysts. This versatile material combines the processing advantages of conventional plastics with superior mechanical properties, including high creep resistance, thermal stability, and excellent stress cracking resistance. PB-1 exists in three primary grades - extrusion, injection molding, and blow molding - each tailored for specific industrial applications.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand from the plumbing industry, where PB-1 pipes demonstrate exceptional durability and flexibility. While Asia-Pacific currently dominates consumption, emerging applications in hot melt adhesives and compounding are creating new growth opportunities across North America and Europe.
