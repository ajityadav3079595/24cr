</p><p>Abrasion Resistant Steel is a <strong>carbon steel alloy</strong> specifically designed to resist wear and tear in abrasive environments. Characterized by its hardness and durability, its performance is typically assessed based on <strong>indentation hardness</strong>, notably using Brinell Hardness values (HBW). This makes it a preferred choice for equipment such as dump trucks, bulldozers, crushers, and conveyor systems.</p><p>
