<strong>Growing Demand for Antioxidant Supplements Drives Market Expansion</strong></p><p>

This trend is particularly significant as global diabetes prevalence is expected to affect <strong>783 million adults by 2045</strong>, creating substantial demand for adjunct therapies.</p><p>

Market leaders are increasingly combining ALA with hyaluronic acid and peptides in premium serums, with the global cosmeceuticals market projected to exceed <strong>$85 billion by 2028</strong>. South Korea's beauty industry, known for early adoption of bioactive ingredients, currently accounts for <strong>22% of Asia-Pacific's ALA demand</strong> in cosmetic applications.</p><p>
