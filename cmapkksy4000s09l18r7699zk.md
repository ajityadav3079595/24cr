</p><p>Rosemary Extract Products refer to a category of natural ingredients derived from the rosemary plant (Rosmarinus officinalis), primarily used for their antioxidant, antimicrobial, and preservative properties. These extracts are rich in compounds such as carnosic acid, carnosol, and rosmarinic acid, which contribute to their effectiveness in extending shelf life and enhancing the safety and stability of food, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical products. Rosemary extracts are typically produced through solvent extraction, supercritical fluid extraction, or steam distillation methods, and are available in liquid, powder, or oil-soluble forms.</p><p>
</p><p>The global Rosemary Extract Products market encompasses a wide range of industries, with primary demand stemming from the food and beverage sector, where these extracts are used as clean-label preservatives. Beyond food, rosemary extracts also find extensive application in personal care, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical industries due to their natural bioactive compounds. This aligns with the growing consumer preference for natural and health-promoting ingredients in everyday products.</p><p>
