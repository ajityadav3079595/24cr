	</p><p><strong>Technological Advancements:</strong> The integration of advanced technologies such as IoT, smart sensors, and mobile app connectivity is a key driver. </p><p><strong>Restraints:</strong></p><p>

</p><p><strong>Challenges:</strong></p><p>

	</p><p><strong>Market Competition:</strong> As more companies enter the market, the competition increases, leading to price wars and a need for constant innovation. New entrants may struggle to differentiate themselves from established players with strong brand recognition.</p><p><strong>Consumer Education:</strong> The complex technological aspects of smart feeding bottles may require additional education and awareness for consumers to fully understand the value proposition. This may slow down market adoption rates.</p><p>
