This broad-spectrum herbicide is widely used for post-emergence control of broadleaf weeds in multiple crops including soybeans, peanuts, and cereals. The compound works by inhibiting protoporphyrinogen oxidase (PPO), disrupting chlorophyll production in target weeds.</p><p>
