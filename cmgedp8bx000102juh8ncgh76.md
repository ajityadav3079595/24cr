</p><p>Metal strips are thin, flat pieces of metal produced through rolling or extrusion processes, primarily used in industrial and manufacturing applications. These strips are available in materials such as aluminum, iron, and copper, each offering distinct properties like corrosion resistance (aluminum), high tensile strength (iron), or superior electrical conductivity (copper). Their applications span critical sectors, including automotive body components, medical devices, and electronics circuitry.</p><p>
