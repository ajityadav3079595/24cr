# Powdered Defoamer Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Powdered defoamers are chemical additives designed to control or eliminate foam in industrial processes. These defoamers are widely used in industries such as papermaking, textile printing and dyeing, petrochemicals, construction, cleaning agents, printing ink, and wastewater treatment. Their ability to provide long-lasting defoaming effects at low concentrations makes them a preferred choice for various applications.</p><p>
