# Refrigerant R32 Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Refrigerant R32, also known as difluoromethane, is a type of halogenated hydrocarbon used in cooling systems. R32 is considered an environmentally friendlier alternative to traditional refrigerants due to its low ozone depletion potential (ODP) and moderate global warming potential (GWP). This refrigerant operates as a gas at room temperature but becomes a colorless, transparent liquid under pressure. It is soluble in oils, and its properties—like high thermal conductivity and low viscosity—make it a preferred choice in air conditioning and refrigeration applications. R32’s flammability, however, poses safety concerns, requiring careful handling and appropriate safety measures during its use and maintenance.</p><p>
