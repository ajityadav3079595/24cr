</p><p>Interior wall putty powder is a critical construction material used for smoothing and finishing walls before painting. It enhances surface durability and aesthetics by filling cracks, leveling uneven surfaces, and improving paint adhesion. The two primary types are <strong>cement-based putty</strong>, known for its moisture resistance, and <strong>gypsum-based putty</strong>, preferred for its smooth finish and quick drying properties.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing urbanization, rising demand for residential and commercial construction, and the growing focus on sustainable building materials. The <strong>Asia Pacific</strong> region dominates the market with over 50% share due to rapid infrastructure development in countries like China and India. Key players such as <strong>Birla White, J.K. Cement Ltd,</strong> and <strong>Nippon Paint</strong> collectively hold more than 30% of the global market share. Recent innovations include eco-friendly formulations to meet stringent environmental regulations and improve indoor air quality.</p><p>
