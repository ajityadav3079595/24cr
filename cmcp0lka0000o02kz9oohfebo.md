</p><p>Gynostemma Pentaphyllum Extract is a botanical supplement derived from the leaves of the Gynostemma pentaphyllum plant, often referred to as "Jiaogulan" in traditional Chinese medicine. The extract is typically processed into powder or capsule form and is consumed as a dietary supplement or incorporated into functional foods and beverages.</p><p>
</p><p>Traditionally used to enhance longevity and vitality, Gynostemma extract contains active compounds known as gypenosides, which are similar in structure and function to ginsenosides found in ginseng. It is often utilized in herbal medicine practices to support cardiovascular health, boost immune function, manage stress, and improve metabolic activity. With increasing consumer demand for plant-based wellness solutions, this extract has found a growing market across health supplements and pharmaceutical industries.</p><p>
