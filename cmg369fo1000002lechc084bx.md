<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>

</p><p>Divergent international regulations regarding byproduct classification and allowable residue applications present substantial barriers to market expansion. These regulatory discrepancies increase compliance costs by <strong>12-15%</strong> for companies operating in multiple markets. Additionally, certain countries impose restrictive tariffs on residue imports to protect domestic amino acid producers, creating artificial market barriers that disrupt global supply chains and limit growth potential in price-sensitive regions.</p><p>
