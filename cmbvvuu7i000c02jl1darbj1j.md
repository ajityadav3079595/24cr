</p><p>Antioxidant FS 042, chemically recognized under CAS number 143925-92-2, is a high-performance phenol-based antioxidant widely used in polymer stabilization. This compound plays a critical role in preventing oxidative degradation, which typically occurs due to exposure to heat, light, and oxygen. Oxidative degradation can lead to loss of mechanical properties, discoloration, and eventual material failure. By neutralizing free radicals formed during thermal and photo-oxidation processes, Antioxidant FS 042 significantly enhances the durability and service life of end products.</p><p>
</p><p>Due to its molecular structure, FS 042 is especially effective in stabilizing synthetic rubbers, plastics, and elastomers during both processing and long-term application. This makes it an essential additive in industries such as automotive, packaging, construction, and consumer goods manufacturing. The compound is particularly valued for its non-discoloring properties, low volatility, and compatibility with other additives, including light stabilizers and synergists, further extending its range of use across thermoplastic and thermoset materials.</p><p>
