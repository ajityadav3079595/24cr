</p><p>Industrial release coatings are low-adhesion formulations designed to prevent composite materials from sticking to molds during manufacturing processes. These coatings play a critical role in maintaining product surface quality while protecting mold integrity, particularly in industries requiring precise molding operations. The primary variants include polyalkylene glycols (PAG), polyethylene glycols (PEG), and silicone-based formulations, with silicone dominating nearly 65% of market share due to superior temperature resistance.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is being driven by expanding applications across food packaging, automotive components, and industrial tapes, where these coatings ensure clean product release without residue. The food &amp; beverage sector accounted for 28% of total consumption in 2023, reflecting stringent hygiene requirements. However, environmental regulations are pushing manufacturers toward water-based formulations, creating both challenges and opportunities for innovation.
