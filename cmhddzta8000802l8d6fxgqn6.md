</p><p>Pharmaceutical grade phospholipids are highly purified lipid molecules consisting of a glycerol backbone, two fatty acid chains and a phosphate group. These naturally occurring compounds serve as critical structural components of cell membranes and are extensively used in drug formulation, particularly for liposomal drug delivery systems and injectable medications.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing pharmaceutical R&amp;D expenditure, which reached USD 238 billion globally in 2023, coupled with rising demand for advanced drug delivery systems. <strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Growing Demand for Liposomal Drug Delivery Systems Accelerates Market Expansion</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The pharmaceutical industry is witnessing robust growth in liposomal drug delivery systems, which is driving significant demand for high-purity phospholipids.
