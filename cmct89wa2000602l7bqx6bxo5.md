# PVC-free LVT Flooring Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>PVC-free LVT (Luxury Vinyl Tile) flooring refers to high-performance flooring solutions that do not contain polyvinyl chloride (PVC). Traditional vinyl flooring, including LVT, often contains PVC, which raises environmental and health concerns due to the presence of dioxins, phthalates, and high chlorine content. These elements contribute to pollution, difficulty in recycling, and potential indoor air quality issues.</p><p>
</p><p>PVC-free LVT flooring is designed to be an eco-friendly and sustainable alternative to conventional vinyl flooring. It is made from alternative materials such as mineral-based or bio-based compounds, offering comparable or superior durability, water resistance, and aesthetic appeal.
