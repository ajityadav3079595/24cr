These specialized chemical compounds decompose under heat to generate free radicals, initiating chain-growth polymerization reactions in non-aqueous systems. Major types include <strong>azo nitrile, azo ester,</strong> and <strong>azo amide</strong> initiators, each offering different decomposition temperatures and solubility characteristics.</p><p>
