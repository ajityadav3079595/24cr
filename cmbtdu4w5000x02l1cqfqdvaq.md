Recent technological breakthroughs in silicon-carbon composite anodes are particularly promising, as they require precisely engineered carbon materials to accommodate volume changes during charging cycles.</p><p>

Battery manufacturers are increasingly testing these alternatives, particularly for premium applications where performance outweighs cost considerations.</p><p>
