</p><p>PVC foam is a lightweight, durable, and versatile material engineered through a chemical foaming process that creates a closed-cell structure. This high-performance material exhibits excellent properties including moisture resistance, chemical stability, and thermal insulation, making it suitable for diverse industrial applications. PVC foam products are classified into multiple types such as decorative boards, skinning boards, closed-cell foams, Celuka boards, and wood-plastic composite (WPC) boards, each serving specific structural and aesthetic requirements.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand from the building &amp; construction sector, where PVC foam is widely used for wall cladding, false ceilings, and signage applications due to its cost-effectiveness and weather resistance. Meanwhile, the aerospace industry's stringent weight reduction requirements are creating new opportunities for high-performance foam solutions. Recent innovations in fire-retardant and anti-microbial PVC foam formulations are further expanding potential applications in healthcare and transportation sectors.</p><p>
