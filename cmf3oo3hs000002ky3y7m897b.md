<p>Global PVC stabilizer market was valued at <strong>USD 3.22 billion in 2024</strong> and is projected to reach <strong>USD 4.14 billion by 2032</strong>, growing at a <strong>compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 3.2%</strong> during the forecast period from 2025 to 2032. </p><p>PVC stabilizers are chemical additives used to prevent the degradation of polyvinyl chloride (PVC) during processing and use. They enhance the thermal stability of PVC by preventing discoloration and maintaining mechanical properties when exposed to heat, light, or mechanical stress.
