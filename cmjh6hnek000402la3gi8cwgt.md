</p><p>Liquid fibrin sealants are biological adhesives derived from human plasma, used as hemostatic agents and surgical adhesives in various medical procedures. These sealants mimic the final stages of the blood coagulation cascade, forming a fibrin clot that aids in tissue sealing and wound healing. The market includes different product types such as 2mL/Set, 4mL/Set, and 10mL/Set formulations, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p><p>
</p><p>Key applications driving market growth include general surgery, cardiovascular surgery, wound management, and orthopedic surgery, with the latter segment showing particularly strong demand due to increasing joint replacement procedures. North America currently dominates both production and consumption, though Asia-Pacific is emerging as a high-growth region. Leading players like Baxter, Johnson &amp; Johnson (Ethicon), and CSL Limited are expanding their product portfolios through strategic partnerships and technological advancements in sealant formulations.</p><p>
