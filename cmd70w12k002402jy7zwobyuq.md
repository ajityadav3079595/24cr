</p><p>PPR Antibacterial Tubes are a specialized form of polypropylene random copolymer (PPR) piping systems integrated with antibacterial agents. These pipes are engineered to inhibit the growth and reproduction of bacteria and other microorganisms on their internal surfaces over time. They are primarily used in applications where hygiene and health are of utmost importance, such as in hospitals, schools, residential buildings, and food processing units.</p><p>
</p><p>These pipes are valued for their non-toxic nature, heat and pressure resistance, and long operational life. The antibacterial feature is generally achieved through the incorporation of natural, inorganic, or organic antibacterial agents into the polymer matrix during the manufacturing process. This innovative combination makes them a preferred solution for potable water distribution, HVAC systems, and sanitation networks.</p><p>
