</p><p>Refined Anthracite Filters are filtration materials primarily used for turbidity and suspended solids (SS) removal in water purification processes. These filters have a lighter specific gravity compared to filter sand and are often combined with it in dual-media filtration systems. Their high carbon content provides excellent resistance to various chemicals, making them ideal for city and industrial water purification applications. Because of this high physical strength, they form reverse grain layers after backwashing, enhancing filtration efficiency.</p><p>
