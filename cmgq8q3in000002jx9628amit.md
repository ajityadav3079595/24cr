These products create barriers that absorb or block sound waves, enhancing acoustic comfort in homes. Common applications include walls, ceilings, floors, and windows, utilizing materials such as mineral wool, fiberglass, and foamed plastics. </p><p>The market is benefiting from significant innovations in acoustic insulation materials. This trade-off between space and acoustic comfort leads many homeowners to compromise on insulation quality, particularly in rental properties where tenants lack control over building modifications.</p><p>
