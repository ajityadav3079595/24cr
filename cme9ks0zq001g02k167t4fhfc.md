# Hypericin Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Hypericin is a natural compound found in the flowering plant Hypericum perforatum, commonly known as St. John's Wort. It is a red pigment with photodynamic and antiviral properties. Hypericin has been extensively studied for its potential therapeutic applications, including as an antidepressant, antiviral agent, and anticancer compound. It interacts with various biological mechanisms and has shown promising results in laboratory studies for inhibiting viral replication and inducing apoptosis in cancer cells.</p><p>
