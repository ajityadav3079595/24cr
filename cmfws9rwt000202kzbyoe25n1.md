In 2023, total consumption reached 85,000 metric tons, with Thailand, Vietnam, and Indonesia accounting for 65% of regional demand. Decabromodiphenyl ether (DecaBDE) alternatives lead the market with a 45% share, following the phase-out of DecaBDE. The market saw a 10% increase in demand for polymeric brominated flame retardants in 2023, reflecting a shift towards more environmentally acceptable options. Tetrabromobisphenol A (TBBPA) is growing at 3% annually, primarily used in printed circuit boards. The trend towards sustainable flame retardants led to a 15% rise in R&amp;D investments in novel, eco-friendly brominated compounds in 2023. Flame retardant synergists saw a 12% surge in adoption, allowing for reduced overall flame retardant loading. The market is facing regulatory challenges, with a 20% increase in demand for alternative flame retardant technologies in certain applications due to environmental concerns.</p><p>
