</p><p><strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Organic Linseed Oil, also known as flaxseed oil, is a natural oil derived from the dried, ripened seeds of the flax plant (Linum usitatissimum). This oil is obtained primarily through cold pressing, ensuring its nutrient-rich composition remains intact. Flaxseed oil is widely recognized for its high content of alpha-linolenic acid (ALA), an essential omega-3 fatty acid. Due to its numerous health benefits, organic linseed oil finds applications in the food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries, among others.</p><p>
</p><p>
