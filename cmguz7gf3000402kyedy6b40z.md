</p><p>3D printer filament materials are thermoplastic polymers used as feedstock for fused deposition modeling (FDM) printers. These materials are extruded into precise diameters (typically 1.75mm or 2.85mm) and enable layer-by-layer creation of three-dimensional objects. The market primarily consists of ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene), PLA (Polylactic Acid), PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), along with specialty composites that offer enhanced properties like conductivity or flexibility.</p><p>
