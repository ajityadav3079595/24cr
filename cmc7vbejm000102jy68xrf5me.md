# Antioxidant TH-CPL Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032, by Manufacturers, Regions, Technology, Application

<p>Antioxidant TH-CPL (Trihydroxy-Chloropropyl Lactone) is a specialized chemical used in the production of polymers and elastomers. It is primarily employed in the rubber, plastic, and latex industries for its ability to increase material resistance to oxidation, heat, and light. This antioxidant enhances product durability, which is essential for manufacturing high-performance materials with extended lifespan, particularly in automotive, construction, and industrial applications.</p><p>
</p><p>Antioxidants like TH-CPL help stabilize materials by preventing degradation caused by environmental factors such as UV exposure, heat, and oxidation.
