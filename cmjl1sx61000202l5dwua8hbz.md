</p><p>Daylight fluorescent pigments are luminescent materials that produce vivid, bright colors by converting ultraviolet light into visible light without requiring external energy sources. These pigments consist of dyed organic polymers specifically formulated as solvents for fluorescent dyes. Unlike conventional pigments, their unique glow effect is achieved through both reflected light and emitted fluorescent light, making them highly visible even in daylight conditions.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand from the printing inks and coatings industries, where these pigments are extensively used for safety applications, advertising, and product differentiation. The Asia-Pacific region dominates global consumption with a 44% market share, followed by Europe at 25%. Major players like Day-Glo Color Corp and SINLOIHI collectively control about 95% of the market, indicating high industry concentration.
