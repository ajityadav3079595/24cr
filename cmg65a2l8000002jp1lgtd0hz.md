</p><p>N-(n-butyl) Thiophosphoric Triamide (NBPT) is a white crystalline or powdered urease inhibitor widely used in agricultural applications. As one of the most effective inhibitors of its kind, NBPT significantly slows down the decomposition of urea into ammonia, thereby extending nitrogen availability for crops. This mechanism helps farmers reduce urea losses while improving fertilizer efficiency and minimizing environmental pollution from nitrogen runoff.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing global demand for high-efficiency fertilizers, particularly in regions with intensive agricultural production. North America currently dominates the market with a 43% share, followed by Europe at 37%.
