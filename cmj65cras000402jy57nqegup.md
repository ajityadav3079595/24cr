</p><p>The podophyllotoxin market faces significant challenges due to rigorous regulatory frameworks governing its production and distribution. Compliance with Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) adds significant overhead, with quality control measures accounting for approximately 30% of production expenses for many manufacturers.</p><p>

</p><p>The podophyllotoxin market faces ongoing challenges related to sourcing the active pharmaceutical ingredient (API), primarily extracted from Podophyllum plant species. Furthermore, environmental regulations in key production regions have limited wild harvesting, while commercial cultivation remains limited to specific geographic areas. This constrained supply situation results in price volatility, with API costs fluctuating by as much as 25% annually, presenting significant challenges for formulation manufacturers and potentially impacting treatment affordability.</p><p>
