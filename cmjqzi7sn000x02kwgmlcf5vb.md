</p><p>Corrugated metal panels are durable construction materials featuring a wavy or ridged profile that enhances structural strength. These panels are manufactured from various metals including steel, aluminum, copper, and zinc, offering benefits such as weather resistance, longevity, and cost-effectiveness. They are widely used in roofing, siding, and exterior wall applications across commercial, industrial, and residential sectors.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing construction activities globally, particularly in emerging economies, coupled with rising demand for sustainable building materials. While steel remains the dominant material segment due to its affordability, aluminum panels are gaining traction because of their lightweight and corrosion-resistant properties.
