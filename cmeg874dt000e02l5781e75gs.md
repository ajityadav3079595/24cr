</p><p>Urethane magnetic wear liners are advanced protective coatings designed for industrial machinery subjected to high wear conditions. These liners combine <strong>polyurethane's exceptional abrasion resistance</strong> with embedded magnets that attract ferrous particles, creating a self-renewing protective layer. This dual-action protection system significantly reduces equipment downtime and maintenance costs in demanding applications such as mining operations, aggregate processing, and material handling systems.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand from the <strong>mining sector</strong>, which accounted for over 45% of total applications in 2024, as operators seek cost-effective solutions to extend equipment lifespan.
