</p><p>Fluoropolymer films, known for their superior thermal resistance, chemical inertness, and electrical insulation properties, are being increasingly adopted in industrial and high-tech applications. These features make them essential in sectors that demand durability, reliability, and minimal maintenance.</p><p>
</p><p>Fluoropolymer films are advanced plastic materials derived from fluoropolymers, such as PTFE, PVDF, FEP, and ETFE, among others. These films offer outstanding thermal stability, low friction, non-stick properties, and resistance to harsh chemicals, UV radiation, and weathering. Their wide-ranging properties make them indispensable across multiple industries including aerospace, automotive, electronics, energy, and packaging.</p><p>
