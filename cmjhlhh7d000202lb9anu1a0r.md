</p><p>(3-Aminopropyl)triethoxysilane (APTES) is a bifunctional organosilane coupling agent widely used in surface modification applications. This chemical compound features both an amino group and triethoxysilane functionality, enabling it to bond organic materials to inorganic surfaces. APTES finds extensive applications in electronics, adhesives, coatings, and nanocomposites where strong interfacial adhesion is required. The compound's ability to enhance mechanical properties and corrosion resistance makes it indispensable in advanced material science.</p><p>
