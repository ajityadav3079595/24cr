</p><p>Maltobionic Acid is a polyhydroxy bionic acid considered the fourth generation of fruit acids. Derived from the fermentation of natural maltose, this compound possesses a unique molecular structure that includes multiple hydroxyl groups. This configuration makes Maltobionic Acid particularly effective in skin care applications, as it enhances skin hydration, promotes collagen synthesis, and improves the overall texture and elasticity of the skin. Its structure also mimics that of lactose acid, making it mild and suitable for sensitive skin. Maltobionic Acid finds prominent usage in facial and neck care, followed by body care, primarily due to its exfoliating properties, ability to retain moisture, and support for skin barrier function</p><p>
