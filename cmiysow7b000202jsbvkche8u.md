</p><p>The global semiconductor market, valued at over $600 billion in 2023, continues to drive significant demand for tungsten foil due to its exceptional thermal and electrical properties. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Renewable Energy Sector Fueling Market Expansion</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Advancements in renewable energy technologies are creating new opportunities for tungsten foil applications. </p><p>Tungsten foil manufacturers face significant challenges due to fluctuating raw material costs, with tungsten concentrate prices experiencing 25-30% annual volatility. <strong>MARKET CHALLENGES</strong></p><p>

</p><p>Advanced material science continues to challenge tungsten foil's market position in several applications. Even in radiation shielding, composites incorporating bismuth or tin increasingly provide viable alternatives with lower density.</p><p>
