</p><p>Ethylene glycol solutions are chemical compounds primarily used as heat transfer fluids and antifreeze agents. These solutions are critical components in automotive coolants, industrial chillers, and HVAC systems, where they prevent freezing in cold temperatures and overheating in hot conditions. The product exists in several formulations including monoethylene glycol (MEG), diethylene glycol (DEG), and triethylene glycol (TEG), each with distinct industrial applications ranging from polyester fiber production to natural gas dehydration.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing automotive production, expanding HVAC infrastructure, and rising demand for polyethylene terephthalate (PET) packaging.
