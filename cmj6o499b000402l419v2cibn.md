</p><p>Triphenylmethyl Chloride (TrCl) is an organochlorine compound primarily used as a protecting group in organic synthesis. This crystalline solid serves as a key reagent for introducing the trityl (triphenylmethyl) group to protect functional groups like amines, alcohols, and thiols during multi-step chemical reactions. Its stability under basic conditions and easy deprotection under acidic conditions make it particularly valuable in pharmaceutical and biotech applications.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from pharmaceutical manufacturers who require high-purity intermediates for drug development.
