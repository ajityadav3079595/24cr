</p><p>Butanone, commonly referred to as methyl ethyl ketone (MEK), is a colorless, flammable liquid with a sweet odor. It is widely used in various industrial applications as a solvent, primarily due to its ability to dissolve a wide range of substances. Butanone is an important chemical compound in the production of coatings, adhesives, inks, and synthetic leather, making it an integral part of the chemical and manufacturing industries.</p><p>
</p><p>The primary chemical formula for Butanone is C4H8O, and it is characterized by its solubility in both water and organic solvents. This makes it versatile for use in various formulations and products. Butanone's high evaporation rate and strong solvent properties allow it to be used effectively in applications where rapid drying is crucial, such as in paints and coatings. In addition, it is employed as a cleaning agent and in the manufacturing of plastics, rubber, and textiles, among other industries.</p><p>
