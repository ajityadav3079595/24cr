Available in anhydrous and nonahydrate forms, it meets stringent food safety standards set by regulatory bodies like FDA and EFSA.</p><p>

</p><p>The food grade magnesium citrate market faces significant challenges from fluctuating raw material costs, particularly for citric acid and magnesium sources.
