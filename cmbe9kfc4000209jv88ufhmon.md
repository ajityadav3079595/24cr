# Process Adsorbents Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Process adsorbents refer to specialized materials that remove impurities and contaminants from industrial processes through adsorption. These materials, such as activated carbon, silica gel, and molecular sieves, are used in applications like natural gas processing, petroleum refining, and petrochemical production. By selectively trapping unwanted substances, process adsorbents enhance efficiency, product quality, and regulatory compliance in various industries.</p><p>
