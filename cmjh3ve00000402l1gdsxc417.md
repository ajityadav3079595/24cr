# Sodium Chlorite for Textile Application Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>
</p><p><strong>Sodium chlorite</strong> is a versatile chemical compound widely used in various industries, including the textile sector. In the context of <strong>textile applications</strong>, sodium chlorite is primarily employed for <strong>bleaching</strong> and <strong>disinfection</strong> purposes. The chemical works as an oxidizing agent, breaking down stains, dirt, and organic contaminants, thus helping to achieve a cleaner, brighter appearance for fabrics. It is particularly effective in the <strong>bleaching of textiles</strong>, where it is used to whiten fabrics and remove undesirable colors or impurities. Additionally, sodium chlorite plays a critical role in <strong>disinfection</strong>, ensuring that textiles, especially those used in healthcare, maintain hygienic standards.</p><p>
