<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyols are essential chemical components used in the production of polyurethanes, which are widely applied in industries such as automotive, construction, furniture, and packaging. Polyurethanes, formed by the reaction of polyols with diisocyanates, provide flexibility, durability, and insulation properties. These polyols are categorized into two main types: polyether polyols and polyester polyols. Their diverse applications make them indispensable in industrial and consumer markets worldwide.</p><p>
