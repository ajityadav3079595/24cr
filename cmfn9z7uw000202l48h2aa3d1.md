<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Supply Constraints and Seasonal Production Limit Market Expansion</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The cashmere ingredients market faces significant supply-side challenges due to the unique biological and geographical factors governing cashmere goat farming.
