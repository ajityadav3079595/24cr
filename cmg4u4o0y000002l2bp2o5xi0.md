</p><p>Phase Change Thermal Interface Materials are advanced thermal management solutions that undergo controlled phase transitions to optimize heat transfer between surfaces. These materials play a critical role in electronics cooling applications by filling microscopic air gaps and reducing thermal resistance at component interfaces. The product category primarily includes thermal pads and thermal pastes formulated with phase-change properties.</p><p>
</p><p>The market expansion is driven by increasing demand for efficient thermal management in high-power electronics, particularly in semiconductor and automotive applications.
