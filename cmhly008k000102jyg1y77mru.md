<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align-align:center"><strong>High Initial Costs Limit Adoption in Price-Sensitive Markets</strong></p><p>
</p><p>While self-leveling screeds offer long-term cost savings, their upfront material costs remain significantly higher than conventional floor preparation methods. </p><p>The performance advantages of self-leveling screeds are heavily dependent on meticulous substrate preparation, which many contractors underestimate. Proper surface cleaning, priming, and moisture testing add complexity to installation processes. In regions with limited technical expertise, improper application frequently leads to delamination and cracking defects, resulting in warranty claims that negatively impact market perception.</p><p>

Recent launches of specialty screeds with enhanced thermal transfer properties specifically designed for underfloor systems are gaining traction in European and North American markets.</p><p>

</p><p>The construction industry's digital transformation is creating demand for smart flooring solutions. Emerging technologies like IoT-enabled screeds that monitor structural health and moisture levels throughout a building's lifecycle represent the next frontier.
