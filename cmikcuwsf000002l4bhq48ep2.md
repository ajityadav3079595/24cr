</p><p>Corrugated Stainless Steel Tubing (CSST) is a flexible gas piping system used for natural gas and propane distribution in residential, commercial and industrial applications. The product consists of thin-walled stainless steel tubing with an exterior polymer coating, offering superior corrosion resistance and durability compared to traditional black iron piping. CSST systems are categorized into ring-type and spiral-type designs, with key applications including gas appliance hookups, meter sets, and building service lines.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing natural gas infrastructure development and the product's advantages including easier installation, reduced labor costs, and improved seismic performance. While North America currently dominates the market due to widespread adoption in residential construction, Asia-Pacific is emerging as the fastest growing region because of rapid urbanization and gas network expansions in China and India. Recent regulatory approvals for CSST in more jurisdictions and product innovations by manufacturers like Omega Flex and TracPipe are further accelerating market adoption.</p><p>
