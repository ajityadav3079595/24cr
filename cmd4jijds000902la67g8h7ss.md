# Polycarbodiimide Crosslinker Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Polycarbodiimide crosslinkers are a class of chemical compounds primarily used in the creation of durable, high-performance materials by linking polymer chains together. These crosslinkers play a crucial role in improving the mechanical and thermal properties of polymers, particularly in coatings, adhesives, sealants, and elastomers. The polycarbodiimide structure is known for its ability to provide excellent resistance to heat, chemicals, and moisture, making it highly sought after in various industrial applications.</p><p>
</p><p>These crosslinkers are mainly used in industries like automotive, construction, electronics, and aerospace due to their superior durability and versatile bonding capabilities.
