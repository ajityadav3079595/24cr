# Functional Coating Composite Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Functional coating composite material is a new type of material developed on the basis of traditional pressure-sensitive tape products. It is transformed and composited with different types of substrates through precision coating, printing, vacuum sputtering, sintering, and other processes. Functional coating composite materials can be divided into adhesive materials and film materials, both of which are produced through a coating process and widely used in various industrial and civil fields.</p><p>
