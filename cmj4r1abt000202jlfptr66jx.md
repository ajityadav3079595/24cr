</p><p>Pilocarpine Nitrate is a cholinergic agonist derived from the leaves of Pilocarpus plants, primarily used in ophthalmic solutions for treating glaucoma and dry mouth syndrome. As a parasympathomimetic alkaloid, it stimulates muscarinic receptors to increase secretion and induce pupil constriction. The compound exists in both powder and solvent formulations, with applications spanning hospital and clinical settings.</p><p>

As healthcare systems prioritize early detection and intervention, the demand for reliable miotic agents like Pilocarpine Nitrate continues to rise.
