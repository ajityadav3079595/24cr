</p><p>Rockfall nets are specialized protective barriers designed to mitigate risks from falling rocks and debris in mountainous or hilly terrains. These engineered safety systems typically consist of high-strength steel wire mesh or synthetic fiber nets, anchored to slopes through cables and support structures. The nets absorb kinetic energy and redirect falling rocks, preventing damage to infrastructure and loss of life.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is being driven by increasing infrastructure development in mountainous regions and stricter safety regulations for transportation corridors. However, installation complexities in remote locations present operational challenges.
