# Latin America Iron and Steel Slag Market Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Iron and steel slag are metallurgical byproducts generated during the iron and steel production process. These materials possess unique physical and chemical properties, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications. The primary types of slag include <strong>blast furnace slag</strong> (produced during ironmaking) and <strong>steelmaking slag</strong> (generated during steel production). Depending on processing methods, slag can be categorized as granulated slag, air-cooled slag, or expanded slag, each with distinct applications in construction, road building, cement manufacturing, and more.</p><p>
</p><p>Iron and steel slag are gaining attention as sustainable construction materials due to their environmental benefits, including reduced CO2 emissions and resource conservation.
