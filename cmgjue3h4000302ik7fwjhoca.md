</p><p>Heliox refers to a mixture of helium (He) and oxygen (O2), used primarily as a medical breathing gas. This mixture is advantageous in treating conditions that affect normal breathing, especially where airway resistance plays a significant role in difficulty breathing. Heliox is often administered to patients suffering from conditions like chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) or asthma, as the mixture allows for easier air passage through constricted airways compared to regular air.</p><p>
</p><p>Heliox has the distinct advantage of reducing airway resistance because helium, a lighter gas, passes through the airways with less friction, requiring less effort from the patient. This makes it easier to breathe and provides relief in medical scenarios. Additionally, Heliox finds applications in specialized fields such as deep-sea diving and saturation diving, where it helps reduce nitrogen narcosis and the risk of decompression sickness.</p><p>
