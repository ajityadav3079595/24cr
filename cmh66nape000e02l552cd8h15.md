This specialized gelatin acts as a binding medium for light-sensitive silver halide crystals, providing critical properties like viscosity control, swellability, and mechanical strength to photographic materials.</p><p>
</p><p>The market continues to demonstrate resilience despite digital disruption, primarily because of sustained demand from professional photographers, artists, and archival applications where analog quality remains irreplaceable.
