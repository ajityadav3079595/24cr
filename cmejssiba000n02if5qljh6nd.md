</p><p>The Baijiu Lees Resource Utilization market refers to the process of converting the byproducts of Baijiu production—specifically Baijiu lees—into valuable products. Baijiu is a traditional Chinese distilled liquor, and during its production, a residual byproduct known as "Baijiu lees" is generated. These lees are rich in proteins, amino acids, and other nutrients. By utilizing these residual materials, industries can recycle waste, reduce environmental impact, and create useful products such as animal feed, biofuels, bioplastics, and more.</p><p>
</p><p>Baijiu lees resource utilization is a growing industry focusing on sustainability and efficiency. The process involves advanced technologies to extract essential nutrients from the lees or convert organic matter into bioenergy.
