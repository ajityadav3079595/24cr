# Chromium Oxide Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p><strong>Chromium Oxide</strong>, chemically denoted as Cr2O3 and also known as chromium sesquioxide or chromia, is one of the most significant oxides of chromium. Widely recognized as ‘chrome green’ when used as a pigment, it serves a variety of industrial purposes due to its excellent chemical and thermal stability, corrosion resistance, and vibrant green coloration. Applications range from coatings and ceramics to metallurgy and rubber manufacturing, making chromium oxide a critical component across diverse industries.</p><p>
