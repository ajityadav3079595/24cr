</p><p>Hexafluoropropylene (HFP) is a fluorinated olefin widely used as a key intermediate in fluoropolymer production. This colorless, odorless gas serves as a critical building block for high-performance materials like fluororubbers (FKM) and fluoroplastics (PTFE, FEP). Its exceptional chemical resistance and thermal stability make it indispensable in aerospace, automotive, and semiconductor industries.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is fueled by expanding demand for fluoropolymers in green energy applications, particularly in fuel cells and lithium-ion battery components.
