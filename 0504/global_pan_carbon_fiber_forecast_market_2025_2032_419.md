<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based carbon fiber is a high-performance carbon fiber known for its exceptional properties such as high tensile strength, high modulus of elasticity, superior thermal stability, and excellent dimensional stability. The production process involves multiple stages, including precursor fiber preparation, pre-oxidation, carbonization, and graphitization, resulting in high-quality PAN-based carbon fiber composites. These materials find applications in aerospace, automotive, sports equipment, and industrial sectors due to their lightweight and superior strength-to-weight ratio.</p><p>
