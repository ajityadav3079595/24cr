</p><p>LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) Flexible Copper Clad Laminates are high-performance substrates used in flexible printed circuit boards (FPCBs). These materials combine excellent electrical properties, thermal stability, and mechanical flexibility, making them ideal for high-frequency applications in 5G communications, automotive electronics, and wearable devices. The laminates consist of a copper foil bonded to LCP film through specialized adhesion technologies.</p><p>
