Its high purity variants (99.99% and 99.999%) are particularly crucial for precision manufacturing processes.</p><p>

</p><p>Octafluoropropane faces growing regulatory pressure due to its high global warming potential (GWP), estimated at 7,000-9,000 times that of CO2 over a 100-year timeframe. Similarly, potential inclusion in future EPA regulations could constrain U.S. market growth. While exemptions exist for semiconductor and medical uses, these environmental concerns are prompting end-users to evaluate lower-GWP alternatives where technically feasible.</p><p>
