</p><p>2-chloro-1, 4-phenylenediamine is an organic compound primarily used as an intermediate in the synthesis of dye and specialty chemicals. It is characterized by its chlorine-substituted phenyl group and amine functionality, making it highly reactive and suitable for various industrial applications. The compound is predominantly utilized in the production of <strong>azo dye</strong>, which is extensively applied in textiles, plastics, and coatings. Additionally, it serves as a critical building block in engineering plastics, enhancing durability and chemical resistance.
