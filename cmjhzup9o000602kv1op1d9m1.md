<strong>Market Size Overview</strong></p><p>
</p><p>
</p><p>Polycrystalline cubic boron nitride (PCBN) is a synthetic material composed of cubic boron nitride (cBN) grains bonded together using various techniques. It is the second hardest material in the world after diamond, offering exceptional thermal and chemical stability. PCBN is widely used in cutting tools, automotive, aerospace, and heavy machinery industries due to its superior wear resistance and durability.</p><p>

<strong>Restraints</strong></p><p>
