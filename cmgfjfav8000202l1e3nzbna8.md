</p><p>Ground protection mats are temporary surface covers designed to protect the ground from damage caused by heavy machinery, vehicles, or foot traffic. These mats are primarily used in industries where access to sensitive ground areas is necessary but must be done without causing harm to the underlying surface. They are especially useful in protecting grass, soil, and other types of delicate ground cover from compaction or erosion.</p><p>
</p><p>Made from durable materials like high-density polyethylene (HDPE) or fiberglass, these mats are designed to withstand the weight of construction equipment, vehicles, and pedestrian traffic. Their primary purpose is to create a stable surface that is both safe for machinery and workers and minimizes environmental impact.</p><p>
</p><p>Ground protection mats are modular and easy to install, making them ideal for short-term usage during construction, maintenance, or event operations. They are often laid down in high-traffic areas where preserving the integrity of the ground is essential.</p><p>
