<strong>MARKET CHALLENGES</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Material Migration Concerns to Impact Market Acceptance</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Recent studies highlighting potential chemical migration from plastic packaging into food and beverage products have raised consumer concerns, particularly regarding long-term storage applications. While PP is generally regarded as safe, these findings have prompted some brands to explore alternative materials, creating adoption barriers. The industry faces the dual challenge of ensuring absolute material safety while communicating these assurances effectively to end-users.</p><p>
</p><p>Additionally, the technical limitations of recycled PP in closure applications present formulation challenges.
