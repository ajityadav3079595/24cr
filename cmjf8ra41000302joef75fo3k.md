</p><p>The global shift toward renewable energy continues to drive substantial demand for PET foam in wind turbine blade cores. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Lightweighting Trends in Transportation to Fuel Market Expansion</strong></p><p>

Major automakers are now integrating PET foam into door panels, headliners, and battery enclosures, with adoption rates in transportation applications growing at approximately 12% CAGR.</p><p>

</p><p>Increasing environmental regulations and corporate sustainability commitments are pushing industries toward recyclable materials like PET foam. While some companies have established long-term supplier contracts to mitigate risks, smaller producers face margin erosion during periods of price volatility.</p><p>
