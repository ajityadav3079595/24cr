</p><p>Anti-corrosion greases are specialized lubricants formulated with rust inhibitors to protect metal surfaces from degradation caused by moisture, chemicals, and environmental factors. These products form protective barriers on metal components, with formulations varying by viscosity (low, medium, high) and application method. Key product types include calcium sulfonate greases, lithium complex greases, and synthetic-based formulations, each offering distinct performance characteristics for industrial applications.</p><p>
