# Latin America Manual Torque Multiplier Market Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>A manual torque multiplier is a mechanical device designed to increase the torque applied to a fastener without requiring additional effort. These tools are essential in industrial applications where high torque is necessary, but space constraints or operator limitations prevent the use of larger tools. They operate through a system of planetary gears, allowing for precision and efficiency in tightening and loosening bolts.</p><p>
