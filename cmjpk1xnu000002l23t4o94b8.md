These labels serve critical functions including ingredient disclosure, barcoding, and marketing communication across food &amp; beverage, personal care, pharmaceuticals, and household products. Label types range from pressure-sensitive and shrink sleeves to in-mold and wet-glue variants, each offering distinct advantages in durability and visual appeal.</p><p>
