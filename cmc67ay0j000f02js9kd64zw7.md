</p><p>The <strong>global grass reinforcement market</strong> refers to the industry that provides solutions for stabilizing grass surfaces to prevent soil erosion, enhance durability, and improve load-bearing capacity. Key products include <strong>polypropylene turf reinforcement mats, straw/coconut turf reinforcement mats, stitch-bonded turf reinforcement mats, and woven turf reinforcement mats</strong>. These products are widely used in applications such as steep slopes, drainage channels, stormwater ponds, and levees.
