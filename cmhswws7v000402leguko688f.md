<strong>MARKET CHALLENGES</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Fragmented Identity Landscape Creates Monetization Headwinds</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The decline of third-party cookies and mobile ad IDs has created identity resolution challenges that impact SSP effectiveness. With Chrome's full cookie deprecation expected in 2025, publishers face potential CPM declines of 30-50% for non-authenticated traffic. SSPs must navigate competing identity solutions from vendors while building publisher tools for first-party data collection and activation. The technical complexity of maintaining match rates across multiple identity graphs forces many platforms to run parallel infrastructure for different identity schemes, increasing operational costs. Smaller publishers particularly struggle with these transitions, creating a bifurcated market where scale players with first-party data assets gain disproportionate advantage.</p><p>

SSPs bear significant responsibility for maintaining inventory quality while balancing publisher monetization needs. The arms race between fraud detection systems and bad actors forces continuous platform investments that strain R&amp;D budgets.</p><p>
