This advanced wire formulation is primarily used for precise alloying and microalloying applications in steelmaking processes, particularly for producing high-grade steel with enhanced mechanical properties. The product comes in two main variants - rectangular cored wire and round cored wire - each offering distinct advantages in different steel production environments.</p><p>
