# Post Consumer Recycled (PCR) Resins Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Post-consumer recycled (PCR) resins are derived from consumer and industrial plastic waste, including plastic film, bottles, milk jugs, buckets, and industrial packaging materials like IBC totes and poly drums. These materials are reprocessed into high-quality recycled plastic that can be used in the manufacturing of new products, helping to reduce environmental pollution and promote a circular economy.</p><p>
