</p><p>Polyethyleneimine Ethoxylate (PEIE) is a water-soluble polymer derivative formed by ethoxylation of polyethyleneimine. This versatile chemical finds applications as a dispersant, flocculant, and viscosity modifier across multiple industries. The product is commercially available in different ethoxylation grades including PEIE 10, PEIE 20, and PEIE 30, each offering distinct molecular weights and performance characteristics.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from water treatment applications, where PEIE serves as an effective agent for heavy metal removal and sludge dewatering. The coatings industry is another major consumer, utilizing PEIE's exceptional adhesion promotion properties.
