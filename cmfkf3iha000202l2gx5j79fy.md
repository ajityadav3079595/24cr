These products work by forming a protective barrier that blocks moisture and oxygen from reaching the metal substrate. The market includes solvent-based and water-based variants, with solvent-based inhibitors currently holding the larger market share. Key application areas span industrial equipment, transportation infrastructure, and other metal-intensive sectors where corrosion prevention is critical.</p><p>
