</p><p>The <strong>global copper wire mesh market</strong> refers to the production, distribution, and application of woven or welded copper wire meshes used in various industries. These meshes are made from high-quality copper wires, which are interwoven or welded to form a grid-like structure. Copper wire mesh is known for its <strong>electrical conductivity, thermal resistance, and anti-microbial properties</strong>, making it ideal for applications such as RFI shielding, Faraday cages, HVAC systems, and filtration. The market includes different types of meshes, such as plain weave and crimped weave, each designed for specific industrial needs.
