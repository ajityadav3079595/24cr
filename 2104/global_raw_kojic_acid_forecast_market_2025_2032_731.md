<h1>Raw Kojic Acid Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Raw kojic acid is a naturally occurring compound derived from various fungi and used primarily in cosmetic formulations to lighten skin pigmentation. It is produced through fermentation processes, typically from substrates such as rice and soybeans, using specific fungi like Aspergillus oryzae. Kojic acid inhibits melanin production in the skin by interfering with the activity of the enzyme tyrosinase, which plays a central role in the synthesis of melanin. Due to its strong pigmentation-lightening properties, kojic acid is primarily found in skincare products designed to treat hyperpigmentation, age spots, and melasma.</p><p>
</p><p>However, raw kojic acid in its concentrated form is not recommended for direct application to the skin due to the potential for irritation.
