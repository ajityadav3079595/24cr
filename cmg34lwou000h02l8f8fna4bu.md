These technical challenges often lead to extended implementation timelines and reduced ROI, discouraging some organizations from upgrading their project management infrastructure.</p><p>
</p><p>Vendor lock-in concerns also restrain market growth, as companies hesitate to commit to platforms that may limit future flexibility. The lack of standardized data formats across different PM tools creates switching costs that make migration between solutions prohibitively expensive for many organizations.</p><p>
