</p><p>Balanced salt solutions (BSS) are isotonic mixtures containing inorganic salts and sometimes glucose, designed to maintain physiological pH and osmotic balance. These solutions are essential for cell washing, media preparation, and maintaining cell viability in vitro. Common formulations include <strong>Hanks' Balanced Salt Solution (HBSS)</strong>, <strong>Dulbecco's Phosphate Buffered Saline (DPBS)</strong>, and <strong>Earle's Balanced Salt Solution (EBSS)</strong>, each optimized for specific research and clinical applications.</p><p>
</p><p>Market expansion is fueled by increasing stem cell research, vaccine development, and rising adoption of 3D cell culture techniques. The biopharmaceutical sector accounted for over 42% of BSS consumption in 2023. Recent product innovations include serum-free and xeno-free formulations meeting stringent regulatory requirements. Key players like Thermo Fisher Scientific and Lonza are expanding production capacities to meet growing demand, particularly for GMP-grade solutions used in therapeutic manufacturing.</p><p>
