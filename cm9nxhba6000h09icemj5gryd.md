</p><p>Diacetone Alcohol, also known as DAA, is an organic compound with the formula CH3C(O)CH2C(OH)(CH3)2. It is a colorless liquid with a pleasant odor, and is used as a solvent, in hydraulic brake fluid, and in antifreeze. Diacetone Alcohol is a beta-hydroxy ketone formed by hydroxylation of 4-methylpentan-2-one at the 4-position, and has been isolated from Achnatherum robustum. It is an oxygenated solvent with a low evaporation rate, making it an excellent solvent for organic peroxides and a common synthetic intermediate in various industrial applications. Additionally, it is used in paints and coatings to protect metallic substrates against corrosion.</p><p>
