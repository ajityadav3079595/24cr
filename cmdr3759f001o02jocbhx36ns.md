</p><p>Sphered form activated alumina refers to a specialized form of activated alumina that has been processed into spherical shapes. Activated alumina is a highly porous form of aluminum oxide that is widely used for adsorption, catalysis, and purification applications. The spherical form offers advantages in terms of flow characteristics, pressure drop, and mechanical strength compared to irregularly shaped activated alumina. Key applications include water treatment (fluoride removal), gas drying, catalyst supports, and petrochemical processing. The spherical shape provides uniform packing density in fixed-bed applications and better resistance to attrition in fluidized bed systems.
