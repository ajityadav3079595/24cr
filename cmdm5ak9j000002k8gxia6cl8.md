End User Industry Analysis</p><p>
</p><p>The global indium alloy market is influenced by several end-user industries, each contributing to demand in different ways. The <strong>electronics industry</strong> is the largest consumer, accounting for over 40% of total demand, primarily for soldering application in semiconductor and display manufacturing. The <strong>automotive sector</strong> utilizes indium alloy in bearing and thermal management system, driven by the shift toward electric vehicle. The <strong>healthcare industry</strong> uses indium alloy in dental application and medical device, benefiting from their biocompatibility. Additionally, the <strong>aerospace and defense sector</strong> relies on these alloy for high-performance component, further boosting market growth.</p><p>
