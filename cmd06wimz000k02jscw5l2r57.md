</p><p>The High Flux Core market refers to the industry involved in the production and supply of high-performance magnetic cores made primarily from a mixture of nickel and iron, typically in a 50:50 ratio. These cores are widely used in applications requiring high energy storage capacity and superior saturation flux density. The cores are compacted from an alloy powder and are considered a more efficient alternative to traditional nickel-iron lamination used in other magnetic core products.</p><p>
</p><p>High Flux cores are designed to store and release magnetic energy more effectively, which makes them ideal for use in transformers, inductors, motors, and other electromagnetic devices. The increased demand for such products, particularly in high-efficiency energy systems, has resulted in a steady rise in the global market for High Flux cores.</p><p>
