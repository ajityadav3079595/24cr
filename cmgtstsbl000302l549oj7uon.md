</p><p>Schisandra extract is derived from the dried fruit of the Schisandra chinensis plant, which is a member of the Magnoliaceae family. Often referred to as the "five-flavor fruit" due to its unique taste profile, Schisandra has been used for centuries in traditional Chinese medicine and is gaining recognition globally for its adaptogenic properties.
