These bricks provide exceptional resistance to thermal shock, slag corrosion, and oxidation, making them ideal for lining electric arc furnaces (EAF) used predominantly in steelmaking processes. The carbon content varies, typically categorized into segments like 70%-75%, 75%-80%, 80%-85%, and above 85%, which influences their performance in direct current (DC) or alternating current (AC) electric furnaces.</p><p>
