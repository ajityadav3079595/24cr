</p><p>Pharmaceutical Grade Xanthan Gum is a high-purity polysaccharide produced through fermentation of glucose or sucrose. As a multifunctional excipient, it serves as a thickener, stabilizer, and emulsifier in drug formulations. Its unique rheological properties, including pseudoplastic behavior and compatibility with various active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs), make it indispensable in oral suspensions, topical creams, and controlled-release formulations.</p><p>
