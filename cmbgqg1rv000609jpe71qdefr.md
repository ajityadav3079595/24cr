</p><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) hoses are specialized tubing systems that leverage the unique properties of PTFEâ€”a synthetic fluoropolymer of tetrafluoroethylene. Known for its exceptional chemical resistance, high thermal stability, low friction coefficient, and non-stick characteristics, PTFE hoses are widely utilized across industries where high-performance fluid transfer is critical. These hoses are particularly effective in transporting aggressive chemical media, high-temperature fluids, and volatile gases, owing to their inertness and durability. PTFE hoses are commonly reinforced with stainless steel braiding to withstand high pressures, ensuring operational integrity even under extreme conditions. Their application spans across sectors such as oil and gas, chemical processing, pharmaceuticals, automotive, aerospace, and food and beverage industries, making them a vital component in modern industrial infrastructure.</p><p>
