# United States Heliox Market Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

Heliox, a helium-oxygen mixture, is widely utilized in medical and diving applications due to its unique properties. This gas mixture typically consists of varying ratios, such as 70% helium and 30% oxygen, 79% helium and 21% oxygen, and 80% helium and 20% oxygen. Heliox is especially known for its effectiveness in medical respiratory therapies and its use in technical diving.</p><p>
