Known for their strength, toughness, and optical transparency, polycarbonates are highly valued in engineering applications. Their unique properties include being easily molded, thermoformed, and processed into various shapes. These polymers are used extensively in industries that require high-performance materials, particularly where durability and optical clarity are essential. <strong>Shift Towards Sustainable Production Methods to Emerge as a Key Trend in the Market</strong></p><p>

This trend is bolstered by circular economy initiatives, where post-consumer polycarbonate waste from electronics and automotive sectors is reprocessed, minimizing landfill contributions and supporting a greener supply chain.</p><p>
