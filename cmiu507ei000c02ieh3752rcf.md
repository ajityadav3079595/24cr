</p><p>Decoration moisture-proof boards are engineered building materials incorporating moisture-resistant additives during manufacturing, significantly reducing water absorption and expansion. These specialized panels, also known as melamine boards, feature a core substrate treated with polymer-based moisture barriers while maintaining structural integrity in humid environments. Primary variants include ordinary moisture-resistant plasterboards and locating point moisture-resistant plasterboards.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is propelled by increasing construction activities in high-humidity regions and stricter building codes mandating moisture-resistant materials.
