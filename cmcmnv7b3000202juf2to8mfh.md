# Dragon Blood Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Dragon Blood is a bright red resin obtained from the plant species belonging to the genus Dracaena, particularly the Dracaena cinnabari tree found in Socotra, Yemen. It is known for its unique color and healing properties, traditionally used in various cultures for medicinal and spiritual purposes. Dragon Blood possesses anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making it sought after in alternative medicine. It has applications in wound healing, skincare, and even as a dye. The resin is obtained by making incisions in the tree bark, allowing the sap to ooze and solidify, forming the precious Dragon Blood resin.</p><p>
