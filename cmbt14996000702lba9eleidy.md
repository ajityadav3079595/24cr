# Asia Pacific PVC Floors  Market Research Report 2025-2032

<p>PVC floors, also known as polyvinyl chloride flooring, are a type of resilient flooring known for their durability, water resistance, and low maintenance. These floors are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications due to their ability to withstand heavy foot traffic and provide a variety of design options, including wood, stone, and abstract patterns. PVC flooring is available in different forms such as homogeneous, heterogeneous, vinyl tiles (VT), and luxury vinyl tiles (LVT), catering to different market needs.</p><p>
