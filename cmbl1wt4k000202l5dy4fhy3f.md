<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Increasing Demand for High-Performance Polymers to Drive PCP Market Growth</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Global GCC para-cumylphenol (PCP) market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for<strong> high-performance polymers</strong> in various industries. The automotive industry's shift towards lightweight materials to improve fuel efficiency has increased the demand for phenolic resins, subsequently driving the PCP market. With the increasing demand for consumer electronics and the region's push towards digital transformation, the PCP market is expected to witness steady growth.
