These bags are composed of multiple polymer layers, each offering specific barrier properties such as resistance to oxygen, moisture, and contamination. The innovative materials used in these bags help extend the shelf life of products like fresh and processed meat, poultry, seafood, and cheese by maintaining product integrity, preventing punctures, and reducing spoilage.</p><p>
