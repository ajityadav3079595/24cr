</p><p>Gamma-Octalactone, chemically known as y-Octalactone (CAS 104-50-7), is a lactone-based aroma compound with the molecular formula C8H14O2. The compound occurs naturally in various food products while also being produced synthetically for industrial use.</p><p>
</p><p>Market expansion is primarily driven by increasing demand for natural flavor additives in food processing and growing fragrance applications in personal care products.
