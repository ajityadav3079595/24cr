</p><p>High performance plastics (HPPs) for 3D printing are advanced thermoplastics that offer superior mechanical, thermal, and chemical resistance compared to standard polymers. These materials, including polyamide (PA), polyetherimide (PEI), and polyetheretherketone (PEEK), are widely used in demanding applications such as aerospace components, medical implants, and automotive parts.
