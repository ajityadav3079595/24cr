</p><p>Biological buffers are specialized chemical solutions that maintain stable pH levels in biological systems, critical for various biochemical and diagnostic applications. These buffers include phosphate-based systems, TRIS (Tris(hydroxymethyl)aminomethane), HEPES (4-(2-hydroxyethyl)-1-piperazineethanesulfonic acid), and MOPS (3-(N-morpholino)propanesulfonic acid), among others. They play a fundamental role in cell culture, protein purification, and molecular biology techniques where precise pH control is essential for maintaining biological activity.</p><p>
