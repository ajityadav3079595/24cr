</p><p>Gold-Tin (AuSn) Alloy Solder Paste is an advanced material used for electronic assembly processes that require superior high-temperature stability, conductivity, and mechanical properties. This solder paste is made by combining gold (Au) and tin (Sn) in different ratios to achieve specific characteristics required for various applications. Unlike other solder pastes, AuSn solder paste offers excellent thermal conductivity, mechanical strength, and resistance to fatigue, making it ideal for devices subjected to high temperatures.</p><p>
</p><p>Gold-Tin (AuSn) alloys have high melting points (typically over 150°C), which make them especially suitable for high-reliability applications in sectors such as military, aerospace, high-power LEDs, and medical devices. The ability to withstand thermal cycling and high-temperature conditions makes AuSn solder paste a preferred choice in these industries.</p><p>
