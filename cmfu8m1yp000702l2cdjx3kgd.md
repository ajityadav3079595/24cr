</p><p>Light Burned Magnesium Oxide (LBMO) is a magnesium oxide compound produced by the calcination of magnesium carbonate at temperatures ranging between 700°C and 1000°C. Unlike Dead-Burned Magnesium Oxide (DBMO), which is sintered at higher temperatures, LBMO exhibits higher reactivity due to its porous structure and increased surface area. Key applications include catalysts for chemical synthesis, wastewater treatment for pH adjustment, refractory binders, and as an additive in rubber and plastic manufacturing.
