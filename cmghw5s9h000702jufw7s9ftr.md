<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Sustainability Demands in Textile Industry Accelerating Adoption</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The global textile industry's shift toward sustainable materials has become a powerful driver for recycled polyester staple fiber adoption. With textile production accounting for nearly 10% of global carbon emissions, brands face increasing pressure to reduce environmental impacts.
