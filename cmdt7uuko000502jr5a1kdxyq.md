End User Industry Analysis</p><p>
</p><p>The <strong>pulp &amp; paper industry</strong> is the largest consumer of sodium hydroxide, accounting for a significant share of the market. The <strong>soap &amp; detergent industry</strong> relies on caustic soda for saponification. Additionally, the <strong>petroleum refining</strong> and <strong>chemical processing</strong> industries use NaOH for pH regulation and chemical synthesis. The <strong>aluminum processing</strong> sector also contributes to demand, as NaOH is used in bauxite refining.</p><p>
