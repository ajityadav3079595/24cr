<strong>PTFE Filled Compound</strong> is a composite material made of Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) as the base material, combined with various fillers such as glass fiber, graphite, and other substances. This combination enhances its mechanical properties, including improved chemical stability, corrosion resistance, and low friction coefficient. PTFE, known for its excellent properties such as high resistance to solvents, flames, and heat, becomes even more versatile when filled with materials that modify its characteristics for specific industrial uses. These compounds are used in a wide range of applications including automotive, industrial, healthcare, and electrical components, where high performance and durability are crucial.</p><p>
