</p><p>Plain bars are smooth-surfaced steel reinforcement bars widely used in construction for concrete reinforcement. These unribbed steel bars provide structural strength and are categorized by diameter ranges including 6-10mm, 10-22mm, and above 22mm. While lacking the deformation patterns of deformed bars, plain bars offer better bonding with concrete through mechanical anchoring.</p><p>
