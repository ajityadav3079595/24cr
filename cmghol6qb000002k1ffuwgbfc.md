</p><p>Tricyclodecane Dimethanol Diacrylate (TCDDA) is a specialty acrylate monomer characterized by its unique molecular structure with two acrylate groups bonded to a tricyclodecane dimethanol backbone. This configuration provides exceptional performance properties including high glass transition temperature (Tg), superior hardness, low shrinkage after curing, and excellent hydrophobicity. These characteristics make it particularly valuable for demanding applications in coatings, adhesives, 3D printing resins, and optical materials.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from industrial coatings and adhesives sectors, where TCDDA's performance advantages justify its premium pricing.
