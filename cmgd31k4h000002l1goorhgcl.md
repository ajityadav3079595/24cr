</p><p>TPEG (Third-Generation Polycarboxylate Superplasticizer Ether) is a key polymer used in construction materials, primarily as a high-performance water reducer in concrete applications. Its chemical structure consists of methyl alkenyl polyoxyethylene ether and polyoxypropylene ether, represented as CH2=CH(CH3)CH2CH2O(CH2CH2O)m(CH2CH3CHO)nH. TPEG products are available in paste and flaky forms, with wide applications in water conservancy projects, nuclear power infrastructure, railway construction, and general building projects.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing infrastructure development globally, particularly in emerging economies. While China dominates production with key players like Liaoning Oxiranchem and Nanjing Yangzi Oxiranchem collectively holding 36% market share, demand is expanding in other regions due to urbanization trends.
