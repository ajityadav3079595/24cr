The product is available in various forms, including <strong>powder, liquid, and crystal</strong>, catering to diverse industrial requirements. Nickel aminosulfonate is widely utilized in industries such as electronics, automotive, aerospace, and metallurgy due to its ability to enhance the performance and longevity of metal components.
