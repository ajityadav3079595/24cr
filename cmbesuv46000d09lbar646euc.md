</p><p>Lavender Essential Oil is a highly concentrated liquid extracted from the lavender plant (Lavandula angustifolia) through steam distillation. It is widely recognized for its aromatic, therapeutic, and medicinal properties. Commonly used in personal care products, aromatherapy, pharmaceuticals, and household applications, lavender essential oil has gained popularity for its calming and anti-inflammatory benefits. The oil is rich in bioactive compounds, including linalool and linalyl acetate, which contribute to its soothing effects and antimicrobial properties.</p><p>
