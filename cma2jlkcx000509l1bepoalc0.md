</p><p>Custom Heat Shrink Tubing refers to a shrinkable plastic tube that is used primarily for insulating wires and providing protection for electrical components. This type of tubing is designed to shrink when heat is applied, typically made from polyolefin, which shrinks radially without reducing in length. When applied to wires, it provides protection against abrasion, moisture, and other environmental factors. It is an essential element in both electrical work and in the repair and bundling of wires.</p><p>
</p><p>The key function of heat shrink tubing is to create an environmental seal, offering both mechanical protection and electrical insulation for wires, cables, and other components. Custom heat shrink tubing is tailored to meet specific requirements in terms of size, color, and performance characteristics, making it a flexible and reliable solution across various sectors, including automotive, aerospace, and military industries.</p><p>
