<h1>Wheel Flange Lubrication Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p><strong>Wheel Flange Lubrication Market</strong> refers to the industry focused on the production, distribution, and application of lubrication systems specifically designed to reduce friction between wheel flanges and rails. These systems are essential in minimizing wear and tear, reducing noise, and improving the operational efficiency of rail and metro systems. The lubrication is applied through onboard systems mounted on locomotives or stationary units installed along the tracks. These solutions are widely used in both freight and passenger rail systems, with an increasing emphasis on environmentally friendly and efficient lubricants.</p><p>
