</p><p><strong>Understanding AC Plasma Generators</strong></p><p>
</p><p>AC plasma generators utilize alternating current to ionize gases, creating plasma—a highly energized state of matter comprising ions and free electrons. This plasma is instrumental in various industrial applications, including surface treatment, thin-film deposition, and material processing, due to its ability to alter material properties at the molecular level.</p><p>
</p><p><strong>Key Market Drivers</strong></p><p>

	</p><p><strong>Technological Advancements in Manufacturing</strong>: The shift towards miniaturization and precision in manufacturing has heightened the demand for plasma-based processes.
