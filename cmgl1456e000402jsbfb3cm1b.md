</p><p>Polyalphaolefin (PAO) lubricants are synthetic hydrocarbons produced through oligomerization of alpha-olefins, offering superior performance characteristics including excellent viscosity index, thermal stability, and oxidation resistance compared to mineral oils. These high-performance synthetic lubricants are categorized by viscosity grades (low, medium, high) and find extensive applications across automotive and industrial sectors.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by China's expanding automotive production (which reached 27 million vehicles in 2023) and the country's industrial sector modernization initiatives.
