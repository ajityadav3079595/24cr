</p><p>Rust converters and inhibitors are specialized chemical formulations designed to treat and prevent metal corrosion. These products chemically convert rust (iron oxide) into stable compounds or form protective coatings to inhibit further oxidation. Common types include tannin-based, water-based, and solvent-based formulations, available in spray, liquid, or gel forms. They play a critical role in metal preservation across industries.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is primarily driven by increasing industrialization, infrastructure development, and automotive production across Southeast Asian countries. Rising maritime activities and humid tropical climates accelerating metal corrosion further boost demand. However, environmental concerns regarding volatile organic compounds (VOCs) in some formulations present challenges. Key manufacturers are responding with eco-friendly innovations to meet stricter environmental regulations while maintaining product efficacy.</p><p>
