# Seaweed Extracts for Cosmetics and Food and Beverage Market Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Seaweed extracts for cosmetics and food &amp; beverage are derived from various algae species, including <strong>fucus vesiculosus</strong> and <strong>furaceae</strong>, as well as nutrient-dense red algae and spirulina. These extracts are rich in bioactive compounds such as proteins, amino acids, vitamins, minerals, and phytonutrients like chlorophyll growth factor (CGF). In cosmetics, seaweed extracts enhance skin hydration, anti-aging, and revitalization properties. In food &amp; beverages, they are used for their nutritional content and natural colorant properties.</p><p>
