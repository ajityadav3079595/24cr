# Lead Nitrate Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Lead nitrate is an inorganic compound with the chemical formula Pb(NO3)2. It is a crystalline, colorless, and highly toxic substance that is widely used in various industrial applications. Primarily utilized as an oxidizing agent, lead nitrate plays a crucial role in the production of dyes, pigments, and explosives. It is also a key component in the mining industry, where it is used as a flotation reagent to enhance the recovery of precious metals. Additionally, lead nitrate is used in PVC stabilizers and as a raw material in the manufacture of other lead-based compounds.</p><p>
