This material is widely used for its adsorption properties, thermal stability, and chemical resistance. Key products include fluoride adsorbents, desiccants, catalysts, and refractory additives. Reactive alumina is particularly valued in water treatment for fluoride removal, in the oil &amp; gas sector as a catalyst support, and in industrial processes requiring moisture control. End User Industry Analysis</p><p>
