<strong>MARKET CHALLENGE</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Stringent Regulatory Requirements Pose Manufacturing Challenges</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Meeting pharmacopoeial standards presents significant challenges for methylene blue manufacturers. </p><p>Additionally, regulatory requirements are not static - pharmacopoeias regularly update their standards, requiring manufacturers to continuously adapt their processes. Recent updates to pharmacopoeial monographs have introduced more stringent testing requirements for impurities and degradation products. These changes often require manufacturers to invest in new analytical equipment and validation processes, increasing production costs. The complexity of maintaining compliance across multiple pharmacopoeial standards (USP, EP, JP) for global markets further compounds these challenges.</p><p>
