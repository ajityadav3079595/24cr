<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The Global 10B Enriched Boron Carbide market refers to the industry segment that involves the production, distribution, and application of boron carbide enriched with the isotope Boron-10 (10B). Boron carbide (B4C) is an advanced ceramic material known for its exceptional hardness, low density, and high neutron absorption capability. The enrichment of boron carbide with 10B significantly enhances its efficiency in neutron shielding and radiation absorption, making it essential in nuclear applications, defense, and aerospace industries.</p><p>
