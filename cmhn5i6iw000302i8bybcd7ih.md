</p><p>Olefins are unsaturated hydrocarbons that serve as fundamental building blocks in petrochemical production. These compounds, including ethylene, propylene, and butadiene, are primarily derived through steam cracking of naphtha or natural gas liquids. As critical feedstocks, they enable manufacturing of essential polymers like polyethylene (PE) and polypropylene (PP) - materials that form the backbone of modern plastic products, packaging solutions, and industrial applications.</p><p>
