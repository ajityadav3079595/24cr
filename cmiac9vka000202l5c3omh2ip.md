These versatile chemicals serve as crucial ingredients in detergents, emulsifiers, wetting agents, and dispersants across multiple industries. Key product types include alcohol ethoxylates, fatty amine ethoxylates, and methyl ester ethoxylates, each offering unique surfactant properties.</p><p>
