# Laser Direct Structuring Grade Resin Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Laser Direct Structuring (LDS) technology requires a specific grade of plastic material with an organo-metallic compound. This compound, when activated by laser ablation, acts as a catalyst for electroless plating deposition. The additive is essential for the process and influences the physical characteristics of the plastic material.</p><p>
