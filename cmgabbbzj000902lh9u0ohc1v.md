</p><p>Sulfurous Oxychloride (SOCl2) is a crucial inorganic chemical compound widely used as a chlorinating and thionylating agent in various industrial applications. This highly reactive, colorless to pale yellow liquid is primarily employed in the synthesis of pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, dyes, and specialty chemicals. The compound’s ability to replace hydroxyl groups with chlorine atoms makes it indispensable in organic synthesis.</p><p>
</p><p>The market is witnessing steady growth due to increasing demand from the pharmaceutical sector, particularly in Thailand and Indonesia, where drug manufacturing is expanding.
