</p><p><strong>Dibenzyl Toluene (DBT)</strong> is a synthetic organic compound classified as an aromatic hydrocarbon. It is primarily used as a heat transfer fluid and a chemical intermediate due to its excellent thermal stability, low vapor pressure, and resistance to oxidation. The compound is particularly valued in industries requiring high-temperature applications, such as chemical manufacturing, plastic and rubber production, and pharmaceuticals. Dibenzyl Toluene is available in various grades, including industrial grade, tailored to meet the specific requirements of different end-use applications. Its chemical properties, such as solubility in organic solvents and non-reactivity with metals, make it a preferred choice in processes that demand consistent thermal performance.
