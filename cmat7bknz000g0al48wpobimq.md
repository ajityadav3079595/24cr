</p><p><strong>Low Melt Polyester Staple Fiber (LMPSF)</strong> refers to a specialized type of polyester fiber designed with a low melting point sheath around a high melting point core. This unique bicomponent structure allows LMPSF to bond with other fibers or materials during the heat treatment process without compromising structural integrity. LMPSF is widely used in industries such as automotive, textiles, construction, and hygiene due to its superior bonding capabilities, softness, durability, and eco-friendly properties.</p><p>
