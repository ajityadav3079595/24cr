</p><p>Pentafluorophenol (CAS 771-61-9) is an organofluorine compound characterized by a phenol group substituted with five fluorine atoms. This highly fluorinated aromatic compound serves as a versatile building block in organic synthesis, particularly for pharmaceutical intermediates, agrochemicals, and liquid crystal materials. Its strong electron-withdrawing properties make it valuable in nucleophilic aromatic substitution reactions.</p><p>
