Its applications span across baked goods, beverages, confectionery, and processed foods as both a sweetening agent and texturizer.</p><p>

</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Expanding Applications in Functional Foods and Beverages Fueling Adoption</strong></p><p>

</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Growing Vegan Population Creating Sustained Demand</strong></p><p>

This consumer-driven shift toward plant-based diets presents significant long-term opportunities for market players.</p><p>
