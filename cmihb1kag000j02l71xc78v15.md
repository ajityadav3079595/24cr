</p><p>The production of ferrous sulfate monohydrate relies heavily on sulfuric acid and iron byproducts from steel manufacturing operations. These practical challenges can deter some potential customers, particularly in humid tropical regions where moisture control is more difficult to maintain throughout the supply chain.</p><p>
