</p><p>Acid dye digital inks are specialized colorants designed for digital textile printing applications, particularly on protein-based fibers like silk, wool, and nylon. These water-based inks contain acid dyes that form ionic bonds with fabric fibers under steaming conditions, delivering superior wash-fastness and vibrant coloration. The product category includes strong acid dyes (pH 2-4), weak acid dyes (pH 4-6), and neutral dyes (pH 6-7), each optimized for specific textile substrates and printing conditions.</p><p>
