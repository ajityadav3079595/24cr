</p><p><strong>Advancements in Material Technology</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Material innovation is transforming the U bend tube landscape, with stainless steel and duplex alloys gaining prominence due to their superior corrosion resistance in harsh operating environments. The development of weld-free bending techniques has also improved product longevity, reducing failure rates in critical applications by approximately 30% compared to traditional welded alternatives.</p><p>
