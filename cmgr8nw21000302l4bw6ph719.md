</p><p>Plastic additives are specialized chemical compounds integrated into polymers to enhance performance characteristics and modify material properties. These additives play a critical role in polymer processing and final product applications, enabling improvements in durability, flexibility, heat resistance, UV stability, and flame retardancy. Major additive categories include plasticizers, stabilizers, flame retardants, impact modifiers, and specialized functional additives.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing polymer consumption across packaging, automotive, and construction sectors, coupled with rising demand for high-performance materials. </p><p>The global push toward lightweight, durable materials across automotive, construction, and consumer goods sectors is significantly driving plastic additive demand. </p><p>The plastic additives market faces persistent headwinds from raw material price fluctuations, with petrochemical feedstocks experiencing 18-22% cost variability since early 2024.
