</p><p>Trioctyl Trimellitate (TOTM) is a fire-resistant primary plasticizer combining properties of polyester and monomer plasticizers, primarily used in PVC applications. Its superior electrical properties make it ideal for high-temperature (105°C rated) fireproof cables, automotive components, pharmaceutical materials, and industrial gaskets. The chemical exhibits excellent migration resistance and durability compared to conventional plasticizers.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by stringent fire safety regulations in construction and automotive sectors, particularly in North America and Europe. Asia-Pacific dominates consumption due to rapid infrastructure development and expanding wire &amp; cable production in China and India. Recent capacity expansions by BASF in Germany and LG Chemical in South Korea indicate strong manufacturer confidence in long-term demand.</p><p>
