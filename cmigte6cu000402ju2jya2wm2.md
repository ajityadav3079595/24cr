The industry categorizes these ingredients into top notes (initial fragrance impression), middle notes (core scent), and base notes (long-lasting aroma), with common examples including citrus oils, floral extracts, and woody compounds.</p><p>

</p><p>The aroma ingredient market, while poised for steady growth, grapples with significant supply chain disruptions that affect availability and pricing.
