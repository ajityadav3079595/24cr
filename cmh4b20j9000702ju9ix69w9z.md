<strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Growing Demand for Natural Food Coloring to Propel Market Growth</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Global shift towards natural food additives is driving significant demand for keto carotenoid canthaxanthin as a safe and effective coloring agent. With synthetic food dyes facing increasing scrutiny due to health concerns, food manufacturers are actively seeking natural alternatives. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Expanding Aquaculture Industry Boosts Feed Grade Segment</strong></p><p>

Canthaxanthin's ability to produce the desirable pink-red pigmentation in fish flesh makes it indispensable for premium seafood products. These restrictions directly impact the potential market size and application scope. For instance, some markets prohibit canthaxanthin use in certain food categories altogether, while others impose labeling requirements that may deter manufacturer adoption.</p><p>
</p><p>The approval process for new applications remains lengthy and costly, sometimes requiring multiple years of safety testing before gaining regulatory clearance. This creates barriers to innovation and limits the ingredient's potential in emerging product categories. Manufacturers must navigate complex compliance requirements that vary significantly between markets, increasing operational costs and slowing market penetration.</p><p>
