</p><p>Coated braided fiberglass sleeving is a specialized insulation material consisting of fiberglass yarn braided into a tubular structure and coated with high-temperature resistant materials like silicone or acrylic. These sleeves provide exceptional thermal protection (withstanding temperatures up to 500°C), electrical insulation, and mechanical durability, making them essential components in demanding industrial applications.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is primarily driven by expanding electrical infrastructure projects worldwide and stricter safety regulations in the energy sector. The automotive industry's shift toward electric vehicles has created additional demand, with EV battery assemblies requiring advanced thermal management solutions. However, price volatility in raw materials like fiberglass and coating polymers presents ongoing challenges for manufacturers balancing quality and cost competitiveness.</p><p>
