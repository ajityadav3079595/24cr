# Global Electromagnetic Field Shielding Fabrics Market Research Report 2025-2032

<p>The global electromagnetic field shielding fabric market was valued at <strong>US$ 427 million in 2024</strong> and is projected to reach <strong>US$ 718 million by 2032</strong>, exhibiting a <strong>Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 6.7%</strong> during the forecast period (2025–2032). The market growth is driven by increasing demand for electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding solutions across various industries, including electronics, healthcare, and aerospace. The rising awareness of electromagnetic radiation hazards and stringent government regulations regarding electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) are also contributing to market expansion.</p><p>
</p><p>Electromagnetic field shielding fabric is a specialized material designed to block or attenuate electromagnetic fields (EMF) and radio frequency interference (RFI). These fabrics are typically composed of conductive materials such as <strong>metal fiber, cotton fiber, or polyester fiber</strong> woven with conductive thread. They are widely used in applications like EMI screens, RFI screens, grounding grids, and bio-circuits. The primary function of these fabrics is to protect sensitive electronic devices from electromagnetic interference and to safeguard human health from prolonged exposure to EMF radiation.
