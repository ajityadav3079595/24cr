</p><p>Glyoxylic acid, or oxoacetic acid, is an organic compound that serves as a key intermediate in various industrial processes. This bifunctional molecule contains both aldehyde and carboxylic acid groups, making it valuable for applications in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and specialty chemicals. The compound plays a critical role in the glyoxylate cycle and is commercially available in varying concentrations, primarily as 40% and 50% aqueous solutions.</p><p>
</p><p>While the pharmaceutical sector remains the dominant application area, growing demand from the fragrance industry is emerging as a significant growth driver. Recent technological advancements in production methods, particularly in China where most manufacturing capacity is concentrated, are helping improve yield efficiency.
