</p><p>Polyamide-6 dipped tyre cord fabric is an industrial textile used for reinforcing tire skeletons. This high-strength fabric is manufactured using nylon 6 (polycaprolactam) fiber cords that undergo a dipping process. The material significantly enhances tire load-bearing capacity, impact resistance, and durability. It finds primary application in bias tires, engineering tires, and high-performance passenger vehicle tires.</p><p>
