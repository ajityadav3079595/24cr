</p><p>Glyceryl stearate, also known as glyceryl monostearate, is a white, waxy solid widely used in the formulation of cosmetic and personal care products. It is a glycerin ester of stearic acid, typically synthesized through esterification. As a non-ionic emulsifier, glyceryl stearate helps stabilize oil-in-water emulsions, giving creams and lotions a smooth and creamy consistency.</p><p>
</p><p>In cosmetic formulations, glyceryl stearate functions as a thickening agent, emollient, and stabilizer. It enhances the texture and application of products like moisturizers, conditioners, sunscreens, and makeup. Due to its mildness and skin compatibility, it is especially favored in formulations targeted at sensitive skin. Cosmetic Grade Glyceryl Stearate meets stringent purity and safety standards suitable for topical applications.</p><p>
