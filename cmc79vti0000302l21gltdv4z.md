# Rock Wool Board For Curtain Wall Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Rock wool boards for curtain walls are specialized insulation materials crafted from natural basalt rock and recycled slag. These boards are engineered to provide superior thermal insulation, acoustic dampening, and fire resistance, making them ideal for use in various dry-hanging curtain wall systems. With a melting point exceeding 1150°C, rock wool boards offer exceptional thermal stability and high-temperature shrinkage resistance, ensuring effective fire prevention and energy efficiency in building applications.</p><p>
