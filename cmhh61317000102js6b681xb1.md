</p><p>Coated freesheet paper is a premium printing paper with a clay or polymer coating applied to enhance surface smoothness and ink receptivity. Unlike mechanical papers containing wood pulp, freesheet is produced from chemical pulps, making it ideal for high-quality printing applications. The product finds extensive use in magazines, catalogs, corporate brochures, and commercial printing where superior image reproduction is required.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by sustained demand from publishing sectors and packaging applications, particularly in e-commerce product catalogs.
