</p><p>High-purity SiC powder is synthesized through a tightly controlled process involving the reaction of high-purity silicon and carbon powders at elevated temperatures. Post-reaction processing, including cleaning and grinding, ensures the final product meets stringent purity criteria for applications like crystal growth in the semiconductor industry.</p><p>
</p><p>High-purity silicon carbide powder is produced by synthesizing high-purity silicon powder and high-purity carbon powder at elevated temperatures in precise ratios. This process ensures a resulting SiC product of exceptional purity (&gt;99.999%), ideal for high-end crystal growth and advanced electronic applications.</p><p>
