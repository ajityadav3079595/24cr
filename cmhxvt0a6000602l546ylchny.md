These pads are increasingly preferred in applications requiring <strong>low outgassing</strong> and <strong>minimal contamination</strong>, making them ideal for sensitive electronics, medical devices, and automotive systems. Their composition typically includes ceramic-filled polymers or phase-change materials that provide superior thermal conductivity while avoiding silicone migration issues.</p><p>
