</p><p>PA6 Semi Dull Chip is a specialized polyamide 6 variant manufactured using caprolactam as the primary raw material, with the addition of 0.1% to 0.5% titanium dioxide (TiO2) as a matting agent during polymerization. These white cylindrical particles exhibit excellent electrical insulation, alkali resistance, and corrosion resistance properties. The material finds extensive application in producing nylon filaments, short yarns, and industrial silk, making it crucial for textile, industrial supply chains, and automotive interior sectors.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand from the textile industry and expanding applications in automotive interiors. While China currently dominates production capacity, Europe and North America remain key markets due to their advanced manufacturing ecosystems. The Fiber Chip segment is expected to show particularly strong growth, though engineering plastics applications are gaining traction in electronics and industrial sectors. Major players like BASF, DSM, and Sinopec continue to invest in production capacity expansions to meet rising global demand.</p><p>
