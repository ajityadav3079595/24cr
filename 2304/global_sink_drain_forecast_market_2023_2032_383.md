</p><p>The Sink Drain Market encompasses the production, distribution, and sale of sink drainage systems and accessories used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. These systems facilitate efficient water drainage, prevent clogging, and maintain hygiene in sinks used in kitchens, bathrooms, and industrial wash stations. Sink drains are typically made of materials such as stainless steel, PVC, brass, and ABS plastic, and they come in various designs, including pop-up, basket strainer, and P-trap configurations.</p><p>
