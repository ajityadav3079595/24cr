</p><p>HFO-1336mzz(Z) is a fourth-generation hydrofluoroolefin (HFO) with the chemical name cis-1,1,1,4,4,4-hexafluoro-2-butene (CAS 692-49-9). It represents an environmentally friendly alternative to traditional refrigerants because it features <strong>zero ozone depletion potential (ODP)</strong> and an <strong>ultra-low global warming potential (GWP)</strong> ranging between 2 and 9.4.
