</p><p>While the garden fertilizer market shows strong growth potential, it faces significant challenges from tightening environmental regulations. These restrictions have led to formulation changes and reformulation costs exceeding $50 million annually for major manufacturers. The regulatory constraints are particularly challenging for conventional fertilizer producers who must invest heavily in R&amp;D to develop compliant products without compromising efficacy.</p><p>
</p><p>Climate change impacts present another restraint factor, with unpredictable weather patterns affecting both fertilizer application schedules and consumer gardening activities.
