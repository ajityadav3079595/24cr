This white hygroscopic salt serves as a versatile precursor in numerous industrial applications. The compound exists in anhydrous and hydrated forms (typically heptahydrate), with purity grades ranging from 2N (99%) to 5N (99.999%) to meet different industrial requirements.</p><p>
