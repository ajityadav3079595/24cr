<strong style="font-size:13px">Colloidal silica</strong> consists of <strong style="font-size:13px">silica nanoparticles suspended in a liquid medium</strong>, stabilized electrostatically to prevent aggregation or settling. <strong style="font-size:13px">Ultra-high-purity colloidal silica</strong>, typically boasting a <strong style="font-size:13px">purity level of 99.999% or higher</strong>, is indispensable for critical applications in electronics, semiconductors, and advanced material coatings. Key market players such as <strong style="font-size:13px">Fuso Chemical, Merck,</strong> and <strong style="font-size:13px">Nouryon</strong> dominate the industry, with <strong style="font-size:13px">Wafer Polishing &amp; CMP Slurry</strong> being the leading application area.</p><p>
