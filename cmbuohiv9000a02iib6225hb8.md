</p><p>Manganous Manganic Oxide (Mn3O4) is an inorganic compound characterized by its mixed-valence manganese states (Mn2+ and Mn3+). This thermally stable oxide is widely used as a precursor material in electronics, ceramics, and battery applications due to its unique magnetic and electrochemical properties. The compound serves as a critical component in the production of <strong>ferrites, lithium-ion battery cathodes</strong>, and specialized glass formulations.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by expanding applications in <strong>energy storage systems</strong>, particularly in lithium manganese oxide (LMO) batteries, which accounted for 12-15% of the global lithium-ion battery market in 2023.
