# Horse Bedding Products Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2031

<p>The global Horse Bedding Products market was valued at 160 million in 2024 and is projected to reach US$ 263 million by 2031, at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period.
Horse beddings create a comfortable and hygienic environment for horses, offering them a soft and supportive surface to rest upon. Horse beddings help cushion horses' joints, reduce the risk of pressure sores, and provide a warm and inviting space in stalls. The right bedding also contributes to cleanliness, making it easier to maintain a healthy living environment.
The Global key players of Horse Bedding Products include Stora Enso, Bedmax, Vida, etc. The top three players hold a share about 37%. Europe is the largest market, and has a share about 44%.
