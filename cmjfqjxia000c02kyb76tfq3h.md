<p>The global urethane scalping screen market was valued at <strong>US$ 127 million in 2024</strong> and is projected to reach <strong>US$ 187 million by 2032</strong>, exhibiting a <strong>Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 5%</strong> during the forecast period (2025–2032). The market growth is driven by increasing demand for efficient screening solutions in industries such as mining, construction, and recycling. The adoption of urethane screen due to its durability, resistance to abrasion, and longer lifespan compared to traditional metal screens is a key factor contributing to this growth.</p><p>
</p><p>Urethane scalping screens are specialized screening solutions used in industries such as mining, recycling, and construction to separate and classify materials based on size. These screens are made from polyurethane, a synthetic polymer known for its high resistance to wear, tear, and abrasion. Unlike traditional metal screens, urethane screens offer superior durability, noise reduction, and resistance to clogging, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. The primary function of these screens is to remove oversized material from the feed stream, ensuring efficient processing of raw materials.
