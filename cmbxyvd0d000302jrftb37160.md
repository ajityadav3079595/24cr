</p><p>The compound is not only recognized for its ability to promote smoother and healthier skin but also for its antiviral, antifungal, and antibacterial properties. </p><p>In cosmetics, Glycyrrhetinic Acid is most often used as an active ingredient in creams, lotions, sunscreens, and serums due to its ability to hydrate the skin and improve overall skin tone. As consumer demand for clean and effective beauty products continues to rise, the use of natural compounds like Glycyrrhetinic Acid is expected to increase.</p><p>
