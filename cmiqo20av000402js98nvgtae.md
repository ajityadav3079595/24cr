</p><p>Animal protein glue is a natural adhesive derived from collagen extracted from animal byproducts such as bones, hides and connective tissues. These bio-based adhesives are prized for their strong bonding properties, biodegradability and historical use in specialized applications including woodworking, bookbinding and restoration work. The product exists primarily in two variants - black type (containing additives for enhanced properties) and amber type (pure protein formulation).</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand for sustainable adhesive solutions across industries, particularly in woodworking and textile applications where environmental regulations are becoming stricter. However, competition from synthetic alternatives and fluctuating raw material costs present challenges. </p><p>The animal protein glue market faces significant challenges from fluctuating raw material prices.
