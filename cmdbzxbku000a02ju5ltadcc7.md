# Fluidization Cloth Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Fluidization Cloth is a material used for the pneumatic transportation of powdery substances like cement and alumina. It is also suitable for use in pneumatic conveying devices for other powder and granular materials. The cloth is placed in the middle of a chute, allowing powder materials to flow through it with the help of pneumatic pressure, achieving the purpose of transportation.</p><p>
