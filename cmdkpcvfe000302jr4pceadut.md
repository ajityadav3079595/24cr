</p><p>The global <strong>Glass and Ceramic Pigment market</strong> encompasses the production and application of pigments used to color and enhance glass and ceramic products. They impart decorative colors, providing both aesthetic appeal and functional properties like thermal resistance, chemical stability, and lightfastness. These attributes make glass and ceramic pigments ideal for use in a wide range of products, from decorative items to industrial applications such as glass bottles, architectural ceramics, household ceramics, and automotive glass.</p><p>
