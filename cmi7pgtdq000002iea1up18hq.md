# PET Substrate Siliconized Film Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Silicone-coated PET films are characterized by their exceptional release properties and the inherent benefits of polyester films. These include high mechanical strength, low shrinkage, optical clarity, and robust chemical resistance. These films are utilized in a variety of applications, such as labels, tapes, and industrial liners, where reliable release performance and durability are critical.</p><p>
