</p><p>Non-polymeric organic nanomaterials are materials that are composed of organic molecules and do not contain any polymeric structures. These materials are used in various applications due to their unique properties such as high surface area, high strength, and excellent electrical conductivity. The key products in this market include carbon black, carbon nanotubes, aptamers, small molecule OLEDs, activated carbon, carbon nanotube composites, and others.
