</p><p>HEPES (N-2-hydroxyethylpiperazine-N'-2-ethanesulfonic acid) is a zwitterionic buffer that is commonly used in biological and biochemical applications, particularly in cell culture media. With a pKa value of 7.3 at 37 °C, it helps to maintain a stable pH environment in biological systems, which is crucial for cell viability and metabolic activity. The application of HEPES is widespread in molecular biology, particularly in the preparation of cell suspensions for flow cytometry, protein extraction, and other research processes.</p><p>
</p><p>HEPES is vital in maintaining a consistent pH in culture media, which is essential for the optimal growth of cells in laboratory environments. As an organic buffer, HEPES offers significant advantages over other buffers, including its minimal toxicity to cells and its ability to maintain pH stability in a variety of experimental conditions. It is also commonly used in the production of vaccines, biologics, and other therapeutic proteins, contributing to the expansion of the global market for HEPES Powder.</p><p>
