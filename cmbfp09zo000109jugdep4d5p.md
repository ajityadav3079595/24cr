The most common grades in this category are GCr15 and GCr15SiMn, both of which are used extensively in manufacturing bearings and bearing parts.</p><p>

Thus, the high-carbon chromium bearing steel provides the necessary mechanical properties such as hardness, fatigue strength, and corrosion resistance.</p><p>
