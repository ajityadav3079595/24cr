</p><p>Nano copper oxide powder is an ultrafine material with particle sizes ranging from 1 to 100 nanometers (chemical formula: CuO). It exhibits unique properties including high specific surface area, superior catalytic performance, antimicrobial activity, and electrical conductivity. These characteristics make it valuable across multiple industries, particularly in catalysis, energy storage, environmental protection, and medical applications. The powder can be synthesized through various methods such as chemical precipitation, hydrothermal synthesis, or sol-gel processes.</p><p>
