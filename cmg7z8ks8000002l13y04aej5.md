</p><p>Rongman, also known as sodium formaldehyde sulfoxylate, is a white crystalline powder widely used as a <strong>reducing agent</strong> and <strong>bleaching agent</strong> in various industries. It is primarily employed in textile dyeing, paper manufacturing, and water treatment due to its ability to remove excess dye, whiten materials, and treat industrial effluents. The product is available in multiple forms, including powder, granule, and flake variants, each catering to specific industrial requirements.
