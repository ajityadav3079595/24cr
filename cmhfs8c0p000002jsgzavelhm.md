<strong>Market Overview</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Large Diameter Graphite Electrodes are cylindrical structures composed primarily of graphite, designed for use in electric arc furnaces (EAFs) within the steelmaking industry. These electrodes are essential in conducting electrical energy to melt scrap steel and other raw materials. The term "large diameter" typically refers to electrodes with diameters ranging from 400mm to 800mm. These electrodes exhibit excellent thermal conductivity, resistance to thermal shock, and high mechanical strength, making them indispensable in high-temperature industrial processes, particularly in steel manufacturing.</p><p>
</p><p>Due to the rising demand for recycled steel and sustainable production methods, Large Diameter Graphite Electrodes have gained prominence. They also find applications in smelting non-ferrous metals, silicon production, and other metallurgical operations. The increasing efficiency and performance requirements in these applications continue to elevate the importance of these electrodes globally.</p><p>
