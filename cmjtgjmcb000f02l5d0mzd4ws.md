# FPC Polyimide Coverlay Material Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>FPC Polyimide Coverlay Material consists of a solid polyimide sheet with a layer of flexible adhesive used in flexible printed circuit boards (PCBs) similar to how solder masks work in rigid boards. Coverlay is essential for flex circuits that require greater bendability, encapsulating and protecting the external copper circuit layers of FPCs.</p><p>
