<h1>Solvent Based Fire Retardant Coating Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>
</p><p>Solvent-based fire retardant coatings are specialized protective materials applied to the surface of flammable substrates to reduce their combustibility and improve fire resistance. These coatings work by altering the combustion characteristics of the material surface, slowing the spread of fire and enhancing the fire resistance of the coated elements. Commonly used in construction, industrial, and manufacturing sectors, solvent-based fire retardant coatings are critical for enhancing safety in environments prone to fire hazards.</p><p>
