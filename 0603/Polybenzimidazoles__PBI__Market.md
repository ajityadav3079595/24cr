 <p>The manufacturing process of PBI is complex and expensive, leading to higher costs compared to conventional polymers. This limits its adoption, particularly in price-sensitive markets.</p>

 <p>Due to the complex synthesis process, there are a limited number of manufacturers producing PBI at scale, creating supply chain constraints.</p>

 <p><strong>Rising Adoption in Medical Devices</strong></p>
 
 
 
 <p>The biocompatibility of PBI makes it suitable for medical applications such as surgical instruments and implants, opening up a promising growth avenue.</p>
