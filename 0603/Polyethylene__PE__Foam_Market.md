 <p>Polyethylene (PE) foam is a lightweight, durable, and closed-cell foam material derived from the polymerization of ethylene. It exhibits excellent cushioning, shock absorption, and thermal insulation properties, making it widely used across multiple industries such as packaging, automotive, construction, and sports equipment. Due to its moisture resistance and high impact strength, PE foam plays a crucial role in protective packaging, ensuring safe transportation of fragile goods.</p>
