<h1>Polybutadiene Rubber Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Polybutadiene rubber (PBR) is a synthetic rubber derived from the polymerization of butadiene. It is widely known for its high resistance to wear, low-temperature flexibility, and strong resilience, making it a crucial material in various industries. PBR is primarily used in the production of tires, but it also finds applications in the manufacturing of golf balls, conveyor belts, footwear, and various automotive components.</p>
