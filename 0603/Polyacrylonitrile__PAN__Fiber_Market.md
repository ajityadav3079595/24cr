 <p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>Market Size</strong></span></p>
 
 
 
 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Polyacrylonitrile (PAN) Fiber is a synthetic fiber derived from the polymerization of acrylonitrile, a monomer obtained from petroleum. It is known for its exceptional strength, stiffness, and durability, making it a crucial precursor in the production of carbon fiber. PAN Fiber exhibits excellent resistance to heat, chemicals, and UV rays, enhancing its applications across various industries, including textiles, filtration, aerospace, and medical fields.</span></p>

 <p><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>Increased Usage in Filtration Applications:</strong> The fiber&#39;s chemical resistance makes it suitable for filtration in air and liquid purification systems.</span></p>
