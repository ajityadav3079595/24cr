<h1>Polyanionic Cellulose (PAC) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Polyanionic Cellulose (PAC) is a water-soluble cellulose ether widely used in drilling fluids, coatings, and other industrial applications. As a key component in the oil and gas industry, PAC enhances the viscosity and fluid loss control of drilling muds, making it indispensable in deep drilling operations. It is also used in pharmaceuticals, food, and personal care products due to its non-toxic and biodegradable nature.</p>
