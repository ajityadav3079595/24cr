 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Polyethylene Furanoate (PEF) is a bio-based polymer produced through the polycondensation process, with its fundamental building block being 2,5-furandicarboxylic acid (FDCA). As a sustainable alternative to polyethylene terephthalate (PET), PEF offers superior barrier properties, recyclability, and a reduced carbon footprint. It is primarily used in the packaging industry for manufacturing bottles, films, and fibers, contributing to the global transition toward eco-friendly materials.</span></p>
