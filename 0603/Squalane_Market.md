<h1>Squalane Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032, by Manufacturers, Regions, Technology, Application</h1><br/><p>Squalane is a saturated derivative of squalene, a natural organic compound found in many plants and animals. Squalane is typically derived from shark liver oil or plant-based sources such as olives, sugarcane, and rice bran. It is widely used in the personal care industry for its moisturizing properties, stability, and non-comedogenic nature. In addition to skincare products, squalane is incorporated into formulations for haircare, pharmaceuticals, and even food products. Its versatility, biocompatibility, and sustainable sourcing are the driving forces behind its expanding applications in diverse industries.</p>
