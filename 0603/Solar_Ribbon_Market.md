It serves as a crucial component in interconnecting solar cells, enabling efficient electron flow and optimizing energy output. The ribbon is soldered directly onto silicon wafers and laminated within the solar module to ensure durability and long-term performance. The two primary types of solar ribbons are interconnect ribbon and bus bar ribbon, both playing essential roles in solar module efficiency and structural integrity.</span></p>
