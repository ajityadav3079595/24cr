 <p><span style="font-size:14px">Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose (HPMC) Capsules are two-piece capsules derived from plant-based cellulose, catering to vegetarian, religious, cultural, and policy-driven dietary needs. These capsules offer an all-natural alternative to gelatin-based capsules while retaining key benefits such as ease of swallowing, effective taste and odor masking, and product visibility. Their popularity has surged in the pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and dietary supplement industries due to increasing consumer preference for vegetarian and vegan-friendly products.</span></p>
