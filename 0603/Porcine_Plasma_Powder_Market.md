The key components in PPP include proteins, amino acids, and other bioactive compounds that are essential for the growth and health of animals. It is considered a valuable ingredient in animal nutrition, particularly in the early stages of growth or during periods of stress, as it supports immune system function and promotes overall health.</p>
 
 
 
 <p>Porcine Plasma Powder is primarily used as a supplement in animal diets due to its high digestibility and nutritional value.
