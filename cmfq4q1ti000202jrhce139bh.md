</p><p>Scandium acetate is a chemical compound with the formula Sc(CH3COO)3, existing in both anhydrous and hydrate forms. This water-soluble crystalline material is synthesized through the reaction of scandium hydroxide with acetic acid. When heated to high temperatures, it decomposes into scandium oxide. Scandium acetate serves as a precursor material for various scandium-containing compounds used across multiple industries.</p><p>
