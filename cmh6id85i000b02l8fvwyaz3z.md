</p><p>Conventional Polyisobutylene (PIB) is a versatile synthetic rubber polymer derived from isobutylene. It exhibits excellent gas barrier properties, chemical resistance, and flexibility, making it ideal for applications across fuel additives, adhesives, sealants, and elastomer modification. The material is categorized by molecular weight into low, medium, and high grades, each serving distinct industrial needs.</p><p>
</p><p>While the automotive sector drives demand for PIB in fuel and lube additives, emerging applications in construction adhesives and medical packaging are accelerating market expansion.
