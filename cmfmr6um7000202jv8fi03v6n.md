These versatile compounds, including monoethanolamides (MEAs) and diethanolamides (DEAs), play a critical role in personal care formulations, household cleaning products, and industrial applications due to their excellent surface-active properties.</p><p>

</p><p>The global personal care industry, valued at over $500 billion, continues to expand rapidly due to increasing consumer focus on hygiene and wellness.
