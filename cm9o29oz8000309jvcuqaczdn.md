<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyester tire cord, also known as polyester tire reinforcement, is a high-strength synthetic textile material used in the construction of automobile tires. It serves as a critical reinforcement layer, enhancing tire durability, performance, and strength. This reinforcement material ensures better load-bearing capacity, high tensile strength, and resistance to heat and fatigue, making it a preferred choice in radial tires, passenger car tires, and truck tires.</p><p>
