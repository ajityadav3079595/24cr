The material's unique properties include high elasticity, temperature resistance, and the ability to create complex shapes with consistent thickness.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing automotive production, particularly in emerging economies, along with rising consumer demand for premium interior finishes. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Raw Material Price Volatility Impacts Market Stability</strong></p><p>

</p><p><strong>Technical Limitations in Processing</strong></p><p>

</p><p><strong>Environmental Compliance Costs</strong></p><p>
Stricter regulations on phthalate content and VOC emissions are forcing manufacturers to reformulate products. The transition to compliant chemistries has increased R&amp;D expenditures by an average of 18% across the industry, with these costs frequently passed through the supply chain.</p><p>
<strong>MARKET CHALLENGES</strong></p><p>

</p><p>The concentrated nature of raw material production creates vulnerability in the slush powder supply chain. </p><p>Additionally, the specialized nature of slush powder formulations means alternative suppliers are limited. Manufacturing Concentration</p><p>
