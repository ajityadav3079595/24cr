# Latin America Fanfold Corrugated Boxboard Market Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Fanfold corrugated boxboard is a specialized type of corrugated packaging material that is supplied in a continuous, zigzag-folded format. This structure allows for enhanced flexibility and cost-efficiency in high-volume packaging applications. Unlike traditional corrugated sheets, fanfold boxboard is designed for on-demand box-making, reducing waste and optimizing material usage. It is widely used in industries requiring custom-sized boxes, including e-commerce, retail, industrial packaging, and consumer goods.</p><p>
