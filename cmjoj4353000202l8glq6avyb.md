</p><p>The PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) coated battery separator is a critical component in lithium-ion batteries, designed to enhance thermal stability, mechanical strength, and electrolyte absorption. These separators are coated with PVDF, a high-performance polymer known for its chemical resistance and durability. The coating improves the separator's wettability, which enhances ion conductivity and battery performance. PVDF-coated separators are widely used in automotive batteries, solar cells, and other energy storage applications.
