Recent supply chain disruptions have created unpredictable cost structures that make long-term planning challenging for electrolyte manufacturers.</p><p>

The classification of dimethyl carbonate as a flammable liquid under international transportation standards imposes additional handling and storage costs throughout the value chain.</p><p>
