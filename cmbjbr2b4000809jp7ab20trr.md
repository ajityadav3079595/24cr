</p><p>Poly(L-lactide-co-?-caprolactone) (LCL) is a biodegradable copolymer synthesized through the ring-opening polymerization of L-lactide and ?-caprolactone. It is widely used in medical applications, particularly in absorbable sutures and artificial dura due to its excellent biocompatibility and degradation properties. The polymer's properties can be tailored by adjusting the ratio of L-lactide to ?-caprolactone, enabling diverse applications in tissue engineering, drug delivery, and biomedical implants.</p><p>
