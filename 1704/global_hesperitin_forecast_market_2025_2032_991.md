<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Hesperitin, also known as hesperetin, is a naturally occurring flavonoid found in citrus fruits such as oranges, lemons, and grapefruits. It belongs to the flavanone subclass of flavonoids, which are known for their antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and potential health benefits. Hesperitin is widely used in the pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, food, and cosmetics industries due to its bioactive properties, making it a valuable compound in multiple applications.</p><p>
