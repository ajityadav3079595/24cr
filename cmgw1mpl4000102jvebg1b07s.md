It serves as a sustainable alternative to petroleum-based vanillin, offering similar aromatic properties while being environmentally friendly. The compound finds extensive applications in food &amp; beverages, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical industries due to its vanilla-like flavor profile and antimicrobial properties.</p><p>
