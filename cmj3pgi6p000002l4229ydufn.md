</p><p>Epoxy silanes are organofunctional silane coupling agents that combine epoxy groups with silanol functionality. These hybrid molecules serve as critical adhesion promoters between organic polymers and inorganic substrates, finding extensive use in coatings, adhesives, and composites. Major product variants include (3-glycidoxypropyl)trimethoxysilane, (3-glycidyloxypropyl)triethoxysilane, and epoxycyclohexyl derivatives.</p><p>
