<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyester Spiral Dryer Fabric is an essential industrial fabric used primarily in the papermaking industry. It plays a critical role in the paper drying process by acting as a conveyor belt in the drying section of paper machines. These fabrics, made of high-quality polyester monofilaments, are engineered to provide excellent permeability, thermal resistance, and mechanical durability. Their primary function is to facilitate the removal of moisture from paper sheets during production. Besides papermaking, they are also utilized in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment.</p><p>
