# Red Copper Fittings Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Red copper fittings, also known as pure copper fittings, are essential components used in a variety of piping systems, including plumbing, HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning), and refrigeration applications. These fittings are made from red copper, a material with a copper content of over 99%. Known for its high thermal and electrical conductivity, corrosion resistance, and exceptional durability, red copper is widely used in industries requiring reliable and efficient pipe connections.</p><p>
</p><p>The primary function of red copper fittings is to connect pipes and tubes, providing leak-proof joints in various systems, from residential water systems to industrial cooling systems.
