</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Raw Material Costs Impacting Profit Margins</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The heat shrink tube market faces ongoing pressure from fluctuating polymer prices, particularly for specialty compounds like fluoropolymers and cross-linked polyolefins. While these standards ensure product reliability, they create a conservative market environment where incremental improvements often outweigh disruptive innovations.</p><p>

</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Workforce Skills Gap in Advanced Manufacturing</strong></p><p>
