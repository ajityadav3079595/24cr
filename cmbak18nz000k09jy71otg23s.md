# Potassium Nitrate Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Potassium nitrate is an essential chemical compound with the molecular formula KNO3. It is an ionic salt comprising potassium (K+) and nitrate (NO3-) ions, making it a highly soluble and reactive alkali metal nitrate. Naturally occurring as niter, potassium nitrate has been widely used in various applications, including fertilizers, food preservatives, explosives, and pharmaceuticals. Its role as a nitrogen source makes it a crucial component in agriculture, contributing to plant nutrition and soil health.</p><p>
