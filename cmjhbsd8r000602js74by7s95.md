</p><p>Silane coupling agents are bifunctional organosilicon compounds that enhance adhesion between organic polymers and inorganic substrates. These chemicals feature reactive groups that bond with both materials, significantly improving mechanical properties, durability, and performance in composite applications. Major product types include sulfur-containing silanes, aminosilanes, epoxysilanes, and vinylsilanes, each serving distinct industrial needs.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is driven by expanding automotive and construction sectors across Thailand, Vietnam, and Indonesia, where these agents are extensively used in tire manufacturing and fiberglass reinforcement. While Thailand currently dominates regional consumption, Vietnam is emerging as the fastest-growing market due to accelerating foreign investments in manufacturing infrastructure. Recent capacity expansions by Dow Chemical and Momentive Performance Materials in Malaysia are further strengthening regional supply chains.</p><p>
