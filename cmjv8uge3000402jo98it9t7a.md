<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Initial Capital Investment Creates Barrier to Market Entry</strong></p><p>
</p><p>While shrink sleeve films offer numerous advantages, the market faces significant restraints from high equipment costs. Additionally, the need for specialized operator training adds to adoption challenges in developing regions.</p><p>
