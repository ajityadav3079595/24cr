</p><p>PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate) microspheres are spherical particles made from PMMA, a transparent thermoplastic known for its high optical clarity, UV resistance, and durability. These microspheres are widely used in industries such as cosmetics, automotive, electronics, and medical devices. Key product variations include <strong>uncoated PMMA microspheres</strong> and <strong>silver-coated PMMA microspheres</strong>, each serving specific applications.
