</p><p>While demand grows, the phone glass backplane market faces significant cost-related challenges. </p><p>The industry also contends with substantial yield rate challenges, particularly for ambitious designs like curved or ultra-thin glass variants. </p><p>Despite technological advances, glass backplanes continue to face perception challenges regarding durability and maintenance costs. </p><p>The situation is compounded by the integrated nature of modern smartphone designs, where backplane damage often necessitates complete device disassembly. </p><p>The mobile industry's sustainability push has prompted backplane manufacturers to develop eco-conscious solutions without compromising performance characteristics. China alone produces nearly <strong>70%</strong> of global smartphones, making it the epicenter of glass backplane consumption and innovation.
