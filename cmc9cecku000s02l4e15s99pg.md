<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>

</p><p>While offering long-term value, vitreous enamel panels carry 40-60% higher upfront costs compared to conventional cladding materials. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Design Constraints</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Custom colors and patterns require minimum order quantities of **500 square meters**, restricting flexibility for small-scale projects. Standard color options remain limited to approximately 30 selections across most manufacturers.</p><p>
