</p><p>Phenoxycyclotriphosphazene is an organophosphorus compound primarily used as a flame retardant additive in polymer applications. This cyclic phosphazene derivative exhibits excellent thermal stability and flame-retardant properties, making it particularly valuable in polycarbonate (PC), PC/ABS blends, and epoxy resin formulations. The compound functions by forming a protective char layer during combustion while releasing non-flammable gases that dilute oxygen concentration.</p><p>
