</p><p>Industrial Hydrofluoric Acid (HF) is an aqueous solution of hydrogen fluoride, a highly corrosive, colorless, and transparent chemical compound known for its potent acidic properties and pungent odor. Industrial-grade hydrofluoric acid is widely utilized across a range of sectors, including chemical manufacturing, mining, refining, pharmaceuticals, and electronics. Its unique ability to react with silica makes it indispensable in glass etching, metal cleaning, and semiconductor fabrication. Due to its aggressive reactivity, hydrofluoric acid requires specialized handling and storage, underscoring its role as a critical, yet hazardous industrial chemical.</p><p>
