<strong>MARKET RESTRAINTS</strong></p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>High Manufacturing Costs and Complex Production Processes Limit Market Penetration</strong></p><p>
</p><p>While double aspheric lenses offer superior optical performance, their widespread adoption is hindered by complex manufacturing requirements and high production costs. Additionally, maintaining tight tolerances (often within 0.1 microns) requires specialized equipment and skilled technicians, creating barriers to entry for smaller manufacturers.</p><p>

</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Material Limitations</strong></p><p>
