</p><p>Zirconium dioxide powder, also known as zirconia, is a white crystalline oxide of zirconium. It is widely used in various industrial applications due to its high thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical strength. The material is commonly used in ceramics, dental implants, thermal barrier coatings, and electronic components. Zirconium dioxide powder is available in different purity levels, with <strong>99.0-99.5%</strong> and <strong>&gt;99.5%</strong> being the most common grades.
