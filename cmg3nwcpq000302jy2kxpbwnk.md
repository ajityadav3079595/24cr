</p><p>Cyclopentanol (CAS 96-41-3) is a cyclic alcohol widely used as a key intermediate in pharmaceutical, agrochemical, and fragrance synthesis. It acts as a polymerization initiator for isocyanates and epoxides, making it essential in various industrial applications. The compound is primarily segmented into industrial and pharmaceutical grades, with the former dominating market share due to its broader application scope.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand from the pharmaceutical sector, where Cyclopentanol is utilized in drug formulations, alongside its rising adoption in fragrance manufacturing. However, stringent environmental regulations and fluctuating raw material prices pose challenges to market expansion. China leads the market with a 75% share, followed by Japan and India, due to robust chemical manufacturing infrastructure. Key players like Zhejiang NHU, Huangshan Basihui Chemical, and Zeon collectively hold over 80% of the market, leveraging strategic partnerships and R&amp;D investments to maintain dominance.</p><p>
