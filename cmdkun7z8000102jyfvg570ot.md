</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Supply Chain Vulnerabilities Threaten Material Availability</strong></p><p>
</p><p>While demand grows, the target material market faces significant supply chain challenges that could constrain expansion. Tellurium, a key component in CdTe solar cells, currently experiences supply deficits exceeding 15% of annual demand, creating pricing volatility. Compounding the issue, over 80% of global rare earth element production is concentrated in a single region, creating potential bottlenecks as solar manufacturing scales up.</p><p>
