</p><p>Corrugated medium sheets are arch-grooved paperboard materials that, when bonded to linerboard with adhesive, provide structural integrity against bending and multidirectional pressure. These sheets form rigid, load-bearing columns when vertically positioned, with flutes enhancing bending rigidity and acting as cushioning insulation against impacts and temperature fluctuations.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is driven by rising e-commerce packaging demands, sustainable packaging trends, and increasing industrial applications. The <strong>A Flute segment</strong> leads in adoption due to its optimal balance of strength and space efficiency, while Asia-Pacific dominates regional demand with China accounting for over 35% of global consumption. Key players like WestRock, International Paper, and Smurfit Kappa are expanding production capacities to meet accelerating demand, particularly for FMCG and electronics packaging solutions.</p><p>
