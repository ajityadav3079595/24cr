</p><p>General laminated glass consists of two or more glass layers bonded together with interlayers of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) or ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA). This specialized construction provides enhanced safety, sound insulation, UV protection, and security features while maintaining optical clarity. The product finds extensive applications in automotive windshields, architectural glazing, and specialty protective installations.</p><p>
