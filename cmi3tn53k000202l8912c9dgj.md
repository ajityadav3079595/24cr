These strips typically consist of durable materials like <strong>bronze-impregnated PTFE, ultra-high molecular weight polyethylene (UHMWPE), or composite alloys</strong>, serving critical functions in equipment longevity across sectors. Metal wear strips offer superior load-bearing capacity, whereas plastic variants excel in chemical resistance and noise reduction.</p><p>
