<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The market’s growth is fueled by increasing demand in the pharmaceutical sector, rising health-conscious consumer behavior, and the growing food and beverage industry.</p><p>
</p><p>Potassium citrate is a highly soluble monohydrate salt derived from citric acid, commonly available in crystalline granular form. It is widely utilized as an excipient in the pharmaceutical industry and as a food additive. Potassium citrate is known for its buffering, stabilizing, and chelating properties, making it a preferred alternative to sodium citrate in reduced-sodium or sodium-free formulations. This compound is extensively used in pharmaceutical products, food and beverage applications, and industrial uses due to its versatility and beneficial properties.</p><p>
