# 1,4-Cyclohexanedicarboxylic Acid (CHDA) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032, by Manufacturers, Regions, Technology, Application

<p>1,4-Cyclohexanedicarboxylic acid (CHDA) is a chemical compound that plays a pivotal role in the production of various high-performance materials and chemicals. As an important intermediate in the production of engineering plastics, pharmaceutical chemicals, coatings, and polyester polyols, CHDA serves as a building block for a wide array of applications across multiple industries. It is primarily used to synthesize polyamides such as nylon-66, which are integral to the automotive and electronics industries, and it is also employed in the formulation of coatings and pharmaceuticals. Due to its versatility, CHDA has become a critical component in the development of advanced materials that offer enhanced properties like durability, lightweight characteristics, and chemical resistance.</p><p>
