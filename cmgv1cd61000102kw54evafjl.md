# PVDC Active Food Packaging Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Polyvinylidene Chloride (PVDC) is a high-barrier polymer primarily used in food packaging due to its excellent moisture and oxygen resistance. It enhances the appearance of packaging by providing gloss, transparency, print adhesion, and scratch resistance. PVDC-based active food packaging helps extend the shelf life of perishable food items, ensuring freshness and safety for consumers.</p><p>
