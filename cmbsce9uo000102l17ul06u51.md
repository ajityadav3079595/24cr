Drivers</p><p>
</p><p>The <strong>rising prevalence of counterfeit products</strong> is a major driver for the anti-counterfeiting sticker market. </p><p>Technological advancements in anti-counterfeiting solutions, such as QR codes, RFID tags, and holographic stickers, are also driving adoption. These technologies provide enhanced security and traceability, making it difficult for counterfeiters to replicate products.</p><p>
</p><p>The <strong>growing e-commerce sector</strong> has increased the risk of counterfeit sales, prompting brands to adopt anti-counterfeiting measures. Online marketplaces are collaborating with manufacturers to ensure product authenticity, further supporting market expansion.</p><p>
