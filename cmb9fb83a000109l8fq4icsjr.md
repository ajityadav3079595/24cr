# Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) Resin Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Polyethylene terephthalate (PET), also known as PETE, is a thermoplastic polymer resin from the polyester family. PET is widely used in fiber production, food and beverage containers, and engineering applications due to its high strength, lightweight nature, recyclability, and resistance to moisture and chemicals. The material plays a crucial role in various industries, including textiles, packaging, automotive, and electronics.</p><p>
