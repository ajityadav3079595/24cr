</p><p>Nylon 66 Chips are a key intermediate product used primarily in the textile and automotive industries. They are produced through a polymerization process involving hexamethylene diamine and adipic acid, forming nylon salt, which is then processed into Nylon 66. This versatile material offers a variety of finishes, such as Bright and Semi Dull, making it ideal for applications ranging from synthetic fiber manufacturing to high-performance industrial products.</p><p>
