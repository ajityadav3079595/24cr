Major automakers have allocated over <strong>$6 billion</strong> collectively toward solid-state development, with some projections suggesting commercial availability by 2028. This technological disruption threatens to strand assets in the conventional separator value chain unless manufacturers can adapt their capabilities to emerging solid electrolyte requirements.</p><p>
