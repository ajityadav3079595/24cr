<strong>Challenges</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Technical Barriers</strong>: Achieving consistent color performance and print quality remains a challenge for manufacturers.</p><p><strong>Market Education</strong>: Raising awareness about the benefits and applications of organic inks requires sustained efforts from stakeholders.</p><p>
