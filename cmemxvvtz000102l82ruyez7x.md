# Piperonyl Butoxide (PBO) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Piperonyl Butoxide (PBO) is a synthetic organic compound widely used as a pesticide synergist. It enhances the efficacy of insecticides by inhibiting the natural defense mechanisms of insects, making it an essential ingredient in various formulations. PBO is commonly utilized in agriculture, public health, and household pest control.</p><p>
