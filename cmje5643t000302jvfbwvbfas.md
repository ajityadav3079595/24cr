</p><p>The polymer modified bitumen emulsion market remains vulnerable to fluctuations in crude oil and polymer prices. </p><p>The absence of uniform global standards for polymer modified bitumen emulsions creates confusion in the market and complicates international trade. </p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Technical Limitations in Extreme Climates Restricting Applications</strong></p><p>
</p><p>While polymer modifications have significantly improved bitumen performance, extreme climate conditions continue to pose technical challenges. Arctic environments with prolonged sub-zero temperatures and desert regions with extreme heat still push the limits of current formulations. The thermal cracking resistance of even advanced SBS-modified emulsions can be insufficient for these harsh conditions, requiring additional additives that further increase costs. These performance limitations constrain market potential in some of the world's most infrastructure-needy regions.</p><p>
