It exists in both reduced (GSH) and oxidized (GSSG) forms, playing essential roles in detoxification, immune function, and cellular protection against oxidative stress. The molecule's unique thiol group enables it to neutralize free radicals and reactive oxygen species while supporting various metabolic processes.</p><p>
