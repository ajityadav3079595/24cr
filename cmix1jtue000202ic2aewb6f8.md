</p><p>Clad plates are composite metal plates consisting of two or more different metals bonded together through explosion or roll bonding techniques. These hybrid materials combine the strength of base metals like carbon steel with the corrosion resistance of cladding materials such as stainless steel, titanium or nickel alloys. The technology enables customized material properties while reducing material costs compared to solid alloy plates.</p><p>
