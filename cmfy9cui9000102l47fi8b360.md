# Polypropylene (PP) Compounds Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Polypropylene (PP) is a thermoplastic polymer produced via chain-growth polymerization from the monomer propylene. PP compounds are formulated by adding reinforcing agents, fillers, and additives to enhance properties such as strength, durability, and resistance to environmental factors. These compounds are extensively used across various industries, including automotive, electrical appliances, building and construction, packaging, and consumer products.</p><p>
