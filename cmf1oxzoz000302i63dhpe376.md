# Reb-A Stevia Extract Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Reb-A, a natural sweetener extracted from the leaves of the Stevia plant, is widely used in food, beverages, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. Reb-A is commonly available in both powder and liquid forms and is a popular alternative to sugar due to its non-caloric properties and its ability to enhance the sweetness intensity without adding excess calories.
