</p><p>EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) elastomers are synthetic rubbers known for their excellent weather resistance, thermal stability, and chemical inertness. These materials consist of ethylene and propylene monomers with a small percentage of diene for crosslinking, making them ideal for applications requiring durability under extreme conditions. Key product variations include solution-polymerized and suspension-polymerized EPDM, each offering distinct performance characteristics.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is driven by expanding automotive applications, particularly in weatherstripping and under-the-hood components, along with rising infrastructure investments boosting demand for roofing membranes.
