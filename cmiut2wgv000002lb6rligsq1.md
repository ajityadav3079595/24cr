</p><p>Oyster shell calcium is a natural calcium supplement derived from the shells of oysters. It is rich in calcium carbonate and other essential minerals, making it a popular choice for dietary supplements and pharmaceutical applications. The product is available in various forms, including powders, tablets, and capsules, catering to different consumer needs. Oyster shell calcium is widely used in the food industry as a fortifying agent in products like dairy, baked goods, and beverages. In the pharmaceutical sector, it is utilized in the production of calcium supplements and antacids. The natural origin and high bioavailability of oyster shell calcium make it a preferred choice over synthetic alternatives.</p><p>
