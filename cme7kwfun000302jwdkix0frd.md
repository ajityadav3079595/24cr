</p><p>Antioxidant SP, also known by its chemical structure CAS No. 61788-44-1, is a significant compound used primarily to prevent oxidation reactions in various materials, notably in rubber, epoxy resins, latex, and plastics. Its role in these applications is to inhibit the degradation caused by oxidative stress, prolonging the longevity and improving the performance of products. Oxidation can lead to the deterioration of material properties, such as brittleness, discoloration, and reduced mechanical strength, making antioxidants like SP essential for maintaining product quality.</p><p>
