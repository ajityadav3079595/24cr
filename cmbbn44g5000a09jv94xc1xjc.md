</p><p><strong>Antioxidant DTPD (also known as 3100) is a high-performance rubber antioxidant primarily composed of para-phenylene diamine ursol. It is a specialized chemical additive used in various rubber applications to prevent oxidative degradation, thus significantly enhancing the durability and lifespan of rubber materials. DTPD is especially effective as an antiozonant for neoprene and is commonly employed in heavy-duty rubber products exposed to harsh environmental conditions. This includes conveyor belts, tires, cables, and other industrial rubber components. Unlike conventional antioxidants, DTPD offers superior heat resistance, oxidation stability, and long-term aging protection, making it a vital material in the rubber manufacturing sector.</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Despite its unique benefits, DTPD has a relatively small market footprint due to limited production capacity and high specialization. Currently, only a handful of manufacturers globally are producing this compound, resulting in a niche but essential market segment within the broader rubber additive industry.</strong></p><p>
