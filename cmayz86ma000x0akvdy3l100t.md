</p><p>High Consistency Rubber (HCR) is a type of solid silicone rubber that is uncured and characterized by its high viscosity. It remains in a semi-solid state until it undergoes curing, which is typically achieved through compression or injection molding processes. HCR is known for its excellent thermal stability, electrical insulation properties, and durability, which makes it ideal for a variety of high-performance applications.</p><p>
</p><p>Commonly used in industries such as automotive, medical, electrical, and consumer goods manufacturing, HCR is valued for its versatility, resilience, and ability to maintain its properties in harsh environments. Silicone rubber, including HCR, is non-toxic and can be processed into a wide range of shapes and forms, making it ideal for producing seals, gaskets, and various other components.</p><p>
