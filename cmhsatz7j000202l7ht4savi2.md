These versatile compounds serve as critical ingredients in formulations requiring emulsification, wetting, and detergency properties. The product portfolio includes various types such as fatty alcohol ethoxylates (FAE), fatty alcohol propoxylates (FAP), and their hybrid derivatives, each tailored for specific industrial applications.</p><p>
