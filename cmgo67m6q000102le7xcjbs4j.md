</p><p>Plant-based alternatives often fail to match the longevity and intensity of synthetic fragrances, presenting technical barriers for certain applications. This performance gap has slowed adoption in prestige perfume segments, which still account for over 60% of global fragrance revenue. Until technological advancements improve fixative properties, this remains a key market restraint.</p><p>
