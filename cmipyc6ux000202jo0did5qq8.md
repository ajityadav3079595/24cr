These products function by lowering the freezing point of water through endothermic reactions. The market primarily includes sodium formate, calcium chloride pellets, potassium acetate, and other variants. While calcium chloride remains the most widely used due to its cost-effectiveness, potassium acetate is gaining traction in eco-sensitive applications because of its biodegradable properties.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing infrastructure development in cold regions and stricter winter maintenance regulations.
