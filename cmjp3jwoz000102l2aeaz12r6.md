</p><p>The high temperature magnet wire market faces significant pressure from fluctuating copper and aluminum prices, which account for approximately 60-70% of production costs. Regulatory bodies in Europe and North America are implementing stricter product stewardship requirements, potentially adding compliance costs. Manufacturers face increasing pressure to develop more sustainable insulation materials that maintain thermal performance while being easier to recycle, requiring significant R&amp;D investment.</p><p>
