</p><p>Metallocene Polyethylene (mPE) is a specialized polymer produced using metallocene catalysts, which enable superior control over molecular structure compared to conventional polyethylene. This results in materials with narrow molecular weight distribution, exceptional toughness, and enhanced optical properties. The technology offers advantages like improved sealability and reduced extractable components, making it particularly valuable for packaging applications. mPE exists in various grades including film, rotomolding, and pipe grades, each optimized for specific end-use requirements.</p><p>
