</p><p><strong>Pentaerythritol Tetra(3-mercaptopropionate)</strong> (CAS 7575-23-7) is a specialized chemical compound primarily used in the formulation of advanced materials and coatings. It appears as a colorless, transparent liquid and has a molecular formula of C17H28O8S4, with a molecular weight of 488.6594 g/mol. Functioning as a multifunctional thiol compound, it plays a significant role in applications such as UV-curable coatings, adhesives, inks, and polymer modifications. It also serves as an effective crosslinker, acidic ion exchange catalyst, and a low-temperature curing agent due to its structural properties and reactive mercapto (–SH) groups.</p><p>
