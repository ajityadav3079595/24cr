</p><p>Foam pump bottles are specialized dispensing containers equipped with foaming mechanisms that convert liquid products into lightweight foam. These pumps work by mixing air with liquid during dispensing, creating a rich lather ideal for products like hand sanitizers, body washes, and facial cleansers. The primary materials used include HDPE (high-density polyethylene) and PET (polyethylene terephthalate), known for their durability and chemical resistance.</p><p>
</p><p>The COVID-19 pandemic significantly accelerated market growth due to heightened demand for foam hand sanitizers in healthcare and public settings. While the personal care segment continues to dominate, emerging applications in premium skincare and home care products are creating new opportunities. Asia-Pacific currently leads global consumption, with China contributing over 35% of regional demand in 2023, followed by North America and Europe where sustainability initiatives are driving innovations in recyclable materials.</p><p>
