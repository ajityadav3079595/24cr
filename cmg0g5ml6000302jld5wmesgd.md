</p><p><strong>Industrial Grade Vinylene Carbonate (VC)</strong> is a high-purity cyclic organic compound with the molecular formula C3H2O3. It is widely recognized for its excellent electrochemical stability, low volatility, and strong film-forming capabilities. These properties make VC an indispensable additive in lithium-ion battery electrolytes, especially in enhancing battery safety, performance, and longevity. Additionally, it is used as a resin modifier and intermediate in various industrial applications. Its capacity to form a robust solid electrolyte interphase (SEI) on electrode surfaces positions it as a key component in high-energy battery chemistries, including those used in electric vehicles (EVs), consumer electronics, and energy storage systems.</p><p>
