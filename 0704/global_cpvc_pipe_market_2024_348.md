<h1>CPVC Pipe Market, Global Outlook &amp; Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Chlorinated polyvinyl chloride (CPVC) pipe is a high-performance thermoplastic piping material known for its durability, corrosion resistance, and ability to withstand high temperatures and pressures.
