</p><p>Non-Grain Oriented (NGO) Electrical Steel is a category of silicon steel that exhibits uniform magnetic properties in all directions, making it ideal for rotating machines. Unlike grain-oriented electrical steel, which is used primarily in transformers and directional magnetic applications, NGO electrical steel is designed for applications requiring isotropic magnetic behavior. This includes a wide array of motor and generator types used in everything from large-scale industrial machinery to small household appliances and precision electronic components.</p><p>
</p><p>NGO electrical steel is typically manufactured by cold rolling and is characterized by its thin gauge, high magnetic permeability, and low core loss. The material is further processed with surface insulation coatings and annealing to enhance its magnetic and mechanical properties. As global demand for electric vehicles, energy-efficient motors, and renewable energy systems increases, NGO electrical steel plays a pivotal role in improving electrical performance while reducing energy losses.</p><p>
