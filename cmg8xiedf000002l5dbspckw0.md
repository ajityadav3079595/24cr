</p><p>Industrial Grade Butylated Hydroxytoluene (BHT) is a synthetic antioxidant widely used to prevent oxidative degradation in polymers, rubbers, and other industrial materials. As a phenolic compound, BHT effectively inhibits free radical chain reactions that cause material deterioration, extending product lifespan. Its applications span across plastics, lubricants, adhesives, and synthetic rubber manufacturing due to its high stability and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand for polymer stabilization in packaging and automotive industries, particularly in emerging economies.
