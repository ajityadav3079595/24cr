</p><p>Ammonia synthesis catalysts are specialized materials used in the Haber-Bosch process to facilitate ammonia production from nitrogen and hydrogen under high temperature and pressure. These catalysts, primarily iron-based, significantly improve reaction efficiency while reducing energy consumption. Emerging alternatives like ruthenium-based catalysts are gaining traction due to their superior performance in low-pressure conditions, though they currently represent less than 15% of the market.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is being driven by increasing fertilizer demand across agricultural sectors, particularly in India and Southeast Asia where food security remains a priority. While China dominates regional consumption with over 50% market share, India's growing fertilizer industry and Japan's focus on catalyst efficiency are creating diverse growth opportunities. However, the market faces challenges from tightening environmental regulations and competition from green ammonia production methods.</p><p>
