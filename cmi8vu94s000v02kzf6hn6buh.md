</p><p>Resin pigments are specialized colorants formulated for compatibility with synthetic polymers and resin systems. These additives provide stable coloration across various applications while maintaining the structural integrity of composite materials. The product range includes both <strong>liquid pigments</strong> (dispersions in carrier fluids) and <strong>powder pigments</strong> (micronized color concentrates), with formulations engineered for specific resin matrices like epoxy, polyurethane, and polyester.</p><p>
</p><p>Market expansion is driven by growing demand from end-use industries, particularly automotive coatings where resin pigments enhance durability and UV resistance.
