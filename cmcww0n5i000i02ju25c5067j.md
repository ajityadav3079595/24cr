# ESBO Plasticizer Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Epoxidized Soybean Oil (ESO) is manufactured from soybean oil through the process of epoxidation. It is widely used as a plasticizer due to its high numbers of epoxidized carbon-carbon double bonds. The epoxide group is highly reactive, making it a good hydrochloric acid scavenger and plasticizer.</p><p>
