# Powder Metal Components Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Powder metal components refer to parts and products manufactured using powder metallurgy (PM) techniques. This process involves compacting metal powders into desired shapes, followed by sintering to form solid, high-strength components. Powder metallurgy is widely used across industries such as automotive, aerospace, medical, and industrial machinery due to its ability to produce complex geometries, enhance material utilization, and minimize waste.</p><p>
