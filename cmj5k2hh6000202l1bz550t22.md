# Nd-Fe-B Magnet Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>The global <strong>Nd-Fe-B Magnet Market</strong> refers to the market for <strong>Neodymium-Iron-Boron (Nd-Fe-B)</strong> magnets, which are high-performance rare-earth magnets primarily used in various applications that require strong and durable magnetic properties. Sintered Nd-Fe-B magnets are manufactured from a blend of raw materials, including neodymium, iron, and boron, which are melted under vacuum or inert atmospheres and processed into strips. These strips are then pulverized into a fine powder and compacted under high pressure to form the final magnet product.</p><p>
</p><p>These magnets are characterized by their high energy density, strong magnetic force, and excellent stability at high temperatures, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial, automotive, and consumer electronic applications.
