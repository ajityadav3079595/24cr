</p><p>2 Nitro Para Cresol (2-Nitro-4-methylphenol) is an important chemical intermediate used in the production of dyes, pesticides, and other specialty chemicals. The compound is particularly significant in manufacturing azo dyes and as a precursor in pesticide formulations.</p><p>
