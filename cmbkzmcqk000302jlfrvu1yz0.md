# Boscalid Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Boscalid is a broad-spectrum fungicide that belongs to the carboxamide class. It is widely used in agricultural applications to protect crops from fungal diseases by inhibiting the activity of succinate dehydrogenase, which is essential for fungal respiration. Its effectiveness against various fungal pathogens makes it a critical component in modern agricultural practices.</p><p>
