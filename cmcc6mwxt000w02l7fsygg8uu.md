Mildew removers are typically applied to surfaces where mold growth is prominent, such as bathrooms, kitchens, tiles, grout, and other unpainted surfaces. These products are primarily used for removing both the visible mold and the spots or stains caused by mold infestation. They are available in various formulations such as sprays, gels, foams, and powders, which are designed to cater to different cleaning needs and user preferences.</p><p>
</p><p>Mildew removers not only help eliminate mold growth but also provide added benefits such as deodorization, antibacterial properties, and mold prevention.
