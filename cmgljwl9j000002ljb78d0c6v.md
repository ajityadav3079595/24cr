These targets consist of precisely formulated nickel-chromium alloys (typically 80/20 or 70/30 composition) designed to produce uniform, high-performance coatings. The material's exceptional properties - including corrosion resistance, thermal stability, and electrical conductivity - make it indispensable for applications ranging from microelectronics to aerospace components.</p><p>
