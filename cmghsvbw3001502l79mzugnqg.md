</p><p>Milk thistle extracts are derived from Silybum marianum, a medicinal plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. The active compound, silymarin, consists of flavonolignans that demonstrate hepatoprotective properties. These extracts are widely used in pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals, and cosmetics due to their antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and liver-supporting benefits. With traditional usage spanning over 2,000 years, modern applications now include liver disease treatment, detoxification supplements, and skincare formulations.</p><p>
