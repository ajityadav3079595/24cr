# Anhydrous Hydroxybenzotriazole Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Anhydrous Hydroxybenzotriazole (HOBt) is an organic compound that serves as a key ingredient in the synthesis of peptides. It is utilized to improve the efficiency of peptide synthesis by preventing racemization in chiral molecules. HOBt is commonly used in automated peptide synthesis processes where it helps in the condensation of amino acids with activated esters to produce amides. This white crystalline powder plays a crucial role in the pharmaceutical and chemical industries for the creation of complex molecules.</p><p>
