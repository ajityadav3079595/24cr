</p><p>Aluminum cladding panels are lightweight, durable architectural materials used for exterior and interior building applications. These panels consist of aluminum composite material (ACM) with a polyethylene or mineral-filled core sandwiched between two aluminum sheets. They offer excellent weather resistance, thermal insulation, and aesthetic flexibility through various finishes including painted, anodized, or PVDF-coated options. Primary panel thicknesses range from 3mm to 6mm, catering to different structural and design requirements.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing construction activities, particularly in commercial and residential sectors, coupled with rising demand for energy-efficient building solutions. Furthermore, stringent fire safety regulations and sustainability initiatives are accelerating product innovation in flame-retardant and recyclable aluminum panels. Key players such as Arconic Corporation, 3A Composites, and Alubond USA are expanding their product portfolios with high-performance solutions to meet evolving architectural demands.</p><p>
