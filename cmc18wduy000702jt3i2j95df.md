# Sodium Hyaluronate Raw Material Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Sodium hyaluronate, the sodium salt of hyaluronic acid, is a naturally occurring glycosaminoglycan widely distributed in the human body, particularly in connective tissues, synovial fluid, and the skin. Known for its superior water retention capabilities, it plays a vital role in medical, cosmetic, and dietary applications. As an essential ingredient in anti-aging skincare products, ophthalmic solutions, and joint therapy treatments, sodium hyaluronate continues to gain traction in the global market.</p><p>
