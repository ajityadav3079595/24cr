</p><p>Metal cable trays are structural components used in electrical installations to securely support insulated cables for power distribution, control systems, and communication networks. These trays provide superior mechanical protection compared to conduit systems while maintaining flexibility for cable routing modifications. Cable trays primarily come in two material variants – <strong>steel (holding 77% market share</strong>) and aluminum, with configurations including ladder, solid-bottom, and wire mesh designs.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by accelerating infrastructure development projects globally, particularly in the <strong>Asia-Pacific region (45% market share)</strong>, alongside stringent safety regulations for electrical installations.
