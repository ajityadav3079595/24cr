</p><p>Water resistant screen printing emulsions are light-sensitive coatings applied to mesh screens to create stencils for printing. These specialized emulsions form durable, water-resistant layers when cured, making them ideal for applications requiring extended print runs or exposure to moisture. The product category includes dual-cure emulsions (combining diazo and photopolymer technologies), pure diazo emulsions, and SBQ-based (styrylpyridinium) emulsions - each offering distinct resolution and durability characteristics.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by expanding textile printing demands, particularly in sportswear and outdoor apparel sectors where moisture resistance is critical.
