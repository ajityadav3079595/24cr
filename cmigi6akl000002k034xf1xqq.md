</p><p>Self-levelling concrete is a specialized polymer-modified cementitious material designed with unique flow characteristics that enable it to spread evenly and achieve a smooth surface without mechanical finishing. This advanced concrete formulation contains precise ratios of fine aggregates, cementitious binders, and flow-enhancing additives that distinguish it from traditional concrete mixes. Unlike conventional concrete that requires labor-intensive trowelling and leveling, self-levelling compounds naturally seek their own level through controlled viscosity and rheological properties.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by increasing construction activity worldwide, particularly in residential and commercial flooring applications.
