These cost factors make high purity zinc sulfide prohibitively expensive for some potential applications, restricting market expansion in price-sensitive sectors. Additionally, the specialized equipment and controlled environments needed for production create substantial barriers to entry for new market players, limiting competitive pressure that could drive prices down.</p><p>

<strong>MARKET CHALLENGES</strong></p><p>

</p><p>High purity zinc sulfide faces growing competition from alternative materials in key applications.
