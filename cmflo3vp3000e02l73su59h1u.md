These chemicals prevent hazardous ice accumulation on surfaces like roads, runways, and walkways through various mechanisms including freezing point depression and ice melting. The product categories include rock salt (sodium chloride), calcium chloride, magnesium chloride, potassium acetate, and glycol-based formulations.</p><p>
