</p><p>Polyiso Exterior Wall Insulation Boards are high-performance rigid foam insulation products composed of polyisocyanurate foam cores bonded to durable facers. These boards deliver exceptional thermal resistance (R-values ranging from R-5.6 to R-8.0 per inch) while offering superior fire resistance and moisture control compared to alternative insulation materials. The facer options include foil-faced, glass-faced, and fiberglass-faced variants, each providing specific performance characteristics for different building applications.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is being driven by stringent energy efficiency regulations like IECC 2021 standards, increased commercial construction activity (particularly in North America and Asia-Pacific), and rising demand for sustainable building materials. Recent innovations include factory-laminated boards with integrated air/water barriers and thicker panel options (up to 6 inches) for high-performance building envelopes. Key players like Kingspan, Owens Corning, and GAF have expanded production capacity in 2023-2024 to meet growing demand, particularly for commercial retrofitting projects in urban centers.</p><p>
