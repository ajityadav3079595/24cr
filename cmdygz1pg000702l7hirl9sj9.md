</p><p>Cosmetic grade iron oxides are inorganic pigments widely used for coloration in personal care products due to their excellent stability, non-toxicity and vibrant shades. These synthetic oxides primarily include red, yellow, black and brown variants that comply with stringent FDA and EU cosmetic regulations. The pigments find applications across facial make-up, lip products, eye shadows and nail polishes, where they provide superior opacity and UV resistance compared to organic dyes.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by expanding cosmetic industries worldwide and increasing consumer preference for long-lasting, vibrant color cosmetics.
