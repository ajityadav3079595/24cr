</p><p>Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) films are thermoplastic polymer films produced by stretching polypropylene in both machine and transverse directions, resulting in superior mechanical properties, clarity, and barrier performance. These films find extensive applications in flexible packaging, labeling, lamination, and industrial uses due to their high tensile strength, dimensional stability, and moisture resistance.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing demand for flexible packaging solutions across food, consumer goods, and pharmaceutical industries. Furthermore, technological advancements in film production and rising adoption of sustainable packaging materials contribute to market expansion. Key players like Jindal Poly Films, Cosmo Films, and Taghleef Industries are investing in capacity expansions and innovative product development to strengthen their market position.</p><p>
