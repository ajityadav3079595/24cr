</p><p><strong>Technical Barriers in Crystal Growth</strong></p><p>
Achieving defect-free single crystals requires precise control over temperature and doping levels. Even minor impurities can degrade performance in optoelectronic applications, raising yield challenges and increasing waste. These technical complexities deter smaller players from entering the market.</p><p>
