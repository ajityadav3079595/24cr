</p><p>Silicon tapes are specialized adhesive materials composed of silicone polymers, known for their exceptional temperature resistance, electrical insulation, and waterproofing properties. These tapes are engineered for applications requiring durable bonding and sealing in extreme conditions, including high-temperature environments, electrical wiring, medical devices, and industrial settings. Common variants include self-fusing silicone tapes, pressure-sensitive adhesive tapes, and fiberglass-reinforced silicone tapes.</p><p>
