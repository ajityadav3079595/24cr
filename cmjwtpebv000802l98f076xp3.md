# EVA Hot Melt Adhesives Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>EVA hot melt adhesive is a solid fusible polymer that requires no solvent and contains no moisture. It is solid at room temperature and melts when heated to a certain temperature, becoming a liquid with a specific viscosity. After melting, EVA hot melt adhesive appears light brown or white and is composed of basic resin, viscosifier, viscosity regulator, and antioxidant.</p><p>
