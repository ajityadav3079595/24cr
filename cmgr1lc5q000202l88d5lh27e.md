</p><p>Heavy metal precipitants are chemical agents used to remove toxic heavy metals from industrial wastewater through precipitation reactions. These compounds typically include hydroxides, carbonates, and sulfides which react with dissolved metal ions to form insoluble solids for easier filtration. The technology plays a critical role in environmental protection across industries like mining, electroplating, and chemical manufacturing where metal contamination poses significant regulatory and ecological challenges.</p><p>
</p><p>Market growth is driven by stringent environmental regulations worldwide, particularly regarding wastewater discharge standards from industries. While the mining sector remains the largest application segment, increasing adoption in electronics manufacturing for PCB wastewater treatment shows promising growth.
