</p><p>Roofing panels are prefabricated construction materials designed to provide durable, weather-resistant coverage for buildings. These panels come in various materials including metal (steel, aluminum), plastic (polycarbonate, PVC), fiberglass, and composite materials, offering solutions for thermal insulation, waterproofing, and structural support. Common panel types include corrugated, standing seam, and sandwich panels with integrated insulation layers.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by increasing construction activities globally, particularly in residential and commercial sectors, coupled with rising demand for energy-efficient building materials. Recent innovations in cool roof technologies and sustainable materials are further accelerating adoption. Key players like Kingspan Group and ArcelorMittal are investing in product development, such as fire-resistant panels and solar-integrated roofing solutions, to meet evolving regulatory standards and consumer preferences for green buildings.</p><p>
