# Industrial Stretch Film Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032

<p>Industrial stretch film is a plastic film made from linear low-density polyethylene used to secure, bundle, and stabilize products. It is applied by pulling and stretching it around products to create tightly secured loads. The film comes in various widths, thicknesses, and colors for different applications.</p><p>
