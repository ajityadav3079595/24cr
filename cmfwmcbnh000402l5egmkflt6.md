</p><p>Arginine is an α-amino acid that serves as a precursor for nitric oxide synthesis in the human body. As one of the 20 proteinogenic amino acids, L-arginine plays crucial roles in cardiovascular function, immune response, and hormone secretion. While classified as a semi-essential amino acid in adults, it becomes essential during periods of growth, stress, or illness. The compound finds extensive application across pharmaceutical formulations, sports nutrition supplements, infant nutrition products, and animal feed additives.</p><p>

</p><p>Global arginine market is witnessing substantial growth due to increasing consumer awareness about health and wellness.
