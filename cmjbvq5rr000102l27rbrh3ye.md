These fluids are essential in maintaining temperature control in applications such as heating, cooling, and energy transfer. The <strong>key products</strong> in this market include glycol-based fluids, synthetic fluids, and other specialized formulations. These fluids are designed to operate efficiently under extreme temperatures and pressures, ensuring optimal performance in diverse industrial environments.
