</p><p>Acrylic viscose yarn is a high-performance synthetic blend combining the durability of acrylic fibers with the softness and draping qualities of viscose. This engineered textile material offers superior color retention, wrinkle resistance, and moisture absorption properties compared to single-fiber yarns. The material composition typically ranges between 30-70% acrylic and 30-70% viscose, allowing manufacturers to tailor performance characteristics for specific applications in knitwear, home textiles, and technical fabrics.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is primarily driven by expanding demand in the garment industry, where this yarn is increasingly preferred for producing affordable yet quality-conscious apparel. Furthermore, technological advancements in fiber blending techniques and growing adoption in emerging economies are creating new opportunities. <strong>MARKET DRIVERS</strong></p><p>

</p><p>Global acrylic viscose yarn market is witnessing substantial growth due to increasing consumer preference for fabrics that combine softness with durability. Viscose provides a silk-like texture while acrylic enhances longevity and color retention, making the blended yarn highly sought after in the apparel sector. Recent trends show that over 65% of global textile manufacturers are shifting toward blended yarns to meet consumer demands for versatile, easy-care fabrics. This shift is particularly evident in activewear and casual clothing segments where comfort and performance are paramount.</p><p>
</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Rapid Expansion of Fast Fashion Industry Accelerates Demand</strong></p><p>
</p><p>The fast fashion sector's explosive growth, projected to reach approximately $39 billion by 2025, is fueling unprecedented demand for affordable yet high-quality textile materials. Major apparel retailers are increasingly incorporating acrylic viscose blends in their collections to reduce production costs while maintaining aesthetic appeal. This trend is most pronounced in emerging markets where fashion-conscious consumers are driving rapid market penetration.</p><p>
</p><p>The home textiles sector is also contributing significantly to market growth, with acrylic viscose yarn gaining popularity for upholstery and decorative fabrics due to its stain resistance and vibrant color options.
