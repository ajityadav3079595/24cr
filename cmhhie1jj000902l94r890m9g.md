</p><p>Metal degreasers are specialized cleaning agents designed to remove oils, greases, oxides, and other contaminants from metal surfaces. These formulations play a vital role in industrial maintenance, preparing metal substrates for finishing processes, and ensuring optimal performance in mechanical applications. The product landscape includes two primary categories: <strong>solvent-based degreasers</strong> (using organic compounds like hydrocarbons or chlorinated solvents) and <strong>aqueous degreasers</strong> (water-based alkaline or acidic solutions).</p><p>
