These specialized additives enhance resin flow and processing efficiency while improving surface properties. Key benefits include better mold filling, reduced extruder torque, faster throughput, and superior wear/scratch resistance.</p><p>

</p><p style="text-align:center"><strong>Technical Limitations in High-Temperature Applications</strong></p><p>
</p><p>While ketone masterbatches excel in many polymer systems, their performance in engineering plastics remains challenging. </p><p><strong>Other Challenges</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Adhesion Issues</strong></p><p>

Some automotive manufacturers report up to 30% rejection rates for interior parts due to paint adhesion failures when standard masterbatch formulations are used without surface pretreatment.</p><p>
</p><p><strong>Recycling Compatibility</strong></p><p>
The growing emphasis on circular economy principles creates challenges for siloxane-modified plastics, as these additives can contaminate recycling streams. Some municipal recycling facilities automatically downgrade or reject polymer waste containing silicone additives due to concerns about process equipment fouling.</p><p>
