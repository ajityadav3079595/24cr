</p><p>Diethyl succinate is an organic compound commonly used as a solvent, plasticizer, and flavoring agent. It is an ester of succinic acid and ethanol, characterized by its mild fruity odor. The compound is widely utilized in the chemical industry for its versatility in applications such as <strong>plasticizers, specialty lubricants, solvents, and food additives</strong>. It is also a key intermediate in organic synthesis, contributing to the production of pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, and fragrances.
