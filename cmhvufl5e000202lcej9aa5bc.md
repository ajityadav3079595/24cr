</p><p>Simeprevir, commercially known as Olysio, is a second-generation NS3/4A protease inhibitor used in combination therapy for chronic hepatitis C virus (HCV) infection. This direct-acting antiviral (DAA) specifically targets genotype 1 and 4 HCV infections, offering improved efficacy and reduced side effects compared to earlier interferon-based treatments. The drug gained FDA approval in 2013 and has since become part of the evolving HCV treatment landscape.</p><p>
</p><p>The market is witnessing steady growth due to the persistent global HCV burden, with WHO estimating 58 million chronic infections worldwide.
