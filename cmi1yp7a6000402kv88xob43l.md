</p><p>The global push for lightweight materials across industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction continues to shape the tough epoxy structural adhesives landscape. </p><p>Environmental regulations and corporate sustainability goals are accelerating the development of low-VOC and bio-based epoxy adhesives. </p><p>The rise of Industry 4.0 and automated production lines has created new requirements for structural adhesives. Construction sector growth in Colombia and Chile presents opportunities, particularly for infrastructure repair applications.
