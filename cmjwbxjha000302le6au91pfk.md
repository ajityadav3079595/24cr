This ultra-thin foil enables higher energy density in batteries because it reduces weight while maintaining excellent conductivity. The material's precision thickness of 4.5 micrometers (about 1/5 the width of a human hair) allows for more compact battery designs without sacrificing performance. As electric vehicles and portable electronics demand lighter, more efficient power solutions, this specialized copper foil has become increasingly vital to battery manufacturers.</p><p>

These technical limitations restrict the application of 4.5um foil in certain battery form factors and manufacturing environments where robustness is prioritized over maximum energy density.</p><p>
