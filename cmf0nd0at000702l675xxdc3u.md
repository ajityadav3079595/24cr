This thermal treatment enhances its hardness (8-9 Mohs scale) and chemical stability, making it indispensable for industrial applications. The material exists in two primary forms: <strong>aggregates</strong> (used in refractory castables) and <strong>powders</strong> (for abrasives and ceramics).</p><p>
