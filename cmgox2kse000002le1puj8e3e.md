</p><p>Additionally, the growing consumer preference for metallic and specialty finishes introduces new technical complexities in the coating process. The delicate nature of pre-coated surfaces creates significant logistics challenges throughout the supply chain. Improper handling can lead to surface scratches or coating damage that renders the material unusable for visible appliance components. These handling requirements add to transportation costs and complicate inventory management, particularly for just-in-time manufacturing systems.</p><p>
