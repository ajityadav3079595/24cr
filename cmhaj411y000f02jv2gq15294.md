</p><p>Amine terminated polyether is a specialized reactive polymer used as a chain extender in polyurethane systems. These compounds feature primary amine groups that enable rapid curing reactions, making them ideal for high-performance applications like spray polyurea coatings, adhesives, and epoxy hardeners. The material offers exceptional properties including corrosion resistance, waterproofing, and wear protection.</p><p>
</p><p>The market growth is driven by expanding polyurethane applications in construction and automotive sectors, along with increasing demand for durable protective coatings.
